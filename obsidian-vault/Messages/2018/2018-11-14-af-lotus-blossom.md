# Lotus Blossom Message from Oz

**Spirit:** [[Spirits/lotus-blossom|Lotus Blossom]] · **Medium:** Al Fike · **Date:** 2018-11-14

> [!quote] The Door
> The healing that begins in prayer does not stop when you sleep; spirits attend to you through the night.

Lotus Blossom offers a brief healing blessing to those gathered in Sydney, encouraging them to pray before sleep and assuring them that she and many other spirits will attend to their physical and spiritual healing through the night.

## Questions this message answers

- Can spirits help heal us while we sleep?
- Should I pray before going to sleep?
- How do spirit healers work with us?
- Is physical healing available through prayer?
- Are spirit guides with us even when we are not aware of them?
