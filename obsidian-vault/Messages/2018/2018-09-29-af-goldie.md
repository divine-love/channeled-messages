# Celebrating Vicky's Passing Into the World of Spirit

**Spirit:** [[Spirits/goldie|Goldie]] · **Medium:** Al Fike · **Date:** 2018-09-29

> [!quote] The Door
> If only more knew how beautiful and wonderful is God's creation, all that worry and concern would melt into joy and laughter

Goldie arrives at a memorial service to bring lightness and joy, reframing the passing of a beloved friend named Vicky as a birth into the beautiful world of spirit. She calls those present to bring joy into their hearts and a spring into their step, celebrating life in all its forms, for if the world truly knew the beauty of God's creation all grief would melt into laughter.

## Questions this message answers

- How do I find joy in grief when someone I love passes away?
- Is death something to be celebrated rather than mourned?
- What is the spirit world like for someone who has just passed?
- How does knowing about the spirit world change how we grieve?
- What does the Bible mean when it says to be absent from the body is to be present with the Lord?
- How do I hold both grief and joy at the same time when someone dies?

Your dear friend wants me to come to your service to lighten the crowd. I will, for this is a joyous occasion, celebrating the birth of Vicky into the world of spirit. What a beautiful occasion, to begin her life anew in this beautiful world that God created. If only more knew how beautiful and wonderful is God's creation, all that worry and concern would melt into joy and laughter. So much the world has to learn. So much the world thinks it knows, and knows nothing at all of God's creation and God's universe.

My friends, bring joy into your hearts, a spring into your step, celebrating life in all its forms and ways and manifestations of God's wonderful creation. We are all so blessed, are we not, in all the wonderment and goodness of our lives. The Touch of God in our souls, the understanding of true life in our mind. So blessed we are, so very blessed.

I love you. God bless you. I am Goldie and I love you.
