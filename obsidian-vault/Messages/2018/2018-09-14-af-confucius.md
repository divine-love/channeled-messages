# We Will Be with You

**Spirit:** [[Spirits/confucius|Confucius]] · **Medium:** Al Fike · **Date:** 2018-09-14

> [!quote] The Door
> You are my little birds and I keep you in my nest of love, beloveds

Confucius cautions the circle about the spiritual risks of moving among those who are psychically gifted but not soulfully grounded, teaching that such souls may take as much as they give and that discernment is essential. He assures them that the angels are always present to protect and uphold them, and calls them to keep their guides in consciousness and prayer as they navigate this environment as part of their God-given work.

## Questions this message answers

- How do I protect my own spiritual condition when around psychically gifted people?
- What is the difference between psychic gifts and spiritual depth?
- How do I discern whether someone is spiritually grounded or just gifted?
- Can psychically gifted people drain my energy or condition?
- How do I ask for and maintain a cloak of spiritual protection?
- How do I stay open to connecting with others while still guarding my own soul?
- What does the Bible mean when it says test the spirits to see whether they are from God?
- How do I stay spiritually centred in environments that are not prayer-focused?
- How do I keep my angel guides in my consciousness throughout the day?

It is not easy to walk in these conditions with so many who are so psychically gifted. There are many there who will take as much as they give. You must be careful to guard your own condition, to not open too wide to those who you may be impressed with, with their gifts, but are not so spiritually inclined. This is the dilemma of the gifted souls. They manifest physical gifts but do not know their own souls. It is for you, beloveds, to discern this when you are with others. It is a test and it is an opportunity to learn.

We will be with you, beloveds. Continue to pray for the cloak of protection around you and continue to be inspired, for you are indeed meant to connect and to approach many. This is part of your work, part of your task for God.

Remember, we are always here to support and uphold you, beloveds. Keep us with you in your consciousness and prayers and you will be protected. God bless you beloveds, God bless you. Confucius loves you. You are my little birds and I keep you in my nest of love, beloveds.
