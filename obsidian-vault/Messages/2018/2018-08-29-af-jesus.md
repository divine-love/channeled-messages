# Jesus, I Am With You

**Spirit:** [[Spirits/jesus|Jesus]] · **Medium:** Al Fike · **Date:** 2018-08-29

> [!quote] The Door
> Follow me to at-onement with God and I shall lead the way. I shall light the lantern of truth for all those who so desire to follow upon this path of Love

Jesus brings a tender and intimate assurance that he is present with each soul in every moment of their journey, whether in struggle, discovery, or prayer. He offers himself as the lantern bearer on the path to at-onement, promising to light the way through the illusions of the world and into the wonderment of God's Creation and the power of His Love.

## Questions this message answers

- Is Jesus present with me in my daily life?
- What does it mean to follow Jesus to at-onement with God?
- Does Jesus pray for us today?
- How does Jesus lead me through darkness and illusion?
- What does the Bible mean when Jesus says I am the way, the truth, and the life?
- How do I know Jesus is with me when I struggle?
- What does it mean that Jesus carries a lantern of truth?

God is upon you beloved souls. Follow me to at-onement with God and I shall lead the way. I shall light the lantern of truth and light it for all those who so desire to follow upon this path of Love. I am Jesus and I am with you in your efforts to discern truth, to walk in light, to gather Love within your souls. I am with you as you struggle in this world, as you continue to peel back the layers of illusion and error, to come to that place of clarity and truth. I am with you when you discover the joys of your soul, as you seek your Heavenly Father and receive His blessings of Love. I am with you as you pray, for I pray with you and pray for you beloved souls. I walk with my lantern of truth. I walk in the light of God's Love. Follow me and I will show you the wonderment of God's Creation and the power of His Love.

God bless you beloved souls. I am with you and I love you my dear friends, brothers and sisters all. God bless you.
