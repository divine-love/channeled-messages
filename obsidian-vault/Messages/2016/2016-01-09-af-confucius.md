# Your Beautiful Souls Are Eager

**Spirit:** [[Spirits/confucius|Confucius]] · **Medium:** Al Fike · **Date:** 2016-01-09

> [!quote] The Door
> The gifts you are waiting to discover are already inside you, already eager to be found.

Confucius celebrates the community's eagerness and love for one another as they enter a new era of service, affirming that each soul carries unique and wondrous gifts waiting to be discovered and expressed in the world. He urges each one to act upon the guidance they receive from God, promising that as they do they will fulfill their purpose and become true instruments of Light and change.

## Questions this message answers

- How do I discover the unique spiritual gifts I carry?
- How do I know when I am receiving guidance from God?
- What does it mean to act on God's Will?
- How do I fulfil my unique spiritual purpose?
- What happens when a spiritual community acts together in love?
- How do I become an instrument of God's Light in the world?
- Can I really receive clear guidance from God myself?

My beautiful birds, precious souls, indeed you celebrate the beginning of a new era in your lives and your service to God, my beloveds, and your minds, your eyes, your souls are open to something new and beautiful. And in the flow of this service, my beloveds, you will walk through many thresholds, opportunities, and understandings that will be fresh and new in greater Love and understanding. Your souls are eager, my beloveds, your souls are eager and we are joyed at your eagerness and your love for one another. For in the power of this Love, in the desires of your souls, in your willingness to follow God's Will, you will accomplish much in this world together and singly as God's channels of Love, creating great, great blessings of Light and Love in this world.

Continue to be together in Love, to pray together in Love and to act upon the guidance which God gives each one, beloved souls, for each of you are capable of receiving that guidance and knowing God's Will in great measure, in clarity. And may you have the strength, each one of you, my beloved, beautiful souls, to act upon your guidance, to know God's Will and to be in alignment with His gracious Will. For in this, in this, you will fulfill your purpose, each one unique in this world, great Gifts each one, wonderful, surprising, beautiful Gifts, each one of you, my beloveds possess these Gifts. And you will come to know these great treasures within you and to manifest these wondrous Gifts in this world, truly being God's instruments of Light and change, of Love and beauty.

We walk with you, beloved souls, always and we shall never leave you and God's gracious Touch and flow of His Love shall carry you forward to greater Light and service, understanding and Love. God bless you. I am Confucius and I bless you and your meeting together, your celebration, your eagerness, your beautiful souls. God bless you, beloveds. God bless you, beloveds.
