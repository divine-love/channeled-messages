# God Provides for Those Who Serve*

**Spirit:** [[Spirits/augustine|Augustine]] · **Medium:** Al Fike · **Date:** 2016-04-04

> [!quote] The Door
> God does not send His servants out unprovided. The worry, not the money, is the barrier.

Augustine answers his students' request for guidance about material support in God's work: when a mission is guided by God, all that is required is provided on every level, including the monetary, and worry, guilt, and conflict are barriers the servant must not raise. Provision meets requirements rather than indulgence, and calls for humility, balance, and integrity. He releases those gathered from responsibility for the choices of others who resist the Light, and from any sense of unworthiness, for each soul answers for its own choices and all are worthy of God's care.

## Questions this message answers

- Will God provide for me financially if I do His work?
- Is it wrong to be paid for spiritual work?
- How do I stop worrying about money while serving God?
- How much is too much when asking God to provide?
- Am I responsible for people who reject my help?
- How do I let go of guilt about the choices other people make?
- How do I know if my work is guided by God?
- Why do some people resist healing when it is offered?
- How do I stop feeling unworthy of God's care?
- What does the Bible mean by consider the lilies of the field?
- What does the Bible mean when it says the labourer is worthy of his hire?

I’ve heard the conversation amongst my students regarding the support that they desire in doing God’s work. They’ve asked for guidance and I am here to give this guidance. First and foremost, when a child embarks upon a mission guided by God, all that is required is provided on every level, including a monetary level, and there should be no need for conflict or worry, a sense of guilt, or a sense that these material and spiritual aspects will not be provided, for indeed they will, and you must not put in front of you barriers to this. But it should also be noted that God’s servants must do so with humility and grace and balance.

At times, when you indulge yourself in the human condition you seek more than what you need and this is not in harmony. When you express yourselves with integrity, balance, fairness, and love there is no question in terms of what you require. Yes, choices must be made, but there should be no negative response to your efforts or consequences. There should be a sense of fulfillment, satisfaction, peace knowing that God has indeed guided you along whatever particular road you take. And there is no conflict, but joy and a sense of deep gratitude that God indeed does provide for His children. He shall provide in ways that meet the requirements of each child as they progress upon their path of service. When God’s Hand is upon an action taken in guidance and a desire to serve there is always harmony.

And though there may be negative responses from others outside of this circle of influence which you carry in your life, this is not to be paid attention to. It is merely a response to the actions taken in Light and some resist this. Some hold on to their own condition which weighs them down. Where they may indeed find healing and respite and an unburdening, they stubbornly hold on to what they carry, and because of this you are not required to have a sense of responsibility towards their choice, for each soul is responsible for what they choose, just as you are responsible for what you choose.

Do not allow or entertain any negative thinking in this regard, but all actions that you take must be in Love and you are all capable of sensing the flow of God’s Will and Love in whatever you do, to choose to take that high road of which our master speaks, to walk in that flow, to be carried by that flow, to allow God to provide for you as you trust in Him and have faith in His plan.

God bless you, my students. May you forever be carried in the flow of God’s Love and choose to be within this flow always and not allow any sense of unworthiness to affect your decisions in this regard. You are all worthy of God’s great care. You all walk in this Light, and you will all receive the bounty of His blessings in your lives. God bless you, my students. Your teacher Augustine has great love for you and will continue to walk with you upon your journeys and be with you when you serve God and travel and touch many souls. So many angels surround you. There is nothing to fear, to be of concern, for you are firmly within God’s grasp. God bless you. Your teacher Augustine loves you. God bless you.
