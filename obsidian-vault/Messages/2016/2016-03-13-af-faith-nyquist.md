# Keep the Flame Burning

**Spirit:** [[Spirits/faith-nyquist|Faith]] · **Medium:** Al Fike · **Date:** 2016-03-13

> [!quote] The Door
> No one may ever thank you or even notice. God sees the flame you keep burning, and that is the whole point.

Faith thanks the circle for their commitment to bring Light through prayer to a place she once knew, urging them to go in faith and humility rather than ambivalence, and to keep the flame of prayer burning there. She teaches that their steadfast presence sows seeds in those who witness it, that recognition from others is of no consequence since God sees their efforts, and that even the neediest and most resistant souls will be touched if they let God's Love flow through them unrestricted. God's Will, she assures them, will manifest, though it may take a circuitous route.

## Questions this message answers

- How do I keep going when I face resistance to doing God's work?
- Does it matter if no one recognizes or acknowledges my spiritual efforts?
- How does my quiet example affect people who witness it?
- How do I respond when the people I try to help react with anger or resentment?
- How does God's Will get accomplished despite obstacles?
- What does it mean to sow seeds wherever I go?
- Why does every loving choice strengthen me?
- What does the Lord's Prayer mean by on earth as it is in heaven?

My beloved friends, thank you for continuing with your commitment to this venture to bring Light to that place where I resided. Thank you that despite the barriers in place and the frustrations that you feel you have continued to listen to God's Desire and Will that you support this place in prayer and love, and I am well pleased that you will venture forth to that place to pray together and that you do so gently, with humility but with a firmness and a knowing that what you do there is God's Will. Do not feel ambivalent or hesitant but go there in faith expressing your true soul's desire in God's Will that you pray in that place, that source of Light, that you keep the flame burning, for that flame brings a great peace in the world and there will be those who gaze upon you and feel the strength of your commitment and feel the power of the Love that surrounds you and is within you and it will cause them to think, to pause, to wonder, and this is often how God moves in the world in these firm yet subtle ways, the flow of His Will, the flow of His Love, His blessings Touch those who are in darkness, there is an awakening, a glimmer, a touch of understanding, and when these seeds are sown there is great potential to bring greater Light, to bring Truth and Love.

Remember, my beloveds, it is your purpose to sow these seeds wherever you go, to be in that flow of God's Love, to walk in that Light, that great Light and river that is God's purpose, purposeful Will in this world, beloveds.

And as you are humble and willing, as you seek to do God's Will, He will bring His Light He will bring this Light and though your minds do not find acknowledgement or recognition from others, this is of no consequence, for God recognizes your efforts, God blesses you for your efforts, for your choice to do His bidding and this is what is important and brings a great change within you, my beloveds, as He blesses you with His Love and you are strengthened by every choice you make towards a harmony with His Will, this changes you irrevocably and it strengthens you and stokes the flame within each time you make this choice, each time you are willing to be guided, each time you love another soul you increase your Light, and is this not what you desire, to be closer to God and in doing so you open the door of service, you open the door to greater Light, you open the door to those needy souls who have come close to you. And often those who are the neediest feel judgment towards you and anger, resentment and you must be strong and steadfast allowing God's Love to flow through you unrestricted and that soul, so resistant and afraid, will be touched.

Continue, my beloved friends, to listen to the Will of God, to go where you are directed and to allow His Touch to reach to them through your prayers and your efforts, your journeys and your conversations and gatherings and all that you do in the name of this Truth. Continue, continue, despite whatever resistance you encounter, you must be steadfast and strong, for God's Will will manifest. It may take a circuitous route but it will be done in this world as it is in Heaven, it will be done. God bless you, my friends, and Faith is ever with you in your efforts guiding you and praying for you and assisting you whenever and wherever I can to bring Light and to help you in your efforts which are so important in this world, so very important. God bless you, my beloveds. I love you. I love you.
