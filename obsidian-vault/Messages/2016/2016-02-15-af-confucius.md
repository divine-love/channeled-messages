# Open Wide Your Souls

**Spirit:** [[Spirits/confucius|Confucius]] · **Medium:** Al Fike · **Date:** 2016-02-15

> [!quote] The Door
> On the coldest night, the warmth you are looking for is not in the room. It is waiting inside your own soul.

Confucius warmly invites the circle to set aside resistance and doubt and lay bare their souls on a winter's night, that the Father's Love may flow in and kindle its warmth and Light. He assures them this Touch brings healing, comfort, and the way to at-onement with God, which grows the soul into a fountain of wisdom, Love, and joy.

## Questions this message answers

- How do I open my soul to receive God's Love?
- How do I set aside doubt and resistance when I pray?
- What does it feel like when God's Love flows into the soul?
- How does God's Love heal my wounds and bring comfort?
- What is at-onement with God?
- How does receiving God's Love grow my soul?

---

God bless you, my little birds, 'tis Confucius and I see you alight again in this little circle of Light. You come for your nourishment to be together in this prayer for Love.

Open wide your souls, my beloveds, open wide your souls for you must put aside any resistance and doubt of this great Gift, which you must beseech your Heavenly Father to receive. Feel the longings within your souls, and on this winter's night, my beloveds, do you not feel the warmth of the Father's Love flowing into your souls kindling that fire, that warmth, that glow of Light within you? For the Father warms you, embraces you and lifts you up into that place of warmth and sunshine, Light and openness that you may feel His Loving Touch, His powerful presence within your consciousness.

Beloved souls, put aside your concerns, lay bare your soul, my beloveds, for the Father may Touch you deeply within bringing the healing and the comfort, healing the wounds, bringing wisdom and Light, showing you the way to at-onement with Him, at-onement, my beloveds, that great embrace by the Heavenly Father, it shall bring you ecstatic joy and deep and abiding peace. It shall grow your souls that you may be a fountain of wisdom and Light, Love and joy. Be unified in this desire, my beloveds, do not hesitate be together in Love, beloved souls, God is with you. The angels are with you in your efforts to seek Light, beloved souls, God bless you. Confucius loves you, Confucius loves you.
