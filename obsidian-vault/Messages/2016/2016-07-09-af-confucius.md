# The Awakened Soul

**Spirit:** [[Spirits/confucius|Confucius]] · **Medium:** Al Fike · **Date:** 2016-07-09

> [!quote] The Door
> The awakened soul understands Truth in ways the mind simply cannot

Confucius welcomes a circle of souls seeking Truth and gently directs them inward — to plumb the depths of their own souls — teaching that it is God's Love that ignites a soul to awaken, and that the awakened soul has a capacity to understand Truth that the mind alone simply cannot access.

## Questions this message answers

- What is an awakened soul?
- How does God's Love awaken the soul?
- Why can the awakened soul understand Truth in ways the mind cannot?
- How do I plumb the depths of my own soul?
- How do I come to truly know myself spiritually?
- What doors open when I pray for God's Love?
- How do I love myself truly and spiritually?

God bless you, precious souls, I am Confucius and I come to bless you as you gather together in your desire to understand this Truth of Love, this redeeming Touch from God. Beloved souls, ask your questions, speak your truth, come to know your true selves, that place within you that is your soul. Plummet deep within this place, for you are drawn to this Light that shall open the doors of Truth, of true understanding and come to a place where you can truly love yourselves and all within your world.

God reaches to you, my beloveds. God reaches and pulls you into this Light, to His Love so that you may be strengthened and healed, that you may add to your knowledge, that you may come to know your souls, beloveds. For it is the touch of God's Essence, His Love, that ignites a soul to awaken and thus far, you understand from your minds what deeper understanding comes with the awakened soul. The awakened soul has the capacity to understand Truth in ways that the mind cannot.

Beloved souls, seek God, seek at-onement with the Creator and the answers shall truly and surely follow as you push aside your resistance and acknowledge the true desires of your souls and in this simple act of prayer and longing so much comes as the doors to your souls open and the doors to infinite understanding come with this Touch of Love.

God bless you, beloved souls, and welcome to this little Circle of Light. God bless you. Confucius loves you and is by your side, precious Lights. God bless you.
