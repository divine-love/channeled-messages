# Message on Good Friday

**Spirit:** [[Spirits/mary|Mary, Mother of Jesus]] · **Medium:** Al Fike · **Date:** 2016-03-25

> [!quote] The Door
> They killed him for the message. Two thousand years later the myths remain, and the work of carrying the real one falls to you.

On Good Friday, Mary remembers her son's death at the hands of those who sought power and feared his message of Love, and grieves how the centuries since have distorted his Truth into myth and error. She honours the circle for carrying his clear message into the world, and urges them to keep defying the darkness, walking in Light, and taking up the lantern of Truth as he did, for the need is even greater now than when he walked the Earth.

## Questions this message answers

- What was the real message Jesus died for?
- Why has the message of Jesus been so distorted over the centuries?
- Why was Jesus put to death?
- What does Good Friday really mean?
- How do I carry the truth of Jesus into a world that misunderstands it?
- How do I keep going when standing for truth feels costly?
- What does it mean to be a lantern of light in a dark world?
- How do I defy evil and error in my own life?

---

God bless you, my children. I am Mary. Many years ago my son was murdered, he was put to death and he dared walk the Earth and spoke Truth and brought Love and Light. He defied the evil ones and those who sought power and control. He brought a message of Love, he touched many souls and still to this day he is acknowledged for this. Yet, many do not understand his message, the distortions that have gone through the centuries creating great myths and errors in the world.

Yet you, my beloved, beautiful brothers and sisters, have acknowledged a clear understanding of his message of Love and just as he stood and defied the darkness with Light and Love and paid the price for this, you my beautiful, precious brothers and sisters continue to carry this message in the world. You continue to defy the darkness and we, in the Celestial Kingdom, acknowledge your efforts to carry this Truth into the world, to continue this great effort to push back the darkness, the evil, and the error, to bring God's presence into this world in every heart and soul. You dwell upon this world and you will continue just as my son did to bring this Truth, to enact this Truth in your own lives, to teach where you can, to give as you can as God's channels of Love in the world.

Continue my beautiful, beautiful brothers and sisters, to push back the darkness, to live in Light, to walk in Truth, to defy evil and error, to be strong and forthright, humble and loving, to be that channel of Light which God wishes you to be, for there is such great need, even greater than when my son walked the Earth, to walk in this way. And it is our wish that many, many souls will take up this lantern, will walk this Path to bring greater Truth to this world, to defy all illusions and the misunderstandings, the myths and the errors, to bring that simple Truth of Love, God's great redeeming Love to the world.

Follow your hearts my beloved, beautiful brothers and sisters, follow your hearts and know that God touches every footfall. He blesses you each day and we all encourage to move forward in loving dedication to the Truths of Love. God bless you and thank you for your prayers, your loving acts, your efforts to teach the Truths. What greater aspirations can there be but these? What noble efforts can there be to bring peace to this world than this? God bless you for your efforts and you are truly loved, truly loved, beloved souls, God bless you. I am Mary and I love you as does my beloved son, as do many, many angels, as does God. God bless you.
