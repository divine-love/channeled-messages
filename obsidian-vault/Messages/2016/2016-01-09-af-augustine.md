# You Are Gardeners in God's Beautiful Garden

**Spirit:** [[Spirits/augustine|Augustine]] · **Medium:** Al Fike · **Date:** 2016-01-09

> [!quote] The Door
> What you think of as tending a small garden, God sees as the beginning of a paradise.

Augustine celebrates the community's growth and faithfulness, describing them as gardeners who, together with the angels and God, have brought a beautiful flower to bloom. He encourages them to continue planting seeds of love wherever God inspires, assuring them that what seems like simple effort will bring wondrous blessing to the world and that their bonds of love will become a great channel for God.

## Questions this message answers

- How does a small, faithful spiritual community grow into something that touches the world?
- What does it mean to plant seeds of love in God's service?
- How does God use the bonds of love between people to serve the world?
- Why does simple, faithful effort matter so much to God?
- How do I trust that my small contributions to God's work are making a difference?
- What does it look like when a spiritual community is bearing fruit?
- How do human effort and divine guidance work together in God's plan?

---

It is Augustine who loves you so and is so joyed at your accomplishments, your willingness to be loving channels for one another and you have been the gardeners, my beloveds, along with we in the spirit, and God in His Heavens have brought about the fruition, the blooming of a beautiful flower and you have tended and nurtured this flower, this bud, for a long time and now it blooms. It shall do great service in this world, this beautiful thing that you have created, that we all have encouraged and nurtured and in time you will bring forward a beautiful garden filled with many lovely and unique flowers, dedications to God grown in Love and wisdom nurtured by God's beautiful Light, and this process of growth and blooming, planting seeds that will come to create beautiful flowers shall continue as God inspires you to plant another seed here, another seed there until this beautiful garden is created and it shall be a paradise, a wondrous place where many can come and enjoy its beauty, be nourished by Love, be strengthened by respite and Truth and healing.

Continue, my gardeners, continue to plant the seeds, to pray to God for His Touch to warm the Earth and bring the beautiful, unique creation of Love forth from the ground. You shall continue to work together. There is truly nothing stopping you from this, for you have built a great, wondrous bond of Love between you. This is strong, this is beautiful and it will continue to grow and strengthen and God will use this and use the bonds that outgrow from this to others to create a wondrous, great channel of Love for the world. It is coming. You are growing. The garden begins to bloom and you must look around you, my beloveds, and acknowledge that you had a part to play in what is and what is coming.

My beautiful gardeners, continue in your efforts as you till the soil, plant your seeds, look to the Heavens for its benediction upon your work. And although these simple acts may seem a trifle to you, my beloveds, it is not. Wondrous things will grow from this. Your simple efforts to love will bring wondrous, joyous, beautiful blessings to this world. Continue, my beloveds, and we will continue to support you in all your efforts to serve God, to bring beauty, to nurture this wondrous life that is God's Love and Truth. God bless you and keep you and guide you. Your teacher Augustine loves you, beloveds. God bless you.
