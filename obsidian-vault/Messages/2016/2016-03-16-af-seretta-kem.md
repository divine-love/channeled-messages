# You Are Truly Beautiful Souls

**Spirit:** [[Spirits/seretta-kem|Seretta Kem]] · **Medium:** Al Fike · **Date:** 2016-03-16

> [!quote] The Door
> The spirits keep telling you that you are beautiful. What would change if you finally believed them?

Seretta Kem affirms that Jesus loves each soul in the circle and holds them close, and promises to return through the instrument to bring greater knowledge. Humbled by the Light of their prayer together, he tells them they are truly beautiful souls and expresses his hope that they will one day fully accept this and learn to love themselves with great joy.

## Questions this message answers

- How do I learn to truly love myself?
- Why is it so hard to accept that I am loved?
- Does Jesus really love each person individually?
- What does it mean to be part of the flock who serve God through love?
- Why do spirits say we are beautiful when we cannot see it in ourselves?
- How does praying together create a beautiful light?

---

Yes, our beloved brother and master Jesus truly loves you, my beloveds, and he is close to each one of you, and you are truly a part of his flock who serve God through Love. I will attempt to reiterate this message which you were so taken with but I must do so at some time in the future. So I promise you, I will work through this instrument to bring greater knowledge to you, for it is my great joy and pleasure to do so and I, too, love you, and I am humbled by the Light that is here in this place as you pray together, such a beautiful Light. You are truly beautiful souls, each one of you, and I hope that someday you can truly accept this, how beautiful you are, and to love yourselves fully with great joy and true knowledge that you are loved, that you carry love, that you are beautiful, truly beautiful. God bless you, beloved, beautiful souls. Your servant Seretta Kem is always by your side. God bless you.
