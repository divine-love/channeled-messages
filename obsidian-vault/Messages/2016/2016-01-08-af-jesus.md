# Encouragement from Jesus

**Spirit:** [[Spirits/jesus|Jesus]] · **Medium:** Al Fike · **Date:** 2016-01-08

> [!quote] The Door
> Your soul already knows what is coming. The joy you feel is the anticipation of a door God has not yet opened.

Jesus offers encouragement to a small group, opening in prayer and then assuring them that great changes and opportunities await as they commit themselves to God's plan. He tells them their souls already sense what is to come, that they will teach and serve as vanguards carrying God's Love and healing to those in darkness, and that doors will open in the perfect timing of God's Will. He promises he is with them always and that all they do will come to reflect this Love.

## Questions this message answers

- How does Jesus encourage those who feel called to serve?
- What does it mean to be an instrument of God in the world?
- How do I know if God has a purpose for my life?
- What does it mean that God will open doors in His perfect timing?
- How can I trust that change is coming even when I cannot see it yet?
- What does it mean to be a vanguard of God's Love?
- Does Jesus still call people to be his disciples today?
- How can I carry light to people who are suffering or in darkness?
- Why does my soul sometimes sense what is coming before my mind understands it?
- What does it mean to live in the flow of God's Will?
