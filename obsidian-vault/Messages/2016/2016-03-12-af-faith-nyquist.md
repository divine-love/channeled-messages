# A Door Has Opened for You

**Spirit:** [[Spirits/faith-nyquist|Faith]] · **Medium:** Al Fike · **Date:** 2016-03-12

> [!quote] The Door
> A door has opened. You cannot see the plan behind it, but your part is only to walk through in joy.

Faith rejoices that a door has opened and urges the circle to walk through it in joy, trusting God to orchestrate what unfolds rather than worrying over outcomes or material needs. She affirms that each of them has an opportunity to be an integral part of God's plan for the salvation of mankind, that travelling the world in prayer as a channel of Love plants seeds whose harvest will be great, and that every prayer they make furthers that plan.

## Questions this message answers

- How do I trust God's plan when I cannot see how things will unfold?
- How do I stop worrying about outcomes and let God orchestrate them?
- How do I stop worrying about money while doing God's work?
- What does it mean to be part of God's plan for the world?
- Why does prayer matter so much, even when I cannot see its effect?
- How does being a channel of Love plant seeds for the future?
- How do I know when to walk through a door that has opened for me?

It is Faith. A door has opened for you. Walk through that door in joy and know that God will orchestrate your gathering and bring the blessings to all those present. And do not worry and fuss about the outcomes and how this is done but be a channel of Love. Bind all your brothers and sisters together in prayer, bring love and Light. All will be well, all will be well. We are joyed at the outcome and a door has been opened and you, my daughter, have helped to facilitate this, by your prayers and your presence. Continue to be an ally for God in this place. It is important and you cannot see the plans that are unfolding, but God does have a plan and as long as you listen to your guidance and enact this guidance in accordance to God's Will, you will be an integral part of this plan. Each of you has an opportunity to be a part of God's plan for the salvation of mankind and there are many who will join you in this unfolding, unfolding events that will bring about great change, great Light in this world. Seek to be a part of this, have faith you are a part of this. And every prayer that you make for this world furthers God's plan.

And do not worry for the material. God will provide for your needs. You do not recognize the value of your efforts, how important it is to travel this world in prayer as a channel of Love. It is very important. It brings a power; it brings an effect wherever you go. And you plant seeds, you plant many seeds with a new world and the harvest will be great in time.

Please continue your efforts and your willingness to go where God guides you. Yes, much will be accomplished and there is so much work to do, so much work awaits, so many souls await you, my beloveds. God bless you, I am Faith and I love you dearly, and I am so pleased with your efforts and the dear daughter who speaks on behalf of the angels to those souls who make the decisions in that place. Many blessings to you all, many blessings of Love. God bless you. God bless you.
