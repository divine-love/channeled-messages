# God's Little Surprises

**Spirit:** [[Spirits/goldie|Goldie]] · **Medium:** Al Fike · **Date:** 2016-03-06

> [!quote] The Door
> The people who arrive in your life unannounced may be the ones God planned for all along.

Goldie delights in the surprising ways God brings souls together in Love, assuring the gathered that this is what is meant for them: to work together, to be generous of heart, and to support one another. She promises to keep surprising them whenever she can.

## Questions this message answers

- Why does God bring certain people into my life unexpectedly?
- What does it mean to work together in love?
- How can I be more generous of heart with the people around me?
- Are the surprising connections in my life part of God's plan?

---

Yes, God brings you together in surprising ways does He not, for interesting purposes, and He will continue to surprise you and bring you together in Love, and this is what is meant for you, my beloveds, to be together in Love, to work together in whatever way in Love, to be generous of heart, to contribute what you can, to support one another. Blessings to you, beloveds, and I will continue and endeavor to surprise you whenever I can. God bless you, [name-removed], you are God's little surprises. God bless you.
