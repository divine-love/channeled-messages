# Mary Will Be with You in Your Prayers

**Spirit:** [[Spirits/mary|Mary]] · **Medium:** Al Fike · **Date:** 2016-01-18

> [!quote] The Door
> When you go to comfort another, you do not go alone. Mary and a host of angels go with you.

Mary blesses those gathered with God's peace, promising to be present with them and a host of angels as they pray and visit a soul in need. She affirms that each carries a mission to bring comfort, Light, and the embrace of God's Love, and that this work of loving prayer is God's Will.

## Questions this message answers

- How do I bring comfort to someone who is suffering?
- How can I be a channel of God's Love when I pray for another person?
- Are angels present with us when we pray together for someone?
- How do I find peace and strength to rest in my soul?
- What does it mean that comforting others in prayer is God's Will?
