# Come Together Again With Your Questions

**Spirit:** [[Spirits/confucius|Confucius]] · **Medium:** Al Fike · **Date:** 2019-02-01

> [!quote] The Door
> You think you may be lost. Confucius answers: no, you have gained the world of God, and the portal is opening wide.

Confucius blesses the harmony the circle has built in prayer and urges them to gather again in the coming year to sit, contemplate, and formulate their questions and their desire for more truth. He reassures the circle host that she is not lost but set upon the path God designed for her purpose and service, that more souls will be drawn to the Light she builds within, and that many gifts are coming as the portal opens wide.

## Questions this message answers

- Why is it valuable to come together with others in prayer and bring our questions to God?
- How do I know I am on the path God intends for me?
- What does it mean to feel lost on a spiritual path, and how do I know I am not?
- How does my own spiritual growth draw others to me?
- What makes someone a good spiritual teacher?
- Is there always more to learn and receive on the spiritual path?
- How does building the Light within my soul affect the people around me?
- What are the thresholds God prepares for us to walk through in service?
