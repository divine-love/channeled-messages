# Mary Brings Comfort for the Journey

**Spirit:** [[Spirits/mary|Mary, Mother of Jesus]] · **Medium:** Al Fike · **Date:** 2019-02-24

> [!quote] The Door
> The thing that holds you down is not as fixed as it feels. It falls away by the measure of your faith.

Mary comes to bring comfort to those she calls her beloved charges, praying for their healing, upliftment, and deep peace on life's journeys. She assures them that as their souls long for God's blessing the light within will only increase, and that whatever debilitates and holds them down will fall away by the measure of their faith, prayer, and resolve, pushed aside by God and awakened in love.

## Questions this message answers

- How do I find comfort when life's journey feels heavy?
- How does God sustain me through difficult times?
- How does faith help remove the things that hold me back?
- What role do my own prayers and resolve play in my healing?
- How does the light within the soul grow stronger over time?
- Can the things that debilitate me really fall away?
- What does the Bible mean when it says God will uphold you with His right hand?
- How do I trust that God will clear the obstacles in my path?

I have come to bring you comfort, my beloved charges. I continue to pray for your healing and upliftment, for peace, deep peace, for you all. On life's journeys, God will surely sustain you and uphold you in light. As your souls long for His great blessing so this light shall only increase and those things that debilitate you and hold you down will fall away. It is a measure of your faith, beloveds, a measure of your prayers, a measure of your resolve. All that stands in your way may be pushed aside by God and awakened in love. God bless you, beloveds. I am with you. All of us are with you. I am here and I too love you dearly.
