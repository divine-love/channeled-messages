# May You Come to Know God's Blessings

**Spirit:** [[Spirits/confucius|Confucius]] · **Medium:** Al Fike · **Date:** 2019-02-23

> [!quote] The Door
> The blessing you are hoping to receive is already in your soul. Confucius only asks that you come to know it.

Confucius offers a short, tender blessing to the travelling group, wishing that they come to know the deep and wondrous blessings of God already within their souls. He assures them that he continues to bring healing to strengthen their bodies and help them navigate the challenges of the world.

## Questions this message answers

- How do I come to know the blessings God has placed within my soul?
- Can prayer and spirit healing strengthen the body?
- How does God help me navigate the challenges of the world?
- What does it mean that God's blessings are already within me?
- How do I find God's blessings in the middle of everyday struggles?
- What does the Bible mean when it says the kingdom of God is within you?
