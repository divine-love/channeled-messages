# Birthday Prayer for Jeanne and Encouragement to All

**Spirit:** [[Spirits/jesus|Jesus of Nazareth]] · **Medium:** Al Fike · **Date:** 2019-04-03

> [!quote] The Door
> You are not a sheep in a flock. God gave you a path that is yours alone. Walk it.

Jesus comes to a circle honouring one of their own on her birthday, affirming that the praise spoken of her is all true and more, for he sees her soul clearly as bright and beautiful, a servant of God who has uplifted many. He widens the blessing to all present, telling them they are lambs of God who each walk a unique path rather than follow as sheep, and calls them to be swept up in the Living Waters of God's Love so that the gifts within them awaken. He assures them they are his family and he will never leave them.

## Questions this message answers

- How does God see my soul compared to how others see me?
- What does it mean that each person has a unique path to God?
- Why does Jesus say we are lambs of God but not sheep who simply follow?
- How do I discover the unique gifts within my soul?
- What are the Living Waters of God's Love?
- What does the Bible mean when it says every good and perfect gift comes from above?
- How do I find my own beautiful destiny?
- What does it mean to be part of the family of God?
- How do I know that I am truly loved and never alone?
- How does serving others make a soul bright and beautiful?

Rest in your souls, beloveds. I come, I am Jesus. I come to acknowledge this dear child. To affirm the words that you have spoken of her, for they are all true, and more. For though you see the souls of your brothers and sisters dimly, I see them clearly and the soul of this dear soul is bright and beautiful and worthy of praise. For she has touched many souls in joy and love. She has uplifted many in many different ways. She has walked this world as a servant of God and found her way into great Light through these efforts and prayers. So she is a bright star amongst you, as each one here is a bright and beautiful soul, with many unique gifts and much to give to this world of yours.

Beloveds, walk in the Light, for you are all lambs of God. Though you do not walk like a sheep who follows, rather you are each unique and beautiful souls who walk their path to God upon this unique path that is gifted to you by God. Beloved souls, continue to grow in Love, to awaken in Love, to follow the Light of your Father's blessings upon you. Allow yourself to be swept up in the mighty rivers, the Living Waters of God's Love. For in this, all good and perfect gifts within your souls will be awakened and you will come to express these true parts of yourselves. In this will be great joy. In this will be the power of God working through you. In this will be your unique path, your beautiful destiny that is shaped by Love and Light, purity and humility. Walk with me, beloved souls. Walk with God. Walk knowing your own true and beautiful selves and all that you do and all that you are will manifest in Light and be a beautiful outpouring of Love.

You are my brothers and sisters. You are a part of my family. I, in my prayers and efforts, assure that you will be in the Light, protected and nurtured in Love. Know this, beloved souls. Know this that I am with you, that I shall never leave, that we are a part of a great family of souls who dwell in the Light.

May God continue to bless you as He has done so abundantly over the years and will carry you ever further in Light. I am your brother, your friend. I am Jesus and I love you. God bless you.
