# Walk in the Grace of God's Love

**Spirit:** [[Spirits/mary|Mary]] · **Medium:** Al Fike · **Date:** 2015-03-29

> [!quote] The Door
> As you give Love so shall you receive Love. Do not falter - God will guide you to where you are meant to be

Mary blesses those gathered in New York, affirming that as they reach out in love to those in need - the children, the lost souls of the world - they bring blessing to themselves as well as to others. She calls them not to falter, to trust that God will guide them where they are meant to go and fill their souls with His Love in great abundance, that they may walk each day in the Grace of God's Love.

## Questions this message answers

- What does the Bible mean when it says give and it shall be given unto you?
- How do I serve others without burning out?
- What does it mean to walk in the Grace of God's Love?
- Is Jesus really the Way the Truth and the Life as he claimed?
- How does giving love to others bring love back to me?
- How do I find purpose and meaning in my life?

And I come to tell you about the beauty that is within your souls, beloved children. And when you reach out to those in need, to the children in this world, to the lost souls in this world, you bring a light into yourselves and you bring a blessing to yourself. As you give Love so shall you receive Love. As you allow yourselves to open in loving ways to one another, to all the children in this world, you will know God's Grace and His blessing.

Yes, this hungering world, this painful world, this burdened world needs those who are strong enough and in the flow of God's Love to bring healing and comfort, to bring light where there is darkness, to walk in strength and truth, to do this work, to bring this healing touch.

Do not falter, my beloveds, but know that as you walk in this light, you will do God's work, you will do God's work. Do not falter, but have faith and God will guide you to where you are meant to be and go. And He will fill your souls with His Love. He will fill you with His Love in great abundance. Open yourself to this blessing and know that this is the Way to the Truth and the Light. And true life, life abundance, life filled with joy, life filled with purpose and meaning.

My beloveds, my beloveds, continue with your efforts. Grow in the Love. And know that there is a great work ahead, a great work. Be that channel of Love each day and you will walk in the Grace of God's Love. God bless you, God bless you. I am Mary, and I love you so. God bless you.
