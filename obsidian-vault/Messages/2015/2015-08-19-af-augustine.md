# We Embark on a Journey to Change the World

**Spirit:** [[Spirits/augustine|Augustine]] · **Medium:** Al Fike · **Date:** 2015-08-19

> [!quote] The Door
> The world is crying out and God's answer is a small group of souls praying together; do not underestimate what that can set in motion.

Augustine speaks of the great beam of Light opened to the world through the gathering's prayers, assuring them that their network of love will multiply and multiply until the power of their longing souls changes the world. He calls them to embark together on a journey to heal the world and all that within each soul that is not in harmony with God's Love.

## Questions this message answers

- Can a small group of praying souls really change the world?
- How does our prayer together create a network of light for others?
- What does it mean that the planet itself cries out for healing?
- How do I become part of God's plan to bring healing to the world?
- How does praying together draw in other souls who are longing for truth?
- What does it mean to be immersed in a great pool of God's Light?
- How does healing within my own soul contribute to healing the world?

---

It is your teacher Augustine and I come to you today to speak of the Love that is directed towards you, my beloveds, by all the angels in the Celestial Kingdom who are praying with you at this moment, opening a great portal of Light to this world and a great beam of Light to your souls. You perform a great service, my beloveds, in coming together, you perform a service for God to bring His Light and Love, peace and comfort into this world. And in many ways you are required to be a part of this effort, for in your soul's desires and longings, in your coming together in Love and harmony these things are possible, this Light manifests in your world, and as this Light manifests in your world it manifests throughout the world of spirit. You bring a great opportunity not only for yourselves but for many, many souls in the spirit world and for those souls from this Earth who are longing what you long for, who are receptive and open to these blessings. And as you pray together, as you will, in these coming days you create a bond, a network of Light and Love to those yearning souls who wish to accompany you and this will manifest and draw in those many souls in the world so that yes indeed your numbers will multiply and multiply and multiply so that the power of your prayer is the power of the longing of your souls and will change the world.

You must change the world for this world is in deep jeopardy. This planet cries out for healing and balance and God wishes for you, my beloveds, to facilitate His desire to bring this balance and healing to the world. Each of you has this possibility and ability to help heal your world and manifest Love in this world to all your brothers and sisters, to bring the healing balm which will bring balance and vitality back to this world that in this time cringes and shudders for all the misdeeds and all that is not in harmony with Love and Love of Creation.

Beloved souls, we embark upon a journey together to help heal your world and to help heal the world within you, all of those infirmities, all of those places that are not in harmony with God's Love, to heal the wounds, to bring Love to all parts of your being so that you may truly acknowledge yourselves as children of your Creator. Bring all these living waters into your souls, my beloveds. This water fills this room; you are immersed. You are at this moment in a great pool of Light. Drink deep, my beloveds, for this water is alive and this water enlivens you from deep within, this healing touch, this transformative blessing, this great and magnificent emanation from your Heavenly Father. He is with you, my beloveds, He longs for you to be with Him, to truly know that you are a part of the firmament of His Creation that dances in the Love of His Soul.

God bless you, my beloveds, and may you continue to long and pray, to love and laugh, to be together in this wondrous journey that is meant for you, that awaits you, that is filled with great adventure, blessings, and discoveries. God bless you, beautiful souls, beautiful souls. God bless you.
