# Birthday Blessings for Al

**Spirit:** [[Spirits/goldie|Goldie]] · **Medium:** Al Fike · **Date:** 2015-07-25

> [!quote] The Door
> God counts the hairs on your head and loves every one of them; joy is not a reward for holiness, it is a gift for simply being His child.

Goldie offers a playful and warm birthday blessing for Al Fike, celebrating his soul's beauty and encouraging all gathered to embrace the joy, laughter, and lightness of heart that God wishes for every one of His children.

## Questions this message answers

- Does God want us to experience joy and laughter in our spiritual lives?
- Is it possible to feel truly light and free in God's Love?
- How do I cultivate more joy in my spiritual practice?
- What does it mean to know the joy of fellowship with other souls?

---

Yes, this beloved instrument is getting older – and wiser. And he is making it easier for God to count the hairs upon his head, and he is beloved, beloved by many [laughing]. Such a precious soul and all of you are precious souls in this circle. Know the joy of God's Love. Know the joy of fellowship, of loving one another and being in God's Light. It is a joyous thing, a beautiful thing. And you all come to know great and deep joy, laughter and a lightness of heart that is meant for you, for God wishes for all his children to know joy and freedom and peace. Blessings to you, beloveds, and to this boy who has grown up to be a man. Blessings to you. God bless you. Goldie loves you.
