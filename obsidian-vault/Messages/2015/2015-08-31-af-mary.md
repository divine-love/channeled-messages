# Mary Assures the Group of Her Continuing Presence and Love

**Spirit:** [[Spirits/mary|Mary]] · **Medium:** Al Fike · **Date:** 2015-08-31

> [!quote] The Door
> Mary is not watching from a distance. She is walking beside you in the Light right now.

Mary offers tender reassurance to a prayer group, affirming that she is always present with them, her prayers constant and her love deep. She notes that channels of Divine Love in the world are few and tended with great care by celestial spirits, and encourages the group to continue seeking God's Love.

## Questions this message answers

- Do celestial spirits really pray alongside us and walk with us on the spiritual path?
- How do I know I am not alone on my spiritual journey?
- Are there spirits who watch over those who pray for Divine Love?
- What does it mean to be a channel of love in the world?
- Why are channels of Divine Love so rare, and why does that matter?
- Can I trust that my prayers are heard and that I am supported from the spirit world?

---

I have come to reassure you that I am always by your side and my love for you is deep and encompassing, and you are always in my consciousness, my beloved, beloved. My prayers for you are constant. You walk in the Light of God's Love and I walk in that Light with you.

The channels of Love in this world are few and are tended to with great care and attention. We will always be with you, my beloveds, nurturing, encouraging and loving. Continue to seek your Heavenly Father. Pray for His Love to enter your souls and we will always pray with you, help you upon your journey, and use you to bring blessings to many souls. I love you, my beloved, beloved, dear souls. I love you dearly.
