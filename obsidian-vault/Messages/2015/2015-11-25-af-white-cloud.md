# Be Warriors of Light

**Spirit:** [[Spirits/white-cloud|White Cloud]] · **Medium:** Al Fike · **Date:** 2015-11-25

> [!quote] The Door
> Not words. Not arguments. A life so full of love that souls shift simply by standing near you.

White Cloud honours the many souls drawn to a long-established Circle of Light and calls those present to be warriors of Light in the world, expressing God's Love through every avenue and gift available to them. He teaches that words alone do not move souls — only Love permeating every action can stop the tide of darkness and bring healing and truth to a yearning humanity.

## Questions this message answers

- What does it mean to be a warrior of Light?
- How do I bring God's Love into everything I do in daily life?
- Why do words alone fail to change souls while love succeeds?
- How do I discover the spiritual gifts I don't yet know I have?
- How can I serve God through my ordinary daily work and interactions?
- What does it mean to walk with integrity on the spiritual path?
- How does one person's Light help stop the tide of darkness in the world?
- How does aligning with God's Love transform the effect of everything I do?

---

I am White Cloud and I've come to acknowledge the efforts that you have made to bring many beautiful souls into our midst both here in the flesh and in spirit. There are many beautiful spirits from many different cultures and places within the spirit realms that have been drawn here to the Light seeking truth, seeking answers, and much Light has been brought to this place, this refuge that has been indeed, a beacon of Light for many, many years.

As my beloved precious and most precious instrument, our dear Barbara, has worked many years to bring a condition - a spiritual Light - to this place, her home. As I have been with her all of her life helping her, supporting her on her spiritual quest and for her dear sister who indeed does God's work for many in this world through her loving ways. And, for you, all of you, my beloveds, who in your own way bring Light into the world, this world so in need of Light, this world filled with lost souls who yearn for peace, who yearn for the touch of the Great White Spirit but who know not what it is they yearn for or how to receive this precious touch. You, my beloved, precious souls, must bring the message of Light and Peace and Love to this world, for you have been blessed mightily, and you have been given a precious prize and you walk in Light. You cannot hide this Light but must express it in your own unique ways in this world through all the avenues and the abilities that you have and the ones that you are not even aware that you have but exist nonetheless. You must discover all of your gifts, uncover them and have them flourish in the Light of the Father's Love. You are entrusted with this; God has given you this, this journey upon this world, to bring Light, to bring Truth, to bring Love to your fellow man.

Some of you work with children, some of you work with the lost and the hungry, some of you walk along the streets with open hearts and you embrace many. There are many avenues to serve God, many ways you can bring Light, but you must, you truly must be with your Heavenly Father with each step so that Light, expression of Love, Divine Love must accompany all that you do and transform all that you do to something that touches and moves a soul of another. Words do not do this; Love does this. You must love and put love into everything. The Love of God must permeate your lives, your beings, all that you are and all that you do must be in the flow of God's Love, for when you are in this alignment with the Creator you bring the change, you bring the healing, you bring the Truth that is required in this world to stop the tide of darkness, to shift the souls of man and in this the thinking, the attitudes, the negativity, the error, the insensitivity, the hatred and the anger, the fear will dissipate and Light will move in filling that hollow place within every soul.

Be warriors of Light, my dear friends. Walk in this way. Always be an example and walk for Truth, walk with integrity, walk that you may not ever compromise what you know to be true, what is truly you, your beautiful souls burning bright in the world. Yes, you bring Light and we bring Light and together we shall carry this Light into the world as God touches all and brings us to that place of harmony and peace and joy.

Bless you, beloved souls. I am White Cloud and I have been with you many, many times. I am pleased to speak through this instrument whom I have watched grow over the years as this precious instrument's son and I too watch over him as I do for many of you. God bless you and keep you in His Light. God bless you.
