# Cleansing of Your Soul Brings Understanding, Perception and Wisdom

**Spirit:** [[Spirits/andrew|Andrew]] · **Medium:** Al Fike · **Date:** 2015-01-05

> [!quote] The Door
> The pain of cleansing is the price of a wisdom that cannot be learned any other way

Andrew explains that receiving God's Divine Love triggers a necessary process of soul expiation — a cleansing of accumulated inharmonious conditions — which the soul welcomes but the mind resists. He encourages his listeners to embrace this uncomfortable inner transformation joyfully, assuring them that it leads to the rebirth of the soul and ever-deeper at-onement with God.

## Questions this message answers

- What is soul expiation?
- Why do I feel emotional turmoil when I pray for God's Love?
- What is the new birth of the soul?
- Why does the mind resist spiritual growth?
- How does God cleanse the soul?
- What does it mean to be born again spiritually?
- Why is spiritual transformation uncomfortable?
- How does Divine Love purify the soul?
- What is the struggle between the soul and the mind?
- How do I become a new creature in God's Love?

---

God bless you beloved souls. There are many details and aspects of this world that need attending to, yet it is your souls, my beloveds, your souls that cry out to move forward in your lives towards spiritual progression and your efforts to be a channel of Love to others and to walk this Divine Path unencumbered. The coming year will be a year of great change and many opportunities and some of you feel restless and have difficulty feeling comfortable within yourselves. This is because God is preparing you and beseeching you to come ever closer to this Light and Love, this Light of Truth. And when you receive God's Touch there is a corresponding change within you and the conditions which have built up from your lifetime of experience and from the prevailing conditions of this world and from the conditions of your forefathers, there is a need to have much of this expiated from your souls. This cleansing of your souls will bring evermore understanding, perception and wisdom within and is a way for God to use you in this world.

God's Love is the most powerful element in the universe my beloveds and when it touches you deep within your souls there is a corresponding shift and cleansing. This is the power of God's Love and in response your mind often feels the disruption from within and casts about seeking for reasons which it does not truly understand. The soul knows, recognizes and allows this expiation to happen and the mind resists and wishes to have control over these stray emotions and confusing feelings within. Yes, this is a struggle which all souls who receive the Father's Love must engage with. This is a struggle between light and darkness, disharmony and pain. To bring about the peace that passes all understanding so that it may flourish within your souls, one must allow these inharmonious conditions to melt and this will bring the Love to flood within you.

God's Touch is upon you and the Angels surround you supporting what is in every way the rebirth of the soul for you to become a new creature, immortal and pure and filled with God's Love, ever to come into at onement with Him. This path my beloveds awaits you all, this process of cleansing and purifying, of coming ever closer to that Light, of coming ever closer to the sure knowledge of whom you are: God's creation, dear, beautiful, and beloved. Yes, it is not easy but I tell you the rewards are great, the joy that is coming will be worth the pain and the discomfort. Then your feet will be more fully upon the Divine Path. You will know God; you will know God, my beloveds in a true and clear way. This is your soul's desire. This is what you seek and in that loving, powerful way you will find the answers to all of your questions and you will know how very loved you are, how very precious and beautiful and glorious is your wondrous soul and being. Do not resist the changes within you, beloveds, but accept with joy that God is changing you and you are coming to the new birth of your soul. This is God's greatest gift to you and in giving this to you, you will bring light into the world. And in bringing light into the world you bring the possibilities of experiencing this new birth, this soulful awakening.

Much awaits you, my beloveds. The time grows short and the work great. Prepare yourselves and accept your Heavenly Father in all ways deeply and purely within your being. Accept in love your Creator. Changes are happening, beloveds, both within you and around you. Walk joyously upon your path and God will lead you in light and you will indeed fulfill your purpose. Much awaits, much awaits you and you are truly loved. God bless you, beloveds. Andrew is with you. Yes, I am with you and I love you. God bless you.
