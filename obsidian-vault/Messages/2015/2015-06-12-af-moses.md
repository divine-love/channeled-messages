# Do Not Stand in the Way of Your Own True and Perfect Healing

**Spirit:** [[Spirits/moses|Moses]] · **Medium:** Al Fike · **Date:** 2015-06-12

> [!quote] The Door
> Do not stand in the way of your own true and perfect healing, but accept as a child that God will carry you into greater Light and joy

Moses encourages the circle that so much awaits within each soul, eager to emerge, and that the only thing standing in the way is what each person clings to that is no longer truly part of them. He calls them to accept God's healing as a child would, to trust in God's care for their loved ones as they step forward in service, and to know that the rebirth of their souls is but a breath away.

## Questions this message answers

- How do I stop standing in the way of my own healing?
- What are encrustations on the soul and how do I release them?
- How do I let go of old habits and ways of thinking that hold me back?
- How do I trust God to care for my loved ones as I pursue spiritual service?
- What does it mean that the rebirth of my soul is close?
- How do I accept God's healing with the openness of a child?
- Why do I cling to old conditions even when they no longer serve me?
- What does the Bible mean when it says be transformed by the renewing of your mind?
- How do I know my soul is on the verge of rebirth?
- How does fear and anxiety block spiritual progress?
- What does it mean that God will never forsake me?
- What does the Bible say about being born again and how does Divine Love relate to this?

God bless you and may the Father's Love find a deep and abiding place within your souls, beloved children of God. I am Moses. I have come to be with you in your prayers and to tell you that your Heavenly Father has a great work awaiting you each one, each precious soul has much work to do in this world in the service of our Heavenly Father. And the changes within your lives and within yourselves are all in preparation for you to venture out into the world, and walk in Light and Truth, and be a channel of Love in the world.

Each of you is surrounded by angels, each of you feels the joy within your souls that is the Father's Love burning bright within and leading you into greater Light and understanding. So much awaits you, my beloved precious souls, so much. And the Path opens before you. You feel its compelling pull towards the Light, towards God, towards greater service and greater enlightenment.

God will care for those you Love, my beloveds, as you embark upon your journeys and walk upon this path of service. Trust in the caring Hand of God for your loved ones. Trust in the guiding Hand of God with each step you take in this world. But do not let your fears and anxieties rule your life, my beloveds. Feel at peace and know that you are guided and know that there are precious few in this world who receive such Light, and guidance, and attention, and care. Accept this, bring this into your hearts, allow the fullness of God's Love to heal all that is within, that is in pain, that does not accept love and allow the great strength and gifts that are within your souls to manifest and emerge in all its beauty and power and grace.

So much is within each of you, my beloveds, waiting, anticipating, eager to come forth in the world, to bring Light and Truth, healing and comfort to the lost souls. All that stands in your way is that which you cling to that is not wholly a part of you anymore, my beloveds. Old habits and thoughts, old ways of thinking. The encrustations upon your soul, cling tenaciously, yet the love within you burns and releases these conditions and it is for you to accept this blessing and to let go of that which you cling to. This is not the real you, my beloveds, this is the old you and your soul stands at the verge of rebirth, and great joy awaits, and great insight, and understanding of who you truly are, and who God truly is.

Much awaits you beloveds, do not stand in the way of your own true and perfect healing, but accept as a child that God will carry you into greater Light and joy.

Precious children, we in spirit await, we await your healing and your acceptance of yourselves in true Love. Continue in your prayers. Continue to ask your Heavenly Father to cleanse your soul with His Love for the rebirth that is but a breath away, my beloveds, a breath away. You walk in Love and Light and we walk with you as you journey upon your Path, a Path of great discovery, and we love you and we are joyed at your progress and the Light that burns within you. You do not walk alone, but you walk with God and His angels always, never to forsake you and always to support you. As you walk through this life and walk in Faith, beloveds, be at peace and know that you are loved beyond measure. God bless you, I am Moses. I come to give you this message of Love and encouragement that you may know how close you are to God and your true selves. Blessings to you, my beloveds. Blessings to you.
