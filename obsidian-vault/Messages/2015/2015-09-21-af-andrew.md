# You Are Now Strong Enough to Walk in This Harsh World without Fear

**Spirit:** [[Spirits/andrew|Andrew]] · **Medium:** Al Fike · **Date:** 2015-09-21

> [!quote] The Door
> God has placed His Hand upon you. Who, then, can hurt you?

Andrew encourages the group that God's Love has now given them the strength and confidence to walk through a harsh world without fear, releasing doubt and self-protection. He teaches that judgment closes the door to Love, and calls them to pray for those who cause harm, seeing every soul as precious and capable of transformation.

## Questions this message answers

- How does Divine Love replace fear with spiritual confidence?
- What does the Bible mean by Living Waters and how does that apply to prayer for Divine Love?
- How do I love people who judge me or treat me with hostility?
- Why does judging others make my own spiritual life worse?
- How do I stop protecting myself emotionally and open up again after being hurt?
- Can God's Love really protect me from the negativity of the world around me?
- What does it mean to see every person as a precious soul regardless of how they behave?
- How do I stop fearing what other people think of me?
- What are the treasures of the soul and how do I accumulate them?
- How can prayer for someone who has wronged me actually help both of us?

---

It is Andrew. Once again you come to the well of the Father's Love to quench your thirst, to drink deeply of these Living Waters that shall never end and always be there for you to receive and be open to. For God is always emanating His Love to you, my beloveds, it is always available for you as His Holy Spirit touches your souls in response to your longings, your desire to receive this Gift, this beautiful Gift of Love, His Essence flowing into you, and changing you, and healing you, and bringing you closer to His Being. Making you more in harmony with His Being and His laws of Creation for you, His laws of Love bring their Great flow of blessings, and awareness, and openings, and knowings.

Yes, you all continue to walk this Path together and it shall lead you, my beloveds, on many wonderful journeys both within and in this world, for now you feel that you are secured with God, that you walk with Him, and He is guiding you, my beloveds, there is no longer that feeling of doubt, of unworthiness. You feel the strength of this Touch within your souls and this strength gives you the confidence to step forward in your lives and to release that part of you that is fearful and confused and filled with doubt.

My beloveds, beautiful souls, continue to be with your Heavenly Father in Love and prayer, asking for His guidance and blessings, His Love. And your eyes slowly open. You have securely shut them for many years out of fear and protection of yourselves, not wishing to be hurt by so many in this world who lack clear understanding, who do not put love before anger and hostility.

My beloveds, you are now strong enough to walk in this world with your head held high, fearing no one, fearing nothing, for there is nothing to fear, there is no one who can hurt you, because God has placed His Hand firmly upon you, my beloveds, and you can now walk securely in His Light and protection. And those who would cast aspersions in your direction, you will see how impotent this is and how misdirected, for those who judge and hurt are themselves in pain and lost. Love those, my beloveds, who direct negativity toward you. Say a prayer for them and say a prayer for yourselves, thanking your Heavenly Father for the Gifts of Truth and Love, Light and Enlightenment that He has given you in your lives. And there are many around you who support you and Love you. This is the result of your Light and their Light, your desire to seek Love and their desire to give Love, your desire to give Love and their desire to receive Love. It is this currency of Love that makes your life filled with wealth, filled with the treasures of the soul.

Continue, my beloveds to love one another, to love all you meet, even those who you may have judged in the past. Be a channel of Love for them. Do not listen to their words that originate from that place of hurt, but see them as God's children, precious souls, who have not yet realized how loved they are. Judgment only brings greater negativity. It closes the door to Love. Accept and allow yourselves to direct Love towards others brings greater blessings to you, my beloveds, for you are blessed and they are blessed and all are given greater Light.

Yes, the world is a harsh place. Many wander this world, lost, afraid, ignorant, unable to love. You do not wish to join them, but you wish to change these conditions in the world as God's channels of Love. This is why we stand by your side, my beloveds. This is why God blesses you so, for he wishes for you to be that channel of Love in the world, to be clear and strong with a deep sense of how beautiful every soul is despite what covers that soul. There is beauty within, there is beauty and a wonderful potential which can be ignited by God's Love, by the natural love. It is a matter of time, and progression, effort, and prayer, learning and seeking. Every soul must do this and seek this, this Light and you precious souls have sought and found a great treasure of Light.

Continue, my beloveds, and know that you are truly loved and guided and well upon the road of deep and eternal healing and transformation. Blessed are those who walk this Path with humility and Grace in loving acceptance of all. You are blessed, my beloveds. Walk in this way and nothing negative or hurtful will touch you with this attitude of Love, gracious Love, and earnest gratitude, and self-love, and love for all. God bless you, my beloveds. I am Andrew and I am with you always upon your journeys. It is my Gift from God to be with you. God bless you and may He keep you always in this wonderful Light. God bless you.
