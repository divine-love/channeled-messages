# The Breath of God

**Spirit:** [[Spirits/isaiah|Isaiah]] · **Medium:** Al Fike · **Date:** 2015-05-18

> [!quote] The Door
> This is the breath of God, the Will of God, and all is done with Love

Isaiah speaks of the breath of God as a sweeping wind of change that carries souls to new realms of understanding, soul awareness, and knowing of the Heavenly Father. He calls the circle not to resist these changes but to embrace them as a gift, and blesses them with the grace of God's continuing breath of Light.

## Questions this message answers

- What does it mean to walk in the grace of God?
- How do I embrace change instead of resisting it?
- What is the breath of God?
- How does God's will sweep through a life and change everything?
- How do I open myself to new realms of spiritual understanding?

I am Isaiah. Bless you and may God continue to embrace you and breathe this new Light within. God bless you.
