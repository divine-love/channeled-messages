# You Cannot See the Events Coming but Have Faith That God Will Use You

**Spirit:** [[Spirits/augustine|Augustine]] · **Medium:** Al Fike · **Date:** 2015-11-26

> [!quote] The Door
> God withholds the future not to hide it, but to keep your choices free.

Augustine explains why celestial spirits do not reveal the full scope of what lies ahead, teaching that foreknowledge would distort rather than assist the free flow of human choices in harmony with God's Plan. He calls the group to stay grounded in daily prayer, love, and patience, trusting that God carries them and will reveal what is needed at the right moment.

## Questions this message answers

- Why don't spirits tell us everything that is coming in the future?
- How does knowing too much about the future actually harm our spiritual growth?
- How do I trust God's plan when I cannot see what is coming?
- What is my responsibility when I cannot see the bigger picture?
- How do I stay focused on today when I am anxious about the future?
- How does prayer keep me aligned with God's Will even when I don't understand what is unfolding?
- Does God really hold us in the palm of His hand?

---

God bless you. When the time comes you will know what to do, my children. You cannot see the events forthcoming, but we are able to see what is indeed coming over the horizon and it is not helpful for us to give you all of this information because it would influence your thinking and decisions in ways that are not in harmony with the flow of God's Plan and Will for you. For with your limited perceptions you are not able to make those choices which would be of the most benefit and assistance towards the goals of bringing greater Light and Truth to mankind.

In time you will gain greater perception and wisdom as the Love ignites these beautiful gifts within you. And for the moment you are reliant upon God's guidance and our support to continue on upon this journey to create Centres and Circles of Light in the world. You must be patient and vigilant, humble and utilize your Gifts and faculties to focus on what it is you must do with each day that passes. Prayer is the foundation, Love is what flows through all and wisdom will allow you to speak and act in harmony.

Continue to walk your Path, eager to learn and to grow within your souls and be with God always. Be with God always and all things shall come, all events will unfold, all knowledge and Gifts will be given within the flow of God's Love. And as you journey, being aware, acknowledging and embracing the true desires of your soul, for God has you within the palm of His Hands, He carries you, He nurtures you and He teaches you. What more could you ask, my dear beloved souls, than these great blessings within your lives? For in this all is possible and great works and miracles, blessings and Truth shall manifest within you and all about you as you grow in the Love.

We are with you, we love you and we shall continue to guide you upon your journey giving to you what you require so that you may make your steps forward. God bless you, beloveds, your teacher Augustine loves you, loves you dearly, beloved souls.
