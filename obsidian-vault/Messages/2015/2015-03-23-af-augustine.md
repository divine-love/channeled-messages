# They Will Fill This Building

**Spirit:** [[Spirits/augustine|Augustine]] · **Medium:** Al Fike · **Date:** 2015-03-23

> [!quote] The Door
> They will fill this building and the hosannas will rise to God. Guidance will be given concerning this place when the time is right

Augustine delivers a brief prophetic blessing in New York, promising that the building where the circle is gathered will one day be filled with seekers raising hosannas to God. He assures those present that guidance will come concerning this place when the time is right, and encourages them to continue in their efforts toward the fulfillment of God's Will and purpose.

## Questions this message answers

- Can God really fill a place with seekers through His plan?
- How do I trust a prophetic promise when I cannot see how it will happen?
- What does it mean to work toward the fulfillment of God's Will?
