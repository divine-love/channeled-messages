# King Solomon Talks about How to Obtain True Wisdom

**Spirit:** [[Spirits/solomon|Solomon]] · **Medium:** Al Fike · **Date:** 2015-11-23

> [!quote] The Door
> The wisest man who ever lived has come to tell you his wisdom was wrong.

Solomon, now a Celestial Angel, reflects on the difference between the intellectual wisdom he was famous for in his Earth life and the true wisdom that comes only from a soul ignited by God's Love. He teaches that even those new to the Divine Love path have access to a deeper knowing than any purely intellectual understanding can provide.

## Questions this message answers

- What is the difference between intellectual wisdom and true spiritual wisdom?
- How does Divine Love give access to a deeper understanding of truth?
- Can a spiritual beginner access genuine wisdom?
- What did King Solomon learn after death that he didn't know in his Earth life?
- How does the soul perceive truth in ways the mind cannot?
- What does it mean for wisdom to grow as the soul expands in love?
- Is there a form of knowledge beyond what the intellect can reach?
- How does God guide us toward truth through His Love?

I was known as Solomon the wise and I have come to speak to you about wisdom.

The wisdom that I possessed when I was on Earth is certainly not the wisdom I possess now. For what became myth and legend of the past was mostly the understandings of the mind, using my own faculties and understanding at the time. That was several thousand years ago, my beloveds. I have since progressed in the land of spirit and I have become a Celestial Angel through the redemption of God's Love within my soul and I have come to know what True Wisdom is. It comes from the faculties of the soul ignited by God's Love. It comes as an understanding so deep and pure and powerful that no intellectual knowledge can approach this. It is the fruits of redemption and rebirth within. Seek to be wise in this way.

I know that you are young and just beginning upon this Path but even you have access to greater wisdom than is available to those who use their minds to discern the Truth. For not many can truly discern Truth with their intellect. Yes, they may have a measure of understanding of the world and the mechanics of the world, but the world and all of creation, the universe has many layers and complexities and beauty. It is unfathomable to the mind of man, yet with a soul open and thriving in the Touch of God's Love many things can be seen, much wisdom can be obtained and great Truth, great Truth which starts as a seed within your consciousness and grows as your soul expands in Love.

Continue, my beloved, beautiful souls, to seek the Truth and to know the wisdom that lies within you. There is much to know, to uncover, to discover, to lay bare into your conscious self and to embrace with faith and with acceptance of the mind. Yes, it is a beautiful journey and God will guide you each step of the way and fill you with Truth which comes with His Love, and that spirit of Truth will dwell within you guiding you towards that which is truly of God's creation, and you will have a full and rich understanding of this beautiful creation which is of God.

Beloved souls, many Angels uphold you and teach you and direct you towards Truth. Be open and willing to take this journey, to walk in the Light of Truth, to seek to know and to test and to evolve as this journey carries you forward in loving understanding, in God's blessed awareness in Love.

Beloved souls, thank you for allowing me to speak and to share with you my wisdom. There is so much, my beloveds, so much to know, to truly understand. May you continue on this journey and we who love you and are with you and attempt to guide and inspire you will walk with you always in Love expressing God's great care for you, and accompany you on this great journey of discovery. For we love you and God loves you, and the world awaits your eyes to open, your souls to know, your minds to accept and your willingness to share the Truths that you know. May you always walk in the Light, my beloveds, and know what Truth is and from that, the great blessing of wisdom. God bless you, beloveds. I am Solomon and I love you. God bless you.
