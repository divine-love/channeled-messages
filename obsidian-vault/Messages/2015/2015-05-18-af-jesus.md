# The Light Within You Will Change the World

**Spirit:** [[Spirits/jesus|Jesus]] · **Medium:** Al Fike · **Date:** 2015-05-18

> [!quote] The Door
> You bring Truth. It will change the world. It will change the world, beloveds

Jesus blesses the circle, affirming that the Truth shining within their souls will change the world, that he walks with them always, and that God will provide all they need to fulfil their calling as channels of Love.

## Questions this message answers

- How does the Light within me change the world?
- What does it mean to be a disciple of Jesus today?
- How do I remember who I truly am and why I am here?
- Does Jesus walk with people today?

I come. It is Jesus. I come to bless each one of you, my beloveds. In your efforts to bring Light into this world, in your efforts to bring Light within yourselves and all those who come into your orbit. I come to bless you, my beloveds, for you do know the truth within you and that Truth shines within your souls, a beautiful Light. And I am with you always, beloveds. Continue to make your efforts. Continue to work together in harmony and mutual support, and we in spirit will surround each one of you and walk with you as you make your way to the Light.

Beloveds, beautiful, beautiful souls, remember who you are and what it is you came to this world to do. Continue to walk this path which you have chosen and to be that channel of love which God has placed within you and all about you. You are my disciples. With each moment that you are here in this world you bring Truth through the Light of your souls, that Love within you. You bring Truth. It will change the world. It will change the world, beloveds. And God will carry you, God will bring to you all that you require to walk this Path and be His channels of Love in this world. Blessings to you, beautiful souls. I love you, my brothers, my sisters I love you. God bless you. Be with God. God bless you.
