# You Are Already Channels of God's Love

**Spirit:** [[Spirits/andrew|Andrew]] · **Medium:** Al Fike · **Date:** 2015-07-25

> [!quote] The Door
> You are already a channel of God's Love; the only thing standing between you and knowing it is the mind's need to see proof.

Andrew teaches that souls who pray and seek God are already channels of His Love, even when they cannot perceive it. He urges the gathering to release mental expectations and ego-driven desires for recognition, and instead walk humbly in love, trusting that God uses them in ways beyond what their minds can see.

## Questions this message answers

- How do I know if I am really serving God or just serving my ego?
- Why can't I see the effect of my prayers and service?
- How do I let go of the need for recognition in my spiritual work?
- What does it mean to walk in God's light with humility?
- Am I already a channel of God's Love without knowing it?
- What does the Bible mean by the peace that passes all understanding?
- What did Jesus mean when he said blessed are the peacemakers?
- How do I release old hurts and patterns that hold me back spiritually?
- How do I stop judging myself for not doing enough for God?
- What does a true spiritual awakening feel like?

---

It is Andrew and I have come to acknowledge this instrument's day of his birth and to say that I love him dearly. He has been my charge for many, many years. And soon comes the time for this instrument to fulfill his purpose, to come to that place of clear understanding of who he truly is as a channel of God's Love in this world. Yes, he has been this channel of Love for many years, yet he does not see this clearly or acknowledge this to any great degree. And so it is for channels in this world who wish to do God's bidding, who bring Light. They often do not see the effects that are produced through their channels of Love, their instrumentality to the world. They often cannot see with their eyes and perceive with their emotions and understand with their minds what it is that their souls are so active in being a channel of love, being God's instrument of Love.

So often, my children, you go about your business expecting God to guide you in overt ways for you to do specific things, things which would gratify your mind and expectations which would make you feel good and happy and fulfilled. And yet if you could see with the eyes of your souls, if you had this clarity and the veil is lifted and your minds quelled by your souls consciousness you would see that God uses you in many different ways. With each day that passes you are being used as His channels of Light in the world. Each one of you has this lesson to learn, my beloveds, to learn the ways of God and the ways of the soul and to put aside those mental expectations, and insecurities, and perceptions, and projections which put forth a desire to do things in this world that are material in nature and receive emotional gratification. This, my beloveds, is error. You are required to be humble and patient and open and connected with your Heavenly Father. And with this deep connection and with His Love pouring into your soul, you are His channels of Love in the world and you are in the Light. Walk in the Light, my beloveds, and do not condemn yourselves or judge yourselves or falsely put forth expectations that are not in alignment with God's Will and God's laws of Love.

Love yourselves, my beloveds, be with God and live your lives as simply, and as gracefully, as humbly, and as effectively as you can, for in this you will find your true purpose and the expressions of your soul will blossom forth in clear and beautiful ways. Do not seek gratification. Do not seek recognition. Seek to Love every soul you are in contact with. Seek to love so in simple and beautiful ways. Do not allow your ego to trip you and to trick you.

And as the Love accumulates within your soul, this wisdom, this understanding will unfold into your consciousness and you will feel a deep gratification in this, in this understanding of who you truly are and in your relationship with your Heavenly Father. You will walk in Grace and Light and you will become a powerful channel for God's Love to work in this world. Yes, it is the simple acts and the loving gestures. It is setting aside your emotions, your judgments, the barriers, and the error in favor of Love. Love is the most powerful thing in the universe. It will change all elements that are not in harmony with God's laws to harmony and balance. So if you pray for the world, my beloveds, pray for God's Love to infuse the world, pray for God's Love to infuse yourself, and pray for God's Love to heal this beautiful world that is in such need of healing and balance.

My beloveds, each of you seeks to be a channel of Love — accept that you are. Accept the beauty which is within you, and release all that which is not a part of this Love and beauty and grace, all those conditions and hurts and pains and their erroneous thinking and habits which need to fall away. Let them go. Free yourself and be at peace and know the peace that passes all understanding for it is available to every child in this world; it is available to all. For those who seek they shall find it and for those who are lost you will assist them in finding this great Peace and Love.

And so I say again, my beloved son, you will come to that threshold very soon, and the universe will open to your eyes, and God's touch will be more real than ever, and we in spirit will be with you as your conscious birth into the true and beautiful place of God's reality. It is coming. Your eyes will open and for each of you, my children, it is possible if you long for this and pray for this you will come to this great awakening, this opening and this deep and profound understanding of God's true creation, and Love, and beauty. Blessed are those who seek truth. Blessed are the peacemakers. Blessed are the beloved children of God. Walk in His Light and yearn for His Love and you will know, you will know of these words that I speak and these promises that I make. It shall come to each one of you in the fullness of your soul's growth in the Father's Love.

God bless you, beloveds, I am Andrew and I love you all dearly and I am with you often. Continue to pray to seek the ways of Love, to be God's channels of Love and all will unfold in the fullness of time in the grace of God's Will. God bless you, beloveds.
