# Cherish This Light

**Spirit:** [[Spirits/augustine|Augustine]] · **Medium:** Al Fike · **Date:** 2015-10-05

> [!quote] The Door
> The Light within you is precious. Treat it accordingly

Augustine blesses the gathered souls and urges them to take the Gift of God's Love into their lives, cherish it, protect it, and feed it through prayer so it may grow into a great flame guiding everything they do. He calls them to reach out to God in prayer and open themselves to a full and rich relationship with their Heavenly Father.

## Questions this message answers

- How do I protect and nurture the spiritual light within me?
- How do I keep the flame of God's Love burning within my soul?
- How do I build a rich and full relationship with God?
- How do I pray to receive more of God's Love?
- Does God wait for me to ask before giving His Love?

---

You have each received the blessing of the Father's Love within your souls. Take this Gift into your lives. Cherish this Light. Protect it and feed it through your prayers for more of this Love that may be a great flame burning within you, my beloveds, guiding all that you do, informing all that you are. Beloved children, know that God awaits your prayers beseeching Him to receive this Gift. He awaits your prayers. Reach out to Him, open yourselves to His Love that you may come to know God in a full and rich relationship filled with Love and Grace, joy and peace. Thank you, my beloveds. Bless you. Bless you on your journeys. Your teacher Augustine loves you. God bless you. God bless you.
