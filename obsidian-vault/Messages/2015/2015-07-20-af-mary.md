# Pray for the Children

**Spirit:** [[Spirits/mary|Mary, Mother of Jesus]] · **Medium:** Al Fike · **Date:** 2015-07-20

> [!quote] The Door
> The children of the world carry the seeds of the next generation of Light

Mary brings a blessing of Love to the gathered souls and all their children, urging them to pray that the next generation will have their eyes and ears opened to God's Love. She calls for prayer that children will be protected from evil, guided along their paths, and that the world may find harmony, peace, and wisdom through souls filled with the Father's Love.

## Questions this message answers

- How do I pray for my children spiritually?
- How do I protect my children from spiritual harm?
- How do I pray for the next generation?
- What does Mary say about children and the future?
- How can prayer help my children find God?
- How do I pray for the world my children will inherit?
- How do I help my children hear God's voice?
- What is the spiritual future of humanity's children?
- How do I pray for peace in the world for my grandchildren?

---

I bring a blessing of Love to you and to all of your children and their children, all the children that are connected to each one of you in this world, a blessing to be given, a blessing of joy and Love. For it is in the children that your future lies, who they are, how they will be in your world and what they do with their lives. Pray for your children, my beloveds, for they hold the future. Pray that God will put within them the desire for His Love. Pray that their eyes will be opened from this deep sleep that humanity has been in for so many long centuries, not willing to look within themselves to find the solutions which God has to give within their souls.

Pray that their ears will be opened that they may hear the quiet voice of God to guide them along their path, to inspire them to greater and higher good. Pray that they will be protected from the forces of evil that they may do good in the world and walk in Light. And pray for this world so burdened by the conditions that humanity has brought to it, that these conditions may dissipate, that there be a brighter day ahead, a day of peace, a day of Love. That all children and all parents and all peoples may live in harmony and peace with one another acknowledging the unique creation which God has made for each soul who inhabits this place. May there be Love. May there be acceptance and may there be wisdom that comes from the soul filled with the Father's Love. God bless you, my beloveds. God bless you and bless your children and their children and all the children in this world. And I love you, beloved beautiful souls, I love you. God bless you and goodnight.
