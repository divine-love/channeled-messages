# Some Comments on What the Angels Are Doing to Facilitate Our Soul Growth

**Spirit:** [[Spirits/seretta-kem|Seretta Kem]] · **Medium:** Al Fike · **Date:** 2015-08-19

> [!quote] The Door
> While you pray, angels are performing intricate work within and between your souls that you cannot see but will one day fully understand.

Seretta Kem reveals the intricate spiritual work performed by angels during prayer gatherings, including chemical transferences between souls that create deep bonds and open pathways for soul-to-soul communication. He urges the group to redouble their prayers for Divine Love as the key to unlocking the magnificent abilities that lie dormant within each soul.

## Questions this message answers

- What do angels actually do during a prayer circle?
- Can souls communicate with each other across distance?
- What happens spiritually when souls gather to pray together?
- How does God's Love unlock the dormant abilities within the soul?
- What invisible bonds are created between souls who pray together?
- How does the soul communicate in ways the mind cannot perceive?
- What gifts might be lying dormant within my soul right now?

---

I am Seretta Kem and I am from Egypt. I lived in this world approximately 4000 years ago. I lived at the time when Egypt was a great nation and we had a beautiful culture, and much is not truly known of how we lived and how we thought, for we did indeed revere the Creator and now I revere the Creator in a different way, through my soul. I love my God who truly loves me and loves all of you deeply, endlessly and completely.

We are performing many tasks while you are present together and I am in charge of performing intricate chemical transferences from one to the other which would be difficult for me to explain to you, my beloveds, but I wish you to know that you will become a part of one another in many different ways as you pray together in this way. This facilitates a deeper bond between you. It helps you to communicate with one another when you are not together, for much happens in this realm of communication of the soul that you are not aware of. We open the avenues that create the possibilities for you who live in this world to have a powerful bond that is of a spiritual nature, that is soul-to-soul and soul awareness to soul awareness. And in this we may use you in different combinations to accomplish things in this world as you pray to be used as a channel of God's Love and an instrument of His peace and change in the world.

He may use each of you in a multiplicity of ways so that you may support one another in these efforts, so that you may not walk alone but walk with many, not only in spirit but in this world as well. This bonding, this reinforcement of your being, in Love and in many intricate ways will help to facilitate the opening of your gifts and of your abilities to be God's instruments in this world.

So, my friends, there are many reasons for you to come together, there are many powerful reasons and outcomes from your efforts. Yes, much of this is very subtle and difficult to perceive and almost impossible to understand and perceive through the material senses and mind. But I tell you this is true, this is real and it is powerful. And as you develop and grow within your souls and the faculties of your souls open your eyes, your ears, your ability to comprehend by your soul faculties, you will understand, you will see, you will know what it is that I speak of.

But you must, my beloved beautiful souls, you must make that effort to develop your souls through praying for the Father's Love. This is the key to everything that can manifest and blossom within you and all about you and through you. Without God's Love none of this is truly known. Make that effort, pray and redouble your efforts to pray and long for these gifts that will be a part of you for all eternity. The soul is a wonderful thing. It is imbued with many magnificent abilities. Do not ignore yourselves, my beloveds, but make that effort to discover this aspect of your being, so powerful, yet unknown, so beautiful yet often ignored, so necessary yet you do not see your own beauty, your own power, your own abilities. Seek the Truth within and know that we who walk with you have come to help you, to assist you in many ways, and to make your journey a little easier. This will make the opening and the flourishing and development of your souls a little quicker, a little more accessible and certainly we are pleased and happy when you allow this and desire this.

God bless you, beautiful souls imbued with magnificent gifts and capable of great works for your Heavenly Father who eagerly awaits your awakening. God bless you, your friend and servant, Seretta Kem is with you. God bless you.
