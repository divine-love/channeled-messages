# Walk in the World as Loving Channels to One Another

**Spirit:** [[Spirits/alec-gaunt|Alec Gaunt]] · **Medium:** Al Fike · **Date:** 2015-11-28

> [!quote] The Door
> What if every soul acknowledged every other in their beauty? That is what the world is meant to be.

Alec Gaunt adds his encouragement to the group, affirming that surprising turns of events await those who continue riding the wave of God's Love and pursuing His work in the world. He holds up a vision of what the world would be if all people could trust, give, and share with one another in love, and calls each soul to walk in the world as a loving channel acknowledging the beauty and uniqueness of every person they meet.

## Questions this message answers

- What would the world look like if everyone walked in love and trust with one another?
- How do I acknowledge the beauty and uniqueness of every soul I meet?
- How do I stay encouraged when I cannot see the results of my spiritual efforts?
- What does it mean to share my gifts and light with others in everyday life?
- How do spirit friends see and respond to our efforts on Earth?

---

... and I too wish to add my love and my appreciation for your efforts, beautiful souls, and I want you to know that many surprising turn of events will take place in your lives as you continue on as you do in pursuing God's Love, in exploring the possibilities of being His channel of Love in the world. Yes, you are riding a wonderful wave of Light and Love and it will bring you to many beautiful souls and wonderful places and great circles of Love and Light in your world. I merely wish to encourage you to carry on in your prayers and efforts, in your desire to connect with one another and to share with one another in loving ways. For how different the world would be if all the peoples of the world were able to do this with one another - trusting in love, giving in love, sharing your thoughts, your efforts, your desires, your Light, your gifts, the beauty of your souls. This is what is meant to be, my beautiful children, to walk in the world in this way, to acknowledge every soul in their beauty and their uniqueness, to truly be a channel of Love in the world.

Beloved souls, carry on in your efforts. Yes, there are many who support you and work with you and work through you to accomplish this. The work continues and I applaud your efforts and I see the beauty of your Light and my heart is warmed at what you have accomplished and I see so much awaits you in the future. Be of good cheer and know that you are firmly within the Hands of God and He will guide you, He will guide you. God bless you, your friend Alec has great love for you. God bless you.
