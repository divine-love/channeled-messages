# Your Purpose in This Gathering Is to Teach the Prayer Practice

**Spirit:** [[Spirits/john-the-beloved|John the Beloved]] · **Medium:** Al Fike · **Date:** 2015-03-23

> [!quote] The Door
> Your purpose in this gathering is to teach the prayer practice. This is but a small portion of what is to come

John the Beloved blesses those gathered in New York, affirming that their purpose in this gathering is to teach the prayer practice — how to pray, how to focus, how to reach out for the Love of God. He encourages them to share their experiences with God as testimony, promises that what they are building now is a small portion of what is to come, and assures them that each step of the gradual awakening is swift in the measure of eternity.

## Questions this message answers

- How do I teach someone else how to pray?
- What is the right way to pray to receive God's Love?
- How do I share my spiritual experiences without sounding strange or preachy?
- How do I walk in faith when I cannot see what is coming?
- Why does spiritual growth feel so slow?
- Is my small spiritual effort really contributing to something larger?
- What does it mean that gradual awakening is swift in the measure of eternity?

And a blessing upon you, my beloveds, for your efforts to bring light into this place, to draw the lost souls together so that they may be nurtured and given shelter and love and comfort. Blessings to you, my beloveds, for your prayers for them and your prayers for yourselves and for this world. Yes indeed this world is in need of prayer and every soul upon it may benefit from this. And you will teach the prayer, how to pray, how to focus, how to reach out for the love of God, and this, my beloveds, is your purpose in this gathering. And in between those times of prayer you may talk about your experiences with God and the growing of your souls and the opening of your consciousness to the laws of God, to the laws of love, and be a testimony towards these truths.

Bring love through, my beloveds, and as you prepare this place you bring greater love, greater light and more possibilities for what is to come. Indeed, you have all had your visions as to what is to come in your lives, in the places where you reside, and the places you desire to travel to; and that which is within the flow of God's Love will manifest in harmony and doors will open that will make possible your efforts and gifts and purpose in the world.

Indeed, it is not easy to walk in faith, to walk trusting that each day your Heavenly Father will put your footsteps where they are meant to be. But as you continue to grow in the love and grow your faith and open your eyes and learn the truths which your Heavenly Father blesses you with and allows you to see, then you will understand why each day is a gift, why each footstep is necessary and is followed by another and another. This gradual awakening, this gradual enlightenment is the sure road to at-onement with God, and may feel at times tedious or slow, but you grow with each step, each breath, and that awakening, my beloveds, is swift in terms of the measure of eternity - it is very swift. And indeed the pace quickens and you all feel yourselves on the verge of something, and you are, and there will be much unfolding and opening and developing within your lives, my beloveds. The small and brief connection that you have together at this time and these efforts to bring the lost souls into prayer and light, this is but a small portion of what is to come. And as you grow and are prepared and ready, the work which will be laid before you will be mighty and beautiful.

Blessings to you, my beloveds. I am John and I will be with you in your efforts to pray with the beautiful souls who are coming and you will learn through this experience and there will be an opening for you, my beloveds, as for them, a learning, a growing and you will come closer the way you are meant to do in this world as you see these efforts unfold and evolve and open to the will of God.

Continue to pray together, to walk together as brothers and sisters in the flow of God's Love and God will lead you to many beautiful places within yourselves and within this world. Feel the joy of this, my beloveds, feel the anticipation, the eagerness, the desires of your souls, to bring light and change and healing to this world, for in this joy and this enactment of love you will find your own salvation and the release of all that which is not in harmony with God's Love. It will come and it will flow in great abundance through you and around you to many, many who await your service, the flow of your love, God's Love - many await. Walk in love and light and know that God guides you and brings you to that place of true knowing, of sure progress on the path Divine. God bless you and I am with you in your efforts, I am John. God bless you.
