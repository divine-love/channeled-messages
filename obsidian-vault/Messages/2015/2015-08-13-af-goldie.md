# Feel the Warmth of God's Love

**Spirit:** [[Spirits/goldie|Goldie]] · **Medium:** Al Fike · **Date:** 2015-08-13

> [!quote] The Door
> The warmth you feel on your skin and the warmth you feel in your soul come from the same source.

Goldie offers a brief warm blessing, connecting the physical warmth of the summer air to the warmth of God's Love and His Breath upon all souls gathered.

## Questions this message answers

- How does God's Love feel in the soul?
- Can we experience God's presence through the natural world around us?
- What does it mean to be embraced by God's warmth?

---

I am Goldie. I love you so. And God has brought the warm, moist air, His breath, upon you, beloved souls. And we are so happy that our friend Geoff feels comfortable and our beloved daughter who lives in such warm climates can feel the warmth. God's breath is Love, it is warm is it not? You are all embraced in His warmth. God bless you. God bless you.
