# Continue Steadfastly on the Path of Love

**Spirit:** [[Spirits/alec-gaunt|Alec Gaunt]] · **Medium:** Al Fike · **Date:** 2015-09-27

> [!quote] The Door
> You cannot see the work being done behind the scenes. But it is being done.

Alec Gaunt offers warm encouragement to the group, affirming they are walking in the right direction and urging them not to doubt their steps. He speaks of working behind the scenes alongside angels in a great celestial orchestra, helping each soul find its place in the healing of the world.

## Questions this message answers

- How do I stay confident on the spiritual path when I cannot see my own progress?
- Do spirit guides and angels really work behind the scenes in our lives?
- How do I know I am heading in the right spiritual direction?
- What does it mean to find my place in God's plan for the healing of the world?
- How does God help us see that we are on the right path?

---

Alec here, wishing to say a few words of encouragement. You are all walking the Path and heading in the directions that are intended, and keeping close to you the Truth of God's Love. Do not doubt this, but continue to be sure of your steps and confirm within you what is the right direction, for God will indeed help you to see that you are heading in the direction intended, towards Light, towards the Truth, towards service, growth, Love.

Carry on, carry on, my friends, and your friend Alec is ever with you, encouraging, guiding, influencing, and working, working behind the scenes, helping in this great orchestra of the dance of the souls and this life, influencing and directing, as are all the angels engaged in this great service for the healing of this world and helping you to find your place within that flow of change and healing. I love you deeply and I am with each one of you often. I love you. God bless you. God bless you.
