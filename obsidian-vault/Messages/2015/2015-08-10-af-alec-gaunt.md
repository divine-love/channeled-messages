# Allow God to Manifest His Love in All That You Do

**Spirit:** [[Spirits/alec-gaunt|Alec Gaunt]] · **Medium:** Al Fike · **Date:** 2015-08-10

> [!quote] The Door
> You have done the preparing; now comes the part where you let go and let God turn your plans into something better than you imagined.

Alec Gaunt blesses a group preparing for a gathering, encouraging them to release anxiety and trust that God and the angels will guide every step. He calls them to embrace joy, laughter, and fellowship, assuring them that a life lived in willing surrender to God's guidance is full of beauty, grace, and fulfilling service.

## Questions this message answers

- How do I stop worrying and trust that God has a plan?
- What does it feel like to truly surrender to God's guidance?
- Can I really enjoy life fully while walking a spiritual path?
- How do I know when God is guiding me versus my own mind?
- Is it possible to feel genuine joy and lightness on the spiritual path?
- How do angels help guide us through life's decisions?
- What does it mean to be fully alive in God's Love?

---

Your friend Alec here. God bless you, my brothers and sisters. So you have made all your preparations, you have had all your conversations, you have decided the course to take. Now it is time to allow God to manifest His Love in all that you do, to trust that with the help of the angels and with your own skills and sure knowledge of the roles that you are to play that all will be well and everything will fall into place. And with this understanding, I want you all to enjoy yourselves, to feel the joy of fellowship, to know the joy that you do all within the Father's Will, and in this there will be Light and Laughter, a joyful reunion, a loving benediction upon all who come together. Embrace your brothers and sisters, dance with your brothers and sisters, smile and laugh, and know that you are all God's children in the Light of His Love and that your future bodes very bright, filled with promise, filled with service, filled with many surprises.

You are being led, guided and surrounded by the angels, taken upon this Path, led by the hand. And each of you has come to a place of willingness to follow, to allow God to lead you forward, to be humble upon your Path, to allow God to set the course and in this I see that you are grateful and joyful and relieved because is there not great anxiety when you feel alone and must make your choices in this way? But when God guides you and leads you, you are never alone and the best and most beautiful course is set. And in your willingness to follow is the journey not full of beauty and grace and wonderment?

Follow the road that God has set before you, beloved souls. Take your steps, enjoy each moment, be fully engaged in life and be fully engaged in your relationship with your Heavenly Father and none can go wrong, no course will be off the chosen path, every experience will be a blessing, every soul you meet will be a gift and all that you do will be fulfilling, and bold, and rich as you serve in Love and walk the Path Divine, beloved beautiful souls.

My brothers and sisters, you have prayed for this and this gift of your life will be full of Light, it is full of Light. Be alive within this beautiful gift and be alive within God's Love. Such blessings for you and for all those you love. Continue to nurture and love one another and follow your guidance. You are on the right course. You will see how things unfold. You will see God's Wisdom, His perfect plan for each one of you. All it takes is your willingness to follow. God bless you, beloved, beloved. God bless you and many angels will be with you as you bring so many together in joyous, wonderful companionship, fellowship and Love. The angels are singing your praises and God is blessing you mightily. God bless you, beloveds, and good night. God bless you.
