# Reaching for Greater Light and Service

**Spirit:** [[Spirits/seretta-kem|Seretta Kem]] · **Medium:** Al Fike · **Date:** 2015-05-04

> [!quote] The Door
> Bless you, beloved souls, who reach eagerly for greater Light and service

Seretta Kem closes an evening of healing and prayer with a tender blessing, affirming that the group's efforts to bare their souls and reach for something higher have been witnessed and celebrated in spirit. He assures them that the next steps lie easily within their grasp and that God's Love will continue to change them and unfold their gifts.

## Questions this message answers

- How do I know my spiritual efforts are being seen and supported?
- How does God's Love continue to change me over time?
- What does it mean to bare my soul in a spiritual community?
- How do I become a willing channel of God's Love?
- What happens in my soul when I reach for something higher?
- How do I trust that the next steps on my path will flow naturally?

God bless you, this is Seretta Kem. You have each received healing this night and received the Love of God within your souls and I wish to say to you, those who were here for the day that we are very pleased with what you have accomplished and that you have bared your souls to one another and made efforts to reach for something higher. And you have great aspirations, my beloved beautiful souls, to be God's willing channels in the world of Love. And as you so desire this and you reach for the highest, you will be those channels of love, you will bring blessings through you to many, and there will be many opportunities to walk in the world in Light, in service, in Love.

Be at peace my beautiful souls. Be at peace and know that the next steps are easily within your grasp and will flow beautifully in Light, in goodness, in harmony with God's Will. Bless you, beloved souls, who reach eagerly for greater Light and service. We are always with you in these efforts and you will continue to walk upon the Divine Path, and the Love within you will grow, and the shifting consciousness, awareness of your Heavenly Father and your soul's desires, and your gifts, and the beauty of your beings will continue to progress forward as the Father's Love enters your souls and changes you forever. God bless you, my beloveds, God bless you and good night.
