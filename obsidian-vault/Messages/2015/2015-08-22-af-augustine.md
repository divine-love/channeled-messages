# Augustine Advises a Retreat Group to Talk about Their Inner Soul Changes

**Spirit:** [[Spirits/augustine|Augustine]] · **Medium:** Al Fike · **Date:** 2015-08-22

> [!quote] The Door
> Sharing your inner changes with others is itself an act of Love and courage

Augustine encourages the retreat group to share with one another what shifts and changes they have experienced within themselves during the gathering. He urges them to bring these inner awarenesses out into the open, examine them consciously, and work together toward the goal of integrating the changes God has brought within them.

## Questions this message answers

- How do I recognize when God has changed me spiritually?
- How do I share my spiritual experiences with others?
- Why is it important to talk about inner soul changes with others?
- How do I integrate spiritual transformation into my daily life?
- How do I know if I have grown spiritually during a retreat?
- How does talking about inner changes help me embrace them?
- How do I support others in their spiritual transformation?

It is your teacher Augustine here. God bless you, beloved students, beautiful souls, children of our Creator. God has touched you deeply this day and has touched you deeply as you have come together in these past days. And we wish to talk to you about the shifts and changes that will be coming within your consciousness and in your beings and your lives. We will do so in the coming prayer on this day and I wish for you, my beloveds, to share with one another what you believe has happened within you. Is there something different and what might that be? Are you different and how is that within you? Do you feel closer to God? Do you feel more in harmony with His Love? And how will you express this when you leave for your homes and your lives in this world.

We wish for you to think about these questions and discuss amongst you what this is, how this is and possibly why this is. You need, my beloveds, you need desperately to bring these things, this awareness, out in the open, so that you may examine the changes within and embrace them in a conscious way. Work towards this goal together. Work towards this goal with each day, for God wants you to make those changes that will bring greater harmony to you, and within you. God bless you, beloved souls, God bless you. Remain together, strengthen your fellowship, support one another and walk in the Love. God bless you.
