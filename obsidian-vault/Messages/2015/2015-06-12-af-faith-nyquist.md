# Do Not Lose Faith in God's Plan

**Spirit:** [[Spirits/faith-nyquist|Faith]] · **Medium:** Al Fike · **Date:** 2015-06-12

> [!quote] The Door
> Even through the maze and forest of these human conditions, God will work and bring His Will and purpose

Faith speaks comfort and encouragement to a soul who has encountered an unexpected obstacle in pursuing God's Will, assuring them that despite the poor stewardship of those in positions of authority, God's plan will not be thwarted. She calls them not to judge or condemn those who are misguided, but to pray for them with compassion and love, and to hold firm in the knowledge that God's Will and purpose will find a way through even the densest of human conditions.

## Questions this message answers

- How do I keep faith in God's plan when I hit a roadblock?
- What do I do when those in spiritual authority seem unworthy of their role?
- How do I have compassion for people who block God's work?
- Does God's Will prevail even when people stand in the way?
- How do I avoid judging others who seem to be failing in their purpose?
- What does it mean to trust God's plan even when outcomes are disappointing?
- How do I pray for those whose choices sadden or frustrate me?
- Can God work His purpose through a situation that seems blocked or hopeless?
- What does the Bible mean when it says God's ways are not our ways?
- How do I stay at peace when I cannot control the outcome of something important to me?

So I see you have hit another roadblock and you are surprised with what has transpired, but I tell you, you will be surprised at the outcomes of what has transpired. Do not give up hope or your desire to be a channel of Light in that place. For God's Will is and will manifest there. And it is true that the present minister has very little to give towards this outcome and this Light. Of all that have gone before him, he is the least of them and it saddens us in spirit to see him supported as he is in a hollow ministry, with no true purpose, without God's guidance and touch. And yet here before you sits a man with great desire to do God's Will and be a channel of His Love in this place.

Yes it saddens us to see the choices made, but do not give up faith and hope in the Will of God. And outcomes and possibilities still exist for this true purpose of this instrument to manifest as is in God's Will and Plan. Do not underestimate the Will of God or the power of His Love to make change in this world, to bring Love into this world.

Yes many are misguided and their intentions are not pure. Yet even through the maze and forest of these human conditions God will work and bring His Will and purpose. Continue to pray for those involved. Continue to be loving and at peace. Do not judge and condemn, but have compassion and Love. And there will be an answer, there will be an answer. I am Faith and I love you, beloveds, and I care for you all and I pray for you all. You are loved, deeply loved. God bless you, beloveds.
