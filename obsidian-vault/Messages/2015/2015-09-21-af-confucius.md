# Continue to Seek the Truth and Express the Gifts of Your Soul

**Spirit:** [[Spirits/confucius|Confucius]] · **Medium:** Al Fike · **Date:** 2015-09-21

> [!quote] The Door
> Every question your soul asks is already drawing the angels closer; keep asking and God will keep opening doors.

Confucius addresses a prayer circle, offering personal encouragement to a new soul whose curiosity springs from her soul, affirming her healing gift. He then broadens his message to all gathered, urging them to continue seeking Truth through prayer, reading inspiring passages, and walking in the flow of God's Love, assuring them that this pursuit brings all they require.

## Questions this message answers

- How do I know if my curiosity about spiritual things comes from my soul?
- Does God really answer the questions I bring to Him in prayer?
- How do I develop a healing gift?
- Why do spiritual questions draw angels closer to us?
- How do I walk in the flow of God's Love in daily life?
- What does it mean to purveyor Light in the world?
- How do I find fulfillment when the world is so full of distractions?

...beautiful circle, my beloved, beautiful soul. Feel the Love here, my beloved. Know that God will guide you further and that spark that you feel deep within you shall grow to a mighty flame, my daughter, a mighty flame. Come to know God and His Love for you, my beloved, and this shall open the doors to greater life and deeper purpose in this world. Beloved daughter, God is with you and touches your soul and brings His Love to you, bringing great Light. You are beautiful and truly loved, precious soul, truly loved. And your deep curiosity, my daughter, springs from your soul. Beloved [name-removed], continue to question and to pray and to seek your answers, for God does not hold back the Truth. He opens doors for you to understand the Truth of His creation, the intricacies of life, the power of His Love. It comes as you yearn from your soul and it comes as you forge your bond with your Creator in Love, in Love, always in Love, beloved soul, God bless you. I am Confucius and I guide you, beloved soul, upon your Path, God bless you. Yes, the Gift of healing, continue in this way and I will support you in your healing Gift, beloved soul. God bless you.

Yes, my beloveds, continue to seek the Truth. You have many questions. Continue to bring your questions to the Heavenly Father, to seek Truth, my beloveds. For as you think of these spiritual things you draw the angels to you, my beloveds. Read the passages that inspire your soul, speak the words of Love to all you meet. Walk in the flow of God's Love. It is simple, my beloveds. Though there be many distractions in this world, none compare in the fulfillment you will find with the search for God and His Love. It is your saving Grace, my beloveds; it brings you all that you require, all that you require. God bless you, beloved and precious souls, seekers of Truth, purveyors of Light, souls who desire Love above all else. You will find what you seek, give what you carry, express your deepened soul's gifts in this world. God bless you.
