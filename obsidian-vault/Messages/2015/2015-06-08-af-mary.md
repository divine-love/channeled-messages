# As You Think of Your Loved Ones in Your Prayers, They Will Be Blessed

**Spirit:** [[Spirits/mary|Mary, Mother of Jesus]] · **Medium:** Al Fike · **Date:** 2015-06-08

> [!quote] The Door
> Prayer for those you love reaches them even when your words cannot

Mary assures the group that their prayers for loved ones are being answered, and that holding dear souls in mind during prayer creates a connection through which spirit can work. She offers a personal assurance to one present that her mother is at peace and surrounded by angels.

## Questions this message answers

- Can my prayers help my loved ones?
- How does praying for others affect them spiritually?
- Can spirits reach my loved ones when I pray for them?
- How do angels respond to our prayers for others?
- Is my mother at peace after passing?
- How do I pray for someone who is dying?
- Does God hear my prayers for my family?

---

The angels have been answering your prayers, God has been answering your prayers, my beloved souls, and as you hold these dear and precious souls within your minds you make it possible for we in spirit to make the connections with these precious souls. Continue in your prayers and your efforts for your loved ones for this world, for all of those in need of your attentions and God's blessings, continue, my beloveds. And my daughter, your beloved mother is at peace. You are not to worry, for the angels surround her at this moment and she will walk in Light. She will walk in Light, beloveds.

God bless you. I am Mary and I love you. Precious, beautiful souls, who seek the Light, may you always find the Light. Wherever you go and whatever you do in your world be in the Light beloveds, be in God's Light, be a channel of His love. Precious and beloved souls, God bless you. I am Mary and I bid you good night.
