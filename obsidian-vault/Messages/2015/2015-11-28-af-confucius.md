# Prayer to Bring Freedom and Joy

**Spirit:** [[Spirits/confucius|Confucius]] · **Medium:** Al Fike · **Date:** 2015-11-28

> [!quote] The Door
> Prayer is not an obligation. It is the most direct path to freedom and joy

Confucius offers a beautiful prayer to the Heavenly Father on behalf of those gathered, asking that their burdens be lifted, that they be freed from the shackles of earthly conditions, and that they be uplifted into glorious Light and the joy of God's presence within.

## Questions this message answers

- How do I pray to be freed from my burdens?
- How do I find joy and lightness in my spiritual life?
- How does God lift the burdens of the flesh and spirit?
- What does it feel like to be uplifted into God's Light?
- How do I pray for freedom from earthly conditions?

---

Father, bless these thy children and lift their burdens that they may feel the glory and joy of your Love, that they may be light of heart, clear of mind, filled with Love within their souls. That those burdens of their flesh and of their spirits may be lifted. That in this circle they be uplifted into glorious Light. Father lift their burdens, free them from the shackles of these earthly conditions and bring them the joy of Your presence within.

My beloved, beloved souls, Confucius loves you, beloveds. God bless you. God bless you.
