# It Is Important for You to Take Time Each Day in Prayer

**Spirit:** [[Spirits/moses|Moses]] · **Medium:** Al Fike · **Date:** 2015-08-31

> [!quote] The Door
> Prayer opens chambers in the soul. Moses has come a long way to tell you this.

Moses visits to bless the group with an awareness of God's immediate presence, likening His touch to a sunbeam parting clouds and His Love to a benediction opening deep chambers of the soul. He urges daily prayer, elevated thoughts, and attentive listening in silence as the path toward maturity and deep serenity in God's Love.

## Questions this message answers

- How do I build a daily prayer practice that actually transforms me?
- Why does it matter to keep my thoughts elevated throughout the day?
- How do I listen for God in the silence of prayer?
- What does it mean for God to open the deep chambers of the soul?
- How does Divine Love bring peace in the middle of everyday stress and worry?
- What does spiritual maturity look like on the Divine Love path?
- How can I release my material cares and be present with God in prayer?
- What did Moses teach about prayer and communion with God?

---

God bless you. I am Moses and I have come a long ways to be with you this day. Yes indeed, God's presence is here like a sunbeam parting the clouds, His Breath blowing away all of your material cares, His Touch bringing deep peace to you, my beloveds, and His Love flows within your souls, a great benediction, touching those very deep parts within you, chambers that are opened by this Love causing you to be in greater harmony with your Heavenly Father. And as these Gifts are given you will come to know God deeply, my beloveds, deeply.

It is important for you to take time each day in prayer and communion with your Heavenly Father. It is important to keep your thoughts elevated. It is important to listen in the silence in your prayers for God has much to give to you, my beloveds. Be you so new, so very new and young and tender. God brings His care and envelopes you with His Love and nurtures you so that you may be strong in this world, your spirits, your souls shining in Light and beauty. May you come to maturity in God's Love, my beloveds, walking this Path in Light, knowing the Truths which God imparts to you in His silence speaking to your souls, raising you up to a place of deep serenity and peace.

Beloveds, be with God at this moment. Release all the conditions of the material world and know this deep and abiding peace, this profound beauty and joy. It is yours at this moment and God is with you. God bless you, beloved souls. God bless you of God.
