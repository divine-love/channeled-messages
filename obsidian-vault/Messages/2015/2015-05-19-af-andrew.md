# Harmony Through Love, Not Judgment

**Spirit:** [[Spirits/andrew|Andrew]] · **Medium:** Al Fike · **Date:** 2015-05-19

> [!quote] The Door
> The only obstacles in your path are those which you create. At the level of the soul there are no barriers, there is only Love and acceptance

Andrew calls the circle to release false judgments and projected assumptions, reminding them that at the soul level there are no barriers, only Love and acceptance. He urges them to honour one another's unique gifts, walk in humility and kindness, and allow the Light within their souls to flood their consciousness, for the only true obstacles on their path are those they create themselves.

## Questions this message answers

- How do I stop judging others in my spiritual community?
- How does love dissolve conflict between people?
- Why do our differences seem so important when they are not?
- What does it mean to live in the world but not of it?
- How do I accept others who walk the spiritual path differently from me?
- How do my own judgments slow my spiritual progress?
- What does the Bible mean when it says judge not lest ye be judged?
- How do I honor the unique gifts of others while expressing my own?
- How do I bring the soul's knowing of Love into my conscious mind?
- Why does fear create barriers between people who share the same path?

---

Do not get caught up in false judgments or project assumptions. Love will heal all and bring about harmony amongst you, my beloveds. You all walk the same Path and you do so in your unique ways. Accept this. Accept what you have to offer one another, and be humble, and kind, and generous, for these are the ways of Love. You are destined to work together, my beloveds, and this is a time of healing and honesty, forthrightness and integrity. Continue on your journeys together and God will guide you to where you are meant to go.

The only obstacles in your path are those which you create, the only differences are those that you perceive within your minds. The true self, the soul, there are no barriers, there is only Love and acceptance. Bring this within your heart, your mind. Allow that Light within your souls to flood your consciousness and bring you to a higher understanding as you live within this world but not of this world, as you work together as God's children, each unique and equally loved, each with gifts which are to be honored amongst you.

God bless you, I am Andrew. And I watch over all of you as you continue to walk this Path unfolding to reveal your purpose in the world. You come close. Do not let these differences, these fears, and these misconceptions stand in your way. For all this does is retard what is meant to be and slows your progress and brings you needless pain. Beloveds, be beloved, express the Love, know the Love. It is within you and God will bless you with ever greater abundance of the transformative essence of His being. God bless you, beloveds. God bless you.
