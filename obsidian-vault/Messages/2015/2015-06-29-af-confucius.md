# You Must Prepare Yourselves for What Is Coming

**Spirit:** [[Spirits/confucius|Confucius]] · **Medium:** Al Fike · **Date:** 2015-06-29

> [!quote] The Door
> You are entering the calm before the storm. When you enter the storm you must be clothed in the raiments of Light and strong in your relationship with your Heavenly Father

Confucius warns the circle that they are in the calm before the storm, and that what follows will be deeply challenging for both the world and each soul within it. He calls them to redouble their efforts in prayer, to accelerate the cleansing of their souls, and to release all attachments that pull them toward the earth condition, so that when the storm arrives they are clothed in the raiments of Light and strong enough to serve as God's channels of Truth.

## Questions this message answers

- How do I spiritually prepare for difficult times ahead?
- What does it mean to be clothed in the raiments of Light?
- How do I redouble my prayer efforts when life is already demanding?
- What attachments should I release to be ready for what is coming?
- How does soul cleansing accelerate in times of urgency?
- What does it mean to be in the calm before the storm spiritually?
- How do I trust God to care for my obligations as I step deeper into service?
- How does the world's disharmony create opportunity for God's channels of Truth?
- What does the Bible mean when it says put on the full armour of God?
- How do I move from casual prayer to deep commitment?
- How does fear hold me back from fulfilling God's purpose for my life?
- How does God use the storms of life to advance His plan for humanity?

Beloveds, it is Confucius. My precious and beautiful souls, such a blessing it is for you to come together in this way to receive the Father's Love. And through the turmoil of this world and the challenges that you face you have each other to rely upon in prayer together, in loving outreach to one another and to support one another in Love, a blessing from God, my beloveds, a blessing from God.

I have come to tell you that you are entering the calm before the storm, my beloveds. This period of time in your Earth time will be peaceful for you and for this Earth, but what comes after this calm will be very challenging for all of you. The world is on the brink of great change and this change accelerates and you, my beloveds, are on the brink of great change and this change is indeed accelerating within you, a shift within your souls. And each day brings a new test, a new opportunity, a new invitation to grow, to learn, to release that which inhibits the flow of God's Love within your souls. And each one of you confronts those challenging parts of yourself in different ways. Yet in your efforts you will find release from these conditions which cling to you and conspire to turn you away from your Heavenly Father.

And I tell you God is with you always, as are the angels surrounding you and supporting you and the time will come when much of this condition that is not in harmony with God's Love will not affect and control your behavior and emotions. You are in the process of cleansing your souls. This expiation is necessary and has indeed been accelerated, for with what comes next you will be required to be strong and centered in the Love of God. You must prepare yourselves, my beloveds, to redouble your efforts in prayer to bring yourself closer to your Heavenly Father. This effort can no longer be a casual one, but one of great commitment and choice. For when you enter the storm you must be clothed in the raiments of Light. You must be strong in your relationship with your Heavenly Father. You must be willing to let go of all of those attachments which pull you to the Earth condition and be in harmony and within the flow of God's Love and all you require will be provided, all your obligations in this world will come into the care of God's Loving Hand, and you will not feel as if you are sacrificing and compromising in your life. It will be harmonious and it will be powerful in its effects and in your world as a channel of Love you will find your way, each one.

And your journey is unique, your understandings and awarenesses unique, as each soul is unique in this world. But now comes the time when you cannot hold back God's intended purpose for each one of you and the flow of His Love to permeate each and every part of your being and your lives. You cannot compromise in this, but move forward in faith and strength and trust. For God needs your strength, my beloveds, God needs your trust. And in your relationship with your Creator, your faith will blossom and grow.

Walk in the Light and know that we who love you have been with you and will continue to support you and bring you more fully into the Light. There is much work to do, my beloveds, and you are so desperately needed in a world so desperately out of harmony with the laws of creation, the laws of Love. You will see how the forces that draw humanity away from God shall come into disarray and will no longer have its power and control over the minds of men. And in this you have an opportunity as God's channels of Truth to make that difference, to have that effect in the world of Light which you have always dreamed of and desired. Doors will open, my beloveds, and you will joyfully walk through with strength and purpose, wisdom and humility.

Continue to pray for the Father's Love to make a great inflowing into your souls. This is the key to all, as you well know, and you make these efforts daily to be with God in this way. Yes the times ahead will be challenging but as you witness and are a part of the great changes in your world there is much to experience and learn and grow and serve, and this will be a fulfilling time in your lives, a time of great joy and wonderment. Release your fears, release all those conditions which hold you from joyful union with your Creator. We will help you. We are with you and God's mighty hand is upon you, beloved beautiful souls. Continue to walk in the Light and be that channel of Love, and I will continue to serve as your guide and teacher and healer in your lives, my beautiful charges. I love you so. I love you so. God bless you. Confucius loves you.
