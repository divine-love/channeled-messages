# A Walk in the Garden

**Spirit:** [[Spirits/goldie|Goldie]] · **Medium:** Al Fike · **Date:** 2015-05-27

> [!quote] The Door
> Joy does not wait for perfection. It lives in a walk in the garden right now

Goldie offers a tender goodnight blessing, describing the gathered souls as beautiful flowers in God's garden and expressing her joy in walking among them.

- What does it feel like to be loved by God?
- How are we like flowers in God's garden?
- How do child spirits communicate with us?
