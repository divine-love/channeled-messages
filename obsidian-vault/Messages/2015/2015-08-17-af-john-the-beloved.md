# Drink Deep These Living Waters

**Spirit:** [[Spirits/john-the-beloved|John the Beloved]] · **Medium:** Al Fike · **Date:** 2015-08-17

> [!quote] The Door
> The angels have crowded close and God's Love is pouring in; all that is asked of you is to drink deeply and not pull away.

John the Beloved blesses the gathering as angels crowd around to help build the light within their midst, inviting the souls present to drink deeply of God's Living Waters and raise themselves from the human condition into the Divine Presence.

## Questions this message answers

- What does the Bible mean by living waters?
- What did Jesus mean when he offered living waters to the woman at the well?
- How do I receive a deep inflowing of God's Love in prayer?
- What does it feel like when angels gather around a prayer circle?
- How do I raise myself from the human condition to God's presence?

---

God bless you, gracious and beloved souls, I am John the Beloved.

A great Light builds within your midst and the angels crowd around you, my beloveds. Helping you to build this Light that is a gift from God, helping you to reach that place of stillness that you may receive a great inflowing of the Father's Love. Beloved souls, you are being blessed at this moment, your souls receiving the great benediction of Love that is promised you by your Heavenly Father. Drink deep these living waters, beloved souls, drink deep and long. Put your longings to your Heavenly Father. Raise yourselves up from the human condition to the Divine Presence of God.
