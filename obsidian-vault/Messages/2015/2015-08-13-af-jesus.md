# I Too Encountered Difficulty in Teaching This Simple Truth

**Spirit:** [[Spirits/jesus|Jesus]] · **Medium:** Al Fike · **Date:** 2015-08-13

> [!quote] The Door
> Jesus faced the same resistance you face; he is asking you now to finish what his first disciples began.

Jesus speaks to a gathered group of disciples, drawing a direct parallel between their mission and that of his original apostles. He reflects on the resistance and difficulty he encountered in his own day, affirms each soul's unique gifts and purpose, and assures them that the time is coming when great world changes will open many hearts to the simple truth of God's Divine Love.

## Questions this message answers

- Did Jesus face the same difficulties teaching Divine Love that we face today?
- What did Jesus's original disciples experience after his death?
- How do I know what my unique spiritual purpose is?
- Can one person really make a difference in bringing God's Love to the world?
- What will happen when great world changes come and people search for answers?
- How do I recognize my spiritual gifts and allow them to flourish?
- What does it mean to be a disciple of Jesus today?
- How does God use our unique personalities and gifts in service?
- What is the network of light and how do I become part of it?
- How do I open fully to God's guidance for my life?

---

Beloved souls, I come and I am Jesus and I am happy that you have heeded my call to come together. Such a blessing for each one of you, such a blessing for this world to have this Light in the world as you come together in beautiful fellowship. And indeed there is great purpose to your gathering together and this purpose will not be elusive or subtle. Guidance will be given. The specifics will not be lost or confused. You will be shown the way, my beautiful brothers and sisters, so earnest in your desire to serve your Heavenly Father and to serve your fellow man, to bring the truth of Love in this world. Already you feel the power of this momentum to reach out to others.

In my day we encountered the same conditions and difficulties to teach this powerful and simple truth. There was great resistance amongst those in power, great ignorance of those who were a part of the place which I inhabited, and we gathered together as a group, and we discussed amongst ourselves what must be done, and God prepared each beautiful soul to bring this message forward into the world. And after my untimely death they dispersed, and traveled, and taught, and continued to be my disciples. And this was done, for some with great sacrifice, but for all it was a joy, a feeling of rightness, a purpose, and they all knew that God was with them in their journeys and their efforts to teach mankind of the rebestowal of the Divine Essence to the world.

That was many, many years ago and yet you begin anew, my beloveds, my disciples, you begin anew to reach many in the world. Do you understand this? Do you acknowledge this within yourselves that you have chosen this Path, this Work, this journey? Not only for yourselves, but for all your brothers and sisters in the world, to bring this simple truth that shall transform the world one soul at a time. And you collect around you many souls who hunger, who feel the beauty and the power of this Truth of Love. And this shall continue and the efforts shall accelerate. Where you teach one or two you will teach hundreds for the time comes in this world where great changes will bring great fear and confusion, and many souls will seek for answers to understand why and what and to seek a solution to their strife and suffering and to bring harmony back to this world for so much is not in harmony, my children, so much requires the healing Hand of the Heavenly Father and every soul requires this gift of Love, of healing and peace.

Beloved daughter, you walk amongst the poor and you bring hope and Truth. They lack so much in the material, yet they hunger so greatly for the spiritual. This brings an innocence and allows these beloved children an opportunity to truly pray and to long. And you bring the simple Truth, the simple prayer and the Love within your soul ignites their souls to long for this. And yes indeed you will travel to many places bringing this Truth and you will know great fulfillment in this work as God carries you upon the roads and byways of your land and to other lands and other places. Continue to pray for the Love. Long intensely for this gift and to keep foremost in your desire to serve God. And in this you will find the fulfillment which your soul seeks and the purpose which God has placed within you, my dear daughter. Be at peace and know that God's Hand is surely upon you, my dear and beloved daughter, disciple of the Love. So beloved.

Dear daughter, you continue to create the bonds of Love and connection amongst the many around the world and your gifts shine in this way, my beloved and beautiful daughter, and you will find that this network that you create, this web of Light, will feed many, many souls and will create great Light in this world as you are used as God's channel to communicate, to draw in, to reassure and to bring comfort to many souls who seek the reassurance and the comfort and who are encouraged to seek the love through your efforts and your gifts. Know, my daughter, what you do is not a waste it is a great work. Continue in your efforts. Continue to listen to the guidance. Continue to travel as you are guided and the ways and the means will be open as you and your mate continue to do the service which is indeed your purpose. Many travels await you, my beloved, as you communicate over the wires you open many doors and you will walk through those doors and embrace many children who await, who long for fellowship. And as you reinforce and strengthen the bonds of Love they too shall reach out to their brothers and sisters and the great network of Light continues to be built and spread around this world bringing the Truth of God's Love, bringing the healing and the Light into the world. Allow yourself to be fully embraced by the Heavenly Father. Release all that bothers you, my daughter. These things are not important. Know that what is in the flow of the Father's Love is what you must heed and know and carry with you. Great work awaits, much planning has been put into laying the foundations for this journey, this road that meanders throughout the world and touches many souls. You will walk this road with your mate and you will embrace many, many souls who are eager and who long for the touch of God within their hearts. And your touch, your embrace will ignite within them an opening, a desire. It is that touch, that smile, that sparkle in your eyes, my daughter, that touches a soul deeply, opens the way to many blessings. Your gifts shine forth. Continue.

Beloved daughter, you are so new and fresh to the insights and blessings of the Father's Love in a conscious way. My beloved daughter, you have known the Father's Love all of your life, but you have now come to acknowledge this, to truly walk in this Light in clarity and your gifts will open and flourish and flower in beautiful and wondrous ways. You just begin upon this Path of service to God and you will know your guidance from God, my beloved daughter. You will know with clarity and surety, and you will act upon this guidance and allow the Father's Will to flow through you. You are my disciple, my beloved, and you are truly a child of your Heavenly Father who holds you within His arms, embraces you and teaches you many things through your heart. Allow His guidance, His wisdom, His Truth to truly be a part of your being and let this flow to the very heights of your consciousness, the very aspects of your being and into your life fully and completely. And in this acceptance will come many gifts, great opportunity of service, and great and deep wisdom which you will teach and demonstrate to many in this world. You are surrounded by angels, my beloved daughter, and shall continue to walk in the Light, protected and guided, given what you require. Such a journey awaits. Such a journey awaits you, beloved beautiful soul.

My son, yes you have toiled long and hard, and you have pushed away many barriers, and you have taken a difficult road. Yet you persist with strength of your soul, knowing the road you must take has carried you as God has placed within you His Love and encouragement and wisdom. And I see that you accept the guidance given and you are willing to be a channel of Love, to assist in the facilitation of this work, to walk the Path Divine. And soon there will be great and deep insights within your soul that will affirm the rightness of what you have done and what you will continue to do, my beloved and precious son. Much awaits you, my beloved, greater openings of wisdom. You will soon come to know us in palpable ways. What lies before you are great openings and understandings, and you will not be in conflict in your mind. You will truly know the Truth and accept this with all your heart and all your being. You are indeed one of my disciples and you do my work and I love you. I love you - you are my brother. You walk in Light.

My daughter, beloved, sweet soul; yes, you feel as you are entering a new phase of your life and indeed you are. And within that opening and that change are many possibilities and opportunities that await you and await the sure and true acknowledgment of your soul of what you are meant to be and who you are meant to embrace in the world and the opportunities that will flow from your openness and your love, your kindness and your generous spirit. You have prayed for many years for the Love and God has prepared you for those years to do this work. This work awaits you, my beloved and precious soul. It awaits you. The doors are not closed but open. The Light shines through and beckons you forth. Do not be concerned but be joyful at what is to come, the changes that will come, the opportunities that will be laid before you and you will know with such surety what you must do that nothing will stand in your way. And remember, when you do God's Will all falls into place in harmony. There will be no barriers, only joy and peace and you will have the strength required to do what you are meant to do. God has placed within your soul all that you require and this holds true for all of you, my dear and beloved souls.

You only glimpsed what is within you these great and wondrous gifts that reside within your souls, ignited by the Father's Love, expanded by this Light. And so often you do not see yourselves in this way but go about your business each day with a limited perception of what reality is. But soon, my beloveds, soon you shall open to this greater reality, to your greater selves, to the greater work that lies ahead. It comes so very close, in fact it is upon you. It is for you to now open your eyes to truly see, to truly understand what is meant for each one of you. For you each walk your own unique path and God guides you upon that path. Yet we all walk upon the Divine Path, we all walk in this Light and Love. And it is for you as you have done to reach out your hands and grab the other, to walk together in joyous harmony, to know that we are truly, truly God's children in this Love, in this reality.

Beloved and precious souls, my heart sings seeing the Light within you and the joy that you share together and as you come together with more and more souls in the coming days this Light will intensify, this joy will reach the very highest Heavens and God will touch you deeply, each one, and bring you to this place of true understanding, of truly knowing that place of great faith and abundant Love. This is a time for your souls to truly be ignited, to truly take precedence within your being, to truly have that conscious and powerful connection with your Creator. And you step upon the threshold, beloveds, of this wondrous, wondrous reality. How few in the world have this opportunity? How few? Seize what is given. Be hungry, be ravenous for these gifts of Love and all shall be given. That hunger shall never be sated, for it goes on and on for all eternity, to be with God, to know God and to be in that joyous flow of God's Love. God bless you, beloveds. I am your friend and I am your brother Jesus and I love you dearly, my precious and beloved friends upon this world.
