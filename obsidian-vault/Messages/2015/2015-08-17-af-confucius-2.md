# The Angels Come to Bless a Retreat Gathering in Gibsons

**Spirit:** [[Spirits/confucius|Confucius]] · **Medium:** Al Fike · **Date:** 2015-08-17

> [!quote] The Door
> Six voices from the Celestial Kingdom take turns to say the same thing: you are loved, you are not alone, go forward.

Six Celestial spirits speak in sequence to bless the August 2015 Gibsons Retreat gathering — Confucius, Mary, John the Beloved, Peter, Goldie, and Jesus — each offering their love, support, and encouragement to carry the truth of Divine Love boldly into the world.

## Questions this message answers

- Do many spirits attend our prayer circles and gatherings?
- How do Celestial spirits support us on our spiritual journey?
- Is it possible for multiple spirits to speak through one medium?
- What does it mean to share our blessings with all we meet?
- How do I walk this spiritual path boldly and in faith?

---

I am Confucius, I bring my Love. Precious, precious souls. Continue to walk together and many will walk with you….. many, many await you upon the Path. God bless you.

And Mary loves you, Mary loves you. I will continue to support you, my beloveds, and help you when you are in need, and guide you when you are in need. You will feel my gentle touch and all will know how loved you are. God bless you, beloveds.

And I am John. And I wish for you to know that many, many spirits are with you in your efforts and will continue to support you. This is an important work. You must continue to plumb the depths of what is meant to be done and continue to fill your souls with Love from the Father. God bless you on your journeys.

And I am Peter. My love for you is great and I will bring my wisdom to each one of you in my effort to help to guide you upon your path. You step upon a great journey and it will continue for all eternity.

And Goldie loves you and I too, will walk with you. And I will bring flowers and joy and tickle you. Bless you, my friends, you are loved.

My beloveds, I come (Jesus). You have been blessed mightily, my beloveds. Share your blessings with all you meet. Bring your Truth wherever you may, walk this Path boldly and in faith and God will do the rest, God will do the rest. The road is clear, the course is set, you walk forward in Love and joy and fellowship. God bless you, my beloveds, my disciples, my friends, God bless you.
