# Knowing God's Will Through Soul Awakening

**Spirit:** [[Spirits/confucius|Confucius]] · **Medium:** Al Fike · **Date:** 2015-08-13

> [!quote] The Door
> God's Will is not a puzzle for the mind to solve; it is a knowing that blooms in the soul the moment you stop trying to figure it out.

Confucius teaches that knowing God's Will comes not through mental speculation but through the awakened soul. He assures the gathering that the upcoming days together will bring a powerful clarity of purpose as the soul takes precedence over the mind, and encourages them to allow this reorganization to unfold without resistance, trusting that the soul holds a more complete understanding of God's Will than the mind ever can.

## Questions this message answers

- How do I know God's Will for my life?
- Why does God's guidance come through the soul rather than the mind?
- How do I let my soul lead instead of my mind?
- What does it feel like when the soul takes over from the mind?
- How do I stop rationalizing away spiritual promptings?
- Why does spiritual awakening make us different from others around us?
- How do I find the courage to be different in the world?
- Can I really reach a point where I no longer doubt or fear rejection?
- What does it mean to have a confidence born from the soul rather than the mind?
- How does God's Love reorganize the way we think and understand?

---

Did I not say that my precious birds will come together, to flock together? God draws you together, my beloveds, for a great purpose. And when these days are done you will know with surety and a powerful clarity what you must do, the steps you must take forward. In many ways you have tried to discern and decipher the Will of God through your minds and speculations. And I tell you, now comes the time to know the Will of God. But this, my beloveds, is not through your minds, it is through your souls. And we have made great preparations for you to come to this place of clarity, of awakening in this way so that your souls may come to understand your purpose, that road which God has placed you upon and the service which is uniquely yours as your gifts unfold, as the Love continues to pour within your souls. Long for this, my beloveds, this understanding, this soul awareness, this soul wisdom. You are ready to allow this awakening, to embrace it. And the dilemmas which you hold within your mind will melt away. And your mind will truly accept what will be given.

Pray that God will remove all the barriers that continue to inhibit you. For it is difficult for the mind to accept the ways of the soul. For you have lived many years upon this Earth utilizing the perceptions and the understandings that you have built within your minds. And we do not say that this is irrelevant but we say that true understanding will come from your souls and influence and put together in a different way the understanding that will evolve within your minds. It is like a reorganizing of your intellect and the knowledge that you have within you. The soul will do this in response to the Love given. It is not anything that you must put great effort into, it is merely an acceptance and an allowing of the changes within you. Your mind cannot continue to take precedence over the will and desire of your soul. You must allow the soul, which has greater wisdom and a more complete understanding of God's Will to inform your mind and allow the power of God's guiding force to manifest in your lives.

And you get glimpses of this, my beloveds. At times you feel this and know this, and these times will become more frequent until they become the norm for you, my beloveds. And yes, others will perceive you differently, for you will be operating in your world in a different way. But know that this is where humanity must go in their existence upon this world. You are breaking the ground, you are showing them the way and you will not doubt anymore. The fears of rejection will be as nothing. The desire for acceptance will only be directed towards your Heavenly Father. You will truly be unique and powerful and beautiful examples of the Father's Love and channels of His Love in the world. This is the power of the Love, this is why it is given, so that each unique soul will flourish and express that uniqueness in the world and in their existence for all eternity.

You are becoming different. You are very different from when you began. And you shall continue to evolve and walk this Path discovering within yourselves many unique qualities and rather than hiding from this or rejecting this, or rationalizing, it will be expressed in purity and beauty and there will come a confidence not born from the mind, but born from the surety within your souls. And that strength, that strength will draw many to you.

Little precious birds. You are growing into eagles with great wingspans that will embrace the world in Love. Ah the angels are so happy with your progress and your willingness to be the Lights that God intends you to be. Carry on in your efforts, beautiful, beautiful souls, beautiful souls.
