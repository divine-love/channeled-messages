# Be Open to God as a Flower Opens to the Sun

**Spirit:** [[Spirits/andrew|Andrew]] · **Medium:** Al Fike · **Date:** 2015-12-07

> [!quote] The Door
> Many roads brought you here. Only one thing: God, drawing each soul into His Light.

Andrew blesses a diverse gathering of souls who have each come by their own unique road, affirming that God has drawn them together into this circle of Light. He calls each soul to open to God as a flower opens to the sun, trusting that in this simple act they are bonded to one another and to God in powerful and wondrous ways.

## Questions this message answers

- How do I open my soul to God the way a flower opens to the sun?
- Why does God draw diverse souls together into prayer circles?
- How does sitting together in prayer bond souls to one another?
- Can people from very different spiritual backgrounds genuinely meet in God's Love?

---

God bless you, I am Andrew.

You are beloved in the eyes of God.

Every soul here desires a connection, an understanding, and an opening to be with God. And every soul here is unique in their lives and life experiences, in their understanding of their own spirituality, and has walked their unique road and has come here to this circle in many different ways. Yet the soul, the beautiful soul within each one of you has come to be together at this precious time in this place of Light sharing in this experience to be with God. And this, my beloveds, is God drawing you together, bringing you into this Light, opening you to His precious Love and in so doing bonding you one to another in powerful and wondrous ways. Be open, my dear beloved souls, open to God as a flower opens to the sun and be at peace, beloved souls. God bless you, Andrew loves you.
