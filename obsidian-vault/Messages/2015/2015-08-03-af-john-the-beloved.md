# Follow the Sweet Yearnings of the Soul So That You May Know Truth

**Spirit:** [[Spirits/john-the-beloved|John the Beloved]] · **Medium:** Al Fike · **Date:** 2015-08-03

> [!quote] The Door
> The sweet yearning you feel is your soul showing you the way to Truth

John the Beloved explains that absolute Truth cannot be known by the mind alone but only through the awakening of the soul touched by God's Love. He invites his listeners to allow their resistance to fall, to open like a child in joyous vulnerability, and to drink deeply of God's Living Waters so that the age-old questions of the soul may finally be answered.

## Questions this message answers

- How do I know what is spiritually true?
- Can the mind know absolute truth?
- How does the soul know truth that the mind cannot?
- How do I awaken my soul to God's truth?
- How do I put aside worldly distractions to be with God?
- What are the age-old questions of the soul?
- How do I open myself to God's presence?
- What does it mean to be vulnerable before God?
- How do I drink of God's Living Waters?
- How do I quiet my mind enough to hear God?
- Does God truly love me personally?
- What does God want to say to me?

Beloved souls, it is John the Beloved. The Heavenly Father longs to touch each of your souls, my beloveds, to bring His benediction of Love into your souls. And He awaits your longings; He awaits your prayers. He awaits that part of you, that small part that seeks to break through the encrustations of your soul, the filters of your mind, that you and your Heavenly Father may connect in Love, in recognition that you may truly know from whence you have come and you may truly know where you are going so that those age-old questions may be answered within your souls. For Truth comes from the recognition of the soul, the understanding and awareness of the soul that is touched by the Heavenly Grace, the Love of God.

And until that time, you, my children, and all the children in this world cannot know what is true and what is false for your minds are incapable of such knowledge that is eternal and is absolute. Truth comes with the awakening of the soul. True faith comes with the infusion of the soul of the Father's Love, his Essence pouring into you, my beloveds, to open your eyes, to open that part of you that is capable of truly understanding the Truth of God's Creation and universe.

God awaits you, my beloveds, he awaits your call, your longing, your desire to be with Him, to put aside all those worldly conditions, all those thoughts which clutter your mind, all those worries and fears and cares that you may be truly with Him in the silence, in the river of His Love, in the Light of His presence that you may walk in this true and wondrous beauty. Take that step my children, take that step and walk in Light, that pure and beautiful Light of God's presence and Love, for at this moment God's Love flows upon you. Drink deep of these Living Waters, my beloved children, and allow your resistance to fall, that you may feel that joyous vulnerability, that openness like a child, that wonderment, that knowing which God gives you in abundance, freely. And only you can decide what you receive. It is for you to be open, to allow, and to draw it in. Feel the touch, my beloveds, and be in the Light, beautiful souls, who desire goodness and harmony and peace. God is with you. Drink in His presence, His Love and listen to those soft words which He wishes to speak to each one of you that says I love you, you are my precious child and I will be with you always for all eternity. I am with you and I love you. Precious children, precious children, be at peace. God bless you, beloveds. God bless you. I am John and I love you.
