# Each of You Is Being Prepared

**Spirit:** [[Spirits/augustine|Augustine]] · **Medium:** Al Fike · **Date:** 2015-05-18

> [!quote] The Door
> What do I have to give to others that is uniquely from me and my experience upon the Divine Path?

Augustine tells the circle that each one of them is being prepared to give a unique gift to those who will gather at the coming retreat, a gift of inspiration, clarity, and truth drawn from their own experience on the Divine Path. He calls each soul to ask what they have to give, to begin expressing their unique gifts with confidence, and to recognise that in gifting others they will discover their own true selves more fully.

## Questions this message answers

- What unique gift do I have to offer others on the spiritual path?
- How do I discover my own spiritual gifts?
- How does sharing my personal spiritual journey help others?
- How do I walk in the world with confidence in what I have to give?
- Why is my experience on the spiritual path valuable to others?
- How does giving to others help me discover my own true self?
- What does it mean to be a unique creation of God?
- How does God prepare me before I step into service?

Each of you is being prepared, and in this preparation, and as you continue to beseech your Heavenly Father to receive His Love in these coming months before you gather the souls together, you will each be able to give those whom you have gathered a gift, a gift that is uniquely from you to them, a gift of inspiration, of clarity, of truth all in the flow of God's Love. When you are in your prayers and when you come together think of this, my beloveds, what do I have to give to the others that is uniquely from me and my experience upon the Divine Path? What are my gifts? What is my voice to express God's Truth in loving and profound ways? And how can I demonstrate to others my journey, my walk upon this Path that will give comfort and Love and acceptance to those whom I am embracing as they come together under one roof?

And indeed as you gift others with this you will inspire them to gift you with their insights and their Love and in so doing you will find a deep and profound, a lasting and significant bond of Love and insight as to how God touches each soul, each unique soul with His Love and carries them upon their journey of discovery and understanding, and healing.

So much to share, beloved souls. Think of what you have to give and how you may gift it. For each soul has something unique to give to the world. Now is the time to discover that gift, to begin to express this gift, this unique quality within you and to walk in the world with confidence and a surety that what you have to give is indeed a treasure, is indeed something that others desire. And all will be within the flow of God's Love, enhanced and transformed to give healing and insight and strength and inspiration to your brothers and sisters. And acknowledging this you begin to acknowledge your true selves, to love yourselves more fully, to say to the world "I am a beautiful creation of my Heavenly Father and I wish to show you how beautiful I am and acknowledge within you how beautiful you are."

Blessings to you my beloveds, my beautiful souls, who bravely walk in this world, and try with all of their soul's desire and beauty to bring greater Light as God's channels of Love. Continue on your journey and hold forth your Light and God will indeed guide you and protect you and show you the way to your true selves, your true Light, and your true purpose. God bless you, your teacher Augustine always by your side and always loving my beautiful students. God bless you and keep you in His Light. God bless you.
