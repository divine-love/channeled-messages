# Encouragement to Establish Centers of Light

**Spirit:** [[Spirits/faith-nyquist|Faith Nyquist]] · **Medium:** Al Fike · **Date:** 2015-08-20

> [!quote] The Door
> Faith built her life around following God's Will without fully knowing Divine Love; she has come back to say the doors are opening and the souls are ready to flood in.

Faith Nyquist encourages the gathering to support and pursue the creation of Centres of Light in the world, assuring them that God will provide all that is required. She speaks from her own experience of dedicating her Earth life to following God's Will, and affirms that these Centres will become refuges of peace, love, and healing for the many souls who are seeking, regardless of religious background.

## Questions this message answers

- What is a Centre of Light and how is it different from a church?
- Does God really provide the practical means when we step forward in service?
- How do I create a spiritual space that welcomes people of all backgrounds?
- What would a place of peace and healing for seekers look like in the world?
- How do I pray for clarity about what God wants me to do?
- What does it mean to follow God's Will as the central commitment of a life?
- How does the Holy Spirit touch the soul in prayer?

Beloveds, I am Faith and I come to speak to you on the subject of creating Centres of Light in the world. I walked this Earth not too long ago and I did my Father's bidding. My life was dedicated to following the Father's Will in this world and He took me on a great and beautiful journey of Love. I did not however understand as you do the power of God's Love, though I knew of God's Love and I was within His care and Light.

And there has been some discussion of late regarding creating Centres of Light in the world and I wish to encourage you, my brothers and sisters who still dwell upon this Earth, to be supportive and to think about the opportunities that have been laid before you to create these Centres, for they are required in the world. God will provide the means, the buildings, the finances, all that is required will be provided given your intention to work and support in loving harmony the possibilities of creating these Centres which will be refuges for the many souls who are seeking, the many souls who desire a place of peace, a place of Love, a place filled with Light and joy.

God wishes that these places be on the Earth, upon the Earth in a way that does not require one to have a specific religious belief but only a soul desire to be in the flow of Love and peace and to receive from the Heavenly Father His healing and touch. And these Centres of Light are formulating within your minds at this time but will become a reality in very short order. It will be a reality, and the doors will open, and the souls will flood in, and their eyes will be open, their souls will be inspired, and they will feel God's touch, just as you feel God's touch with you at this time. You are creating here a Centre of Light, a place of refuge, a place where you will receive the nourishment you require and desire to progress within your souls. The Father's great Love awaits your opening and desire.

Many from my side of life are working with you, each one of you, beloved souls. For each soul here desires to be a channel of Love and Light in the world. Each soul aspires to be with God. Each soul continues to struggle through their human conditions and the Earthly conditions and to raise themselves above that condition, to heal within themselves those hurts and errors that lie within, which are a part of every soul on this planet. You struggle to be in Light and God answers your prayers. God touches your soul. God wants you to be in that place of deep and abiding peace within the flow of His Love, embraced by His presence. His Love is here for you. His Love is here for you. Open yourselves, my beloveds. Open, ask and desire. Bring this unity of desire and openness together, direct it towards your Heavenly Father and feel the response of the Holy Spirit touching your souls, bringing you to a place of joy, of upliftment and peace.

Blessed and beloved souls, you are truly brothers and sisters and this bond of Love will grow as His Love grows within you. God bless you, I am Faith and I reside within the Celestial Kingdom and I am with you often. I am with you working to bring Light into this world, to bring Love, more Love into this world and to bring Truth where there is error, to bring clarity where there is confusion, to bring comfort where there is hurt and fear and error. And you, my beloved brothers and sisters, will also do this work wherever you may be in the world. God desires for you to do this work and if you are unclear as to what you must do, pray for guidance, pray that the angels will help you to find that clarity and to do this work in humility and grace and Love. God bless you, my dear friends, and know that what is the Father's Will, will be. It will be. God bless you, God bless you, I love you.
