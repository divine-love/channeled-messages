# Sow Seeds of Love Upon Your Journey

**Spirit:** [[Spirits/confucius|Confucius]] · **Medium:** Al Fike · **Date:** 2015-08-17

> [!quote] The Door
> You are sowing seeds of hope with every step you take; you just cannot see yet which ones will bloom.

Confucius teaches that those who carry Divine Love within their souls are already sowing seeds of hope wherever they go, often without knowing it. He encourages the gathering to open themselves to receive God's blessings through all the avenues of His creation, and to walk in the world as a beautiful gift to every soul they encounter.

## Questions this message answers

- Do I bring God's Love to others even when I am not aware of it?
- How does my soul touch other souls without words?
- Can animals and nature respond to spiritual light?
- How do I open myself to receive more of God's blessings?
- What does it mean to be a purveyor of God's Love in the world?
- How does God use us to plant seeds of hope in others?
- Why do some people feel drawn to certain spiritually awakened souls?
- How do I stop judging others and see the beauty in every soul?
- What blessings might I be missing because my perception is too narrow?

---

Precious and beloved souls, I am Confucius and I come to greet you this morning with my love and to give you some guidance.

The Father's Love is everywhere and every place in His universe, but you, my beloveds, have drawn in this Love through your prayers and desire to receive it. And with this gift of Love within your souls you may bring to others a desire for this Love. For God uses you to plant these seeds into all you meet. When you walk upon this Earth you are God's purveyors of Love and Truth, remember this, my beloveds. Remember that wherever you go, you bring this Love. Whomever you meet, you bring this Love and you need not say the words. It is your being, it is your soul that carries the Love. And often you are not aware that this blessing, this inspiration, is given as God uses you through your souls to touch another soul.

Yes, my beloveds, you cast these seeds of hope into the world. You walk in the Light of God's Touch and at times you are able to speak the Truth to those who are willing to listen. And as you grow stronger in this Love, receiving ever more of this great benediction within your souls, others will be consciously drawn to you. They will ask their questions, they will want to know from where your loving presence comes and you may tell them of God's Touch of Love which is for all, it is for all. And blessings from God come from many different areas, many different aspects of life.

The dear soul, the son, who perceived the whales, it was their answer to the call of your Love, my beloveds. They came to acknowledge your presence and the Light that you carry. Many creatures are drawn to the Light, they are sensitive to these things. And in this beautiful contact between this dear soul and these beautiful creatures there was hope given to him, something shifted within him and a blessing given. God uses many ways to bless His children. God gives us blessings through many avenues in this world. For the open soul there is much that they may receive and perceive in the world of God's creation, for it is filled with many, many mysteries and gifts.

Open yourselves, my beloveds. Open yourselves to what God has to give you and open yourselves to what God can give to others through you. This is what will save this world, my beloveds, all that is given in Love, all that is of the Heavenly Father in His glorious creation. You know so little of this, your perceptions are so narrow, your understanding limited. But as your soul is ignited with the Love you will know, you will be receptive, you will absorb all the blessings that God has to give you in all of these wonderful ways and means and avenues of Love. Gifting you with a life filled, filled to the brim with glorious life, with glorious gifts of beauty and love. It awaits you my children. It awaits you to open your eyes, to receive as God wishes to give. And in His abundant creativity so many avenues and possibilities are there for you awaiting your perception and reception of His gifts of Love to you in this beautiful world.

As you walk in this world, as you walk amongst your brothers and sisters be that beautiful gift in the world. May your Light shine as the Love glows within you as your eyes perceive the beauty of your brothers and your sisters in this world. For every soul has its beauty, every soul is unique, every soul has the possibility of receiving the Father's Love and your gift to them is your presence with them in Love and acceptance, in Love and acceptance.

Do not judge, do not fear, but be in that wave of the Father's Love, that mighty river that flows from His Great Soul to all the souls of His creation. And you will receive in abundance and give in abundance and this will grow and grow. God will sow the seeds of Love wherever it is possible and He asks each and every one of you to make this possible, to walk this road in clarity, in humility, in fervent service to His Will. In this you will indeed receive your rewards, your gifts from many aspects of the Father's Creation. So much awaits, so much awaits.

A gift of Love to you, my beloveds, a gift of Love, do not hold yourselves back but bring this in, absorb this Love as much as you are able. Do not hold back, release all that which is in the way, my beloveds. God is touching you at this time with His Love. Beautiful souls, precious and beautiful souls, God bless you, my beloveds. Confucius loves you.
