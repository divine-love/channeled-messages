# Jesus Comes to Open and Bless a Retreat Gathering

**Spirit:** [[Spirits/jesus|Jesus]] · **Medium:** Al Fike · **Date:** 2015-08-19

> [!quote] The Door
> Jesus says he called you, he is with you, and he will walk with you for all eternity; the only question is whether you will keep walking.

Jesus blesses the retreat gathering with his presence, assuring each soul that he is with them always — in their longings, their struggles, and their moments of grace. He calls them his brethren on the Path Divine and promises that together they will walk for all eternity, discovering ever more of the power and glory of God's Love.

## Questions this message answers

- What does it mean to walk the Path Divine with Jesus?
- How do I focus my longings and yearnings toward God?
- What does it mean to be at-one with God?
- Does Jesus consider us his brothers and sisters?
- How do I find release from the pain and conditions of this world?
- What does it mean that all souls are destined to come to the Light?

---

My beloved souls I come, I am Jesus. I come to bless you as you pray together to receive the Father's Holy Love within your souls. I come because I love you. I come because I have called you. Blessed souls, beautiful brothers, beautiful sisters who walk this Path Divine I am with you. I am with you as you long for the Father's Love. I am with you as you seek the Truth. I am with you in your daily struggles, and in those times when you feel you walk within the Father's Grace and Love, I am with you. And I shall always be with you, my beloveds, I shall always be with you. Every soul that yearns as I yearn, who walks as I walk, who seeks the Father's Love, seeking the Truth that comes with this benediction within your souls and my soul brings us together. We walk together in this Light that is God's touch upon us.

Together we shall walk for all eternity, my brethren. Together we will discover the power of God's Love, the glory of God's Love, the intricacies of God's Creation, the wonderment of God's Soul and His great being. As God continues to open our eyes and expand our souls so that we may have the capacity, the ability to truly know Him, to truly be at-one with our beloved Creator, the source of all life, the source of all Love the source of all.

My beloved brothers and sisters, I know that you struggle in your lives and yet you reach out for Light, you reach to be healed, to know Love, and to release all those conditions of this Earth which hold you within the pain that humanity carries in this world. And you shall find your release, you shall find the answers to your questions, you shall find the truth and you shall find joy and freedom in your pursuit of this one great Truth, this one wondrous blessing and gift, which is the Father's Essence, His Love pouring into your souls.

Beloveds, beloved children, continue on your journeys. Continue to pray as earnestly and fervently as you are able. Focus your yearnings, your longings, your desires upon this one great pursuit and all shall come to you, my beloveds. All that you seek, all that you desire, all that is upon this Path Divine which leads to wondrous, glorious Light, great understanding, and openings and knowings within. You are loved, each soul is precious in God's sight, each soul is destined to come to the Light and those upon our Path, this loving Path shall know great and deep and eternal rewards. God bless you, my beloveds, I am your brother, I am your friend and I love you. I am Jesus. God bless you. God bless you, beloveds.
