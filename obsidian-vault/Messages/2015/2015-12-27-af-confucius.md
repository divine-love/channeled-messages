# Awakening of the Soul Mind

**Spirit:** [[Spirits/confucius|Confucius]] · **Medium:** Al Fike · **Date:** 2015-12-27

> [!quote] The Door
> The flower has been budding for so long. Now it is beginning to bloom.

Confucius announces that something new is dawning for the group — a place of inner knowing where the mind no longer doubts but opens to God's guidance and the soul takes ascendency. He calls them to accept the coming changes with joy, assuring them that this awakening will bring serene strength, effortless service, and the full blossoming of what has long been budding within.

## Questions this message answers

- What does it feel like when the soul mind begins to awaken?
- How do I move from doubt and nervousness about my spiritual path to inner peace and knowing?
- What does it mean for the soul to take ascendency over the mind?
- How does God prepare us for the work He has planned for us?
- What are the signs that a spiritual awakening is beginning?
- How do I release the shackles that keep me from fully becoming who I truly am?
- How does walking in God's Will make service feel effortless rather than difficult?

---

Beloved children, it is Confucius. I have been with the dear soul and the soul that is transitioning into spirit and healing has been given to all those present. Something new is coming for you, my children, and I know that you feel this within your hearts, a newness, an opening, a coming to a new place of understanding within you where the mind will no longer doubt but be open to God's Touch and guidance and acceptance and love for yourselves and for all those around you, a deep inner peace and a knowing that comes from your souls, bringing you a calmness within. And rather than being nervous about each step you take, you will glide along your path in serenity, deep peace and a strength that shall not detract you from your purpose. This is a Gift that God has given you, my beloveds, for the time fast approaches when you will need this strength, this inner peace, this knowing and this Love to accomplish what must be done in this world, to be in the flow of God's Will.

Accept the changes that are coming, both within you and around you, and be at peace. There is nothing to fear and there are great gains to be had, treasures laid up within your souls, service, wonderful service of love and a life that is filled with giving and being close to God, a life that is a true Gift which you will experience in this world. And you feel this, beloved souls, you feel the dawning time, the awakening and the changes that it will bring. Step joyously forward in the Light and rejoice for God has touched you deeply and has a great purpose for each one that will bring harmony to your lives, yourselves and to many souls in this world, who will be touched by your channel of Love. And you will work effortlessly in this flow, for it will bring forth your true natures, your true selves and as such will bring great joy. Awaken my children, awaken. To the truth you truly are. Awaken, my dear beloveds, to God and His Touch upon you and release your shackles and be joyously free, beloveds. Confucius loves you and is joyed to see what is coming for each one of you, my beloveds. The flower that has been budding for so long shall finally bloom bringing great beauty to this world. God bless you, beloved souls, God bless you.
