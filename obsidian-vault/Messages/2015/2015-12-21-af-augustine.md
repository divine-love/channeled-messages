# Be a Channel of Love in a Changing World

**Spirit:** [[Spirits/augustine|Augustine]] · **Medium:** Al Fike · **Date:** 2015-12-21

> [!quote] The Door
> The world's heart softens at Christmas. Step in.

Augustine speaks at Christmas time, encouraging the group to take full advantage of the season's openness in the world to be channels of love. He warns of great and surprising changes coming and calls them to hold firm to their path without compromise, letting love inform every thought, word, and action.

## Questions this message answers

- How can I be a more effective channel of love during the Christmas season?
- What did Jesus really come to teach the world?
- How do I hold to my spiritual path without compromise when the world around me is changing?
- How do I let love inform every aspect of my daily life?
- How do I stay spiritually grounded through difficult and surprising changes in the world?

---

It is your teacher, Augustine. And you come upon a time of peace and goodwill in your world, celebrating the birth of the Master and this is a time where you, my children, may be channels of Love in great measure as many souls are receptive and open and feeling the power of this condition of Love and Light in the world. Continue to be that channel of Love, beloveds, that beautiful channel of Love, to walk in the Light. Continue in your prayers and efforts to reach ever higher to that place close to God in the flow of His Love, beloved souls.

For the time is upon us, great change in the world, great change within you, surprising changes, difficult changes, wonderful changes. So much is in store as you progress in this world and the world changes and progresses in the flow of God's Hand and Love upon it. Beloved souls, continue to pray earnestly, to walk your truth, your path, your Love for the world. Do not compromise, but be more firmly within this Light, walk more fully in the Truth and be that pure and beautiful channel of Love in your world, beloveds, for Jesus came to bring this message of Love and you my children have understood this message. Bring it to the world in your actions, your thoughts, your prayers, your speech, in all the ways that you are able may love inform all and be your touchstone with every breath. Beloved souls, God bless you and keep you in this Light and the angels will be with you as you continue to reach out to many, we will be with you. God bless you, your teacher Augustine.
