# Answering the Call to Gather in Love

**Spirit:** [[Spirits/confucius|Confucius]] · **Medium:** Al Fike · **Date:** 2015-03-29

> [!quote] The Door
> The journeys will continue and you will come again and again to those who desire your presence. This is your purpose and calling

Confucius gently notes that some present do not yet understand the courtesies of prayer and meditation and asks that they be educated in God's Love. He then affirms two souls who have journeyed far and gathered many in prayer, confirming their mission and calling, before turning to the broader gathering and urging all present to seek God together in fellowship and allow the bonds of love to support them to greater heights.

## Questions this message answers

- How do I introduce someone to prayer who has never prayed before?
- How do I share the truth of God's Love with people from a different culture or religion?
- What does it mean to answer a spiritual calling?
- How does fellowship with other seekers deepen my own spiritual experience?
- Why do some people seem drawn to spiritual truth while others are not interested?
- What does Confucius teach about love and spiritual community?

I am Confucius. It seems that some of my countrymen do not understand the courtesies of prayer and meditation. You must educate them. Tell them of God's Love and how to receive this Love.

My two beloved souls, you have journeyed far and you have gathered many together in prayer and you have answered our call, the call of the Master, to do so. And you have found that God will draw those who have eager souls and desires for this Love. And you see that there is a mission, a need in the world for this effort. And within your minds, you may have desired greater numbers, more souls to be drawn, those who know nothing of this Love to come and understand the importance and power of this love and this will come my brethren, this will come. And the journeys will continue and you will come again and again to those who desire for your presence - for greater prayers, for greater love, this is indeed your purpose and a mission and a calling.

And you my precious souls have answered this call as well and you have come together with sincerity and honesty, with a desire to be together in Love and to be with God in Love. And I would urge you to come together and seek this in fellowship. And as you share this experience of Love you will create a bond amongst yourselves that will be powerful and will support one another to greater heights of love and fellowship and understanding. Beloved souls, seek God in this way, and you will take a wondrous journey and God will show you the marvels of His creation, the depths of His Love, the beauty that is all around and all within you. Much to explore and to experience. And to be in that rapturous Love and true soul awareness. It awaits you, my beloveds, it awaits. It is for the asking and it is for you to open this mighty door of truth and peace and wonderment. God rises you up now, drink deep of this Love. Know that you are loved. And I love you, beautiful souls. I love you.
