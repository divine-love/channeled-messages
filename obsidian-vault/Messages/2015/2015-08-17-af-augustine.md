# What Will You Do...The Choice Is Yours to Make

**Spirit:** [[Spirits/augustine|Augustine]] · **Medium:** Al Fike · **Date:** 2015-08-17

> [!quote] The Door
> The choice is already made within your soul; the work now is simply to uncover what is already there.

Augustine closes a retreat session with a brief but penetrating message, reminding the gathering that their soul purpose is already chosen within them and waiting to be uncovered. He encourages complete honesty in their sharing, whether lost or knowing, and assures them that help will come.

## Questions this message answers

- How do I uncover the purpose that is already within my soul?
- Is it okay to admit I feel spiritually lost?
- How do I know when I am speaking from my soul versus my mind?
- What does it mean that I have already made a soul choice?
- How do I ask for help when I feel lost on my spiritual path?

Your teacher Augustine here. What will you do, my beloveds? The choice is yours. What are you willing to do? The choice is yours. And that choice rests within your souls. That answer rests within your souls. And in fact, you have already made that choice. It is for you to uncover — your purpose, that gift that lies within.

Continue to speak to one another, and to be completely and truly honest. For when you are speaking from your soul, this is what is required. If you feel lost, say you are lost. If you know, say that you know. If you are somewhere between that journey of being lost and knowing, ask for help. It will come. And you will discover, in surprising ways, the answers.

God bless you, beloveds. We are now finished with this session. You are all beautiful. You are all loved. And you will all assist in the great changes that are coming in many diverse ways. God bless you. God bless you.
