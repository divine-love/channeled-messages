# Comfort for Parents and Their Children

**Spirit:** [[Spirits/mary|Mary]] · **Medium:** Al Fike · **Date:** 2015-05-11

> [!quote] The Door
> I am with your children and I love you and love them

Mary brings a tender assurance to parents in the circle that she is present with their children and grandchildren, that prayers for loved ones are answered, and that the Light drawn into a praying soul flows outward to bless all those within its reach.

## Questions this message answers

- Can I pray for my children and grandchildren even if they are not on a spiritual path?
- Does God send angels to watch over my children?
- How does my own prayer life bless the people I love?
- Is Mary present with families who pray?
- How does the Light I receive in prayer reach those around me?

I want you to know how much I love you. And I want you to know that I am with your children often, those that struggle and those who are making their way through their lives, and for some the children of your children I am with them. For your prayers are answered in this way that Love is given and Love is given to all those within your grasp and Love. So many are blessed and given the comfort, and the protection, the love and the encouragement from the angels through your prayers, through your efforts for Light. Yes, I am with your children and I love you and love them. God bless you, beloveds, beautiful souls continue to be with God and to draw his love within you, and this great light will be imparted to many and all your loved ones and all those who seek to be with you in this love. God bless you. God bless you beloveds.
