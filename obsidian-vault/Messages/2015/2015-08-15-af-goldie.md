# Message of Joy and Support

**Spirit:** [[Spirits/goldie|Goldie]] · **Medium:** Al Fike · **Date:** 2015-08-15

> [!quote] The Door
> Goldie comes because she can see you need to laugh; even heaven knows that joy is as necessary as prayer.

Goldie arrives with her characteristic joy to remind the gathering that many spirits surround them with love, blessings, and unique gifts, and encourages them to laugh and feel the depth of God's care for each of them.

## Questions this message answers

- Do spirits really surround us with love and support?
- Is laughter a spiritual gift?
- How deeply does God care for each individual soul?
- Can I feel the presence of angels around me?

---

I am Goldie,….. because I see you, you all need to laugh and I come to help you to laugh and to feel joy. Although you've heard from few of us, know that there are many of us that come to you to support you and love you and bring their blessings to you. And we each bring our special gifts to give to you precious souls. Many gifts given, much love poured upon you. Truly know this my friends, truly know this, how beautifully cared for you are in the Light of God's Love and I love you. Many times you make me laugh. God bless you.
