# Be Open to His Love

**Spirit:** [[Spirits/andrew|Andrew]] · **Medium:** Al Fike · **Date:** 2015-08-21

> [!quote] The Door
> Openness is not passive. It is an active choice to receive what God is already offering

Andrew tenderly calls the gathered souls to reach beyond one another and open themselves completely to their Heavenly Father, who pours His Love upon them in that moment. He invites those who are hiding to come out and receive God's deep and abiding Love.

## Questions this message answers

- How do I open myself completely to God's Love?
- What does it mean to reach out to God?
- How do I know how much God loves me?
- How do I release my burdens to God?
- Why do I hide from God and how do I stop?
- Does God really love me personally and deeply?

---

My beloved children, you have reached out to one another, and you have joined hands in Love, and you have found harmony amongst you, beautiful, beloved souls. Now you must reach God and find harmony with Him. Open yourselves completely to your Heavenly Father, who, at this moment, pours His Love upon you. Release your burdens. Those who are hiding, come out, come out. We all long for God and He is here for you in His great Love. He is here for you in His deep and abiding care for you. He wants you to know how much you are loved. He wants you to know how deep that Love is. Blessed children, come together and be with God. He is here and He loves you. God bless you. I am Andrew and I love you.
