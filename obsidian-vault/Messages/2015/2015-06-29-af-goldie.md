# Be Joyful and Walk in the Light

**Spirit:** [[Spirits/goldie|Goldie]] · **Medium:** Al Fike · **Date:** 2015-06-29

> [!quote] The Door
> Take time to enjoy your life and be happy in this world, for God has given you much and you live in the Light

Goldie brings a characteristically light and humorous close to a serious session, gently reminding the circle that joy and enjoyment of life are themselves a form of walking in the Light, and that God has given them much to be grateful for.

## Questions this message answers

- Is it spiritual to enjoy life and be happy?
- How does joy help me walk in the Light?
- What does it mean that God has given me much to be grateful for?
