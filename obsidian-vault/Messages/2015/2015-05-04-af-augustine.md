# Be Aware of Each Step You Take

**Spirit:** [[Spirits/augustine|Augustine]] · **Medium:** Al Fike · **Date:** 2015-05-04

> [!quote] The Door
> You must all be aware of each step that you take, each word that you speak, each feeling that you feel

Augustine impresses upon the circle how closely they are watched and how much is entrusted to them as a force for Light in the world. He urges each one to be conscious of every step, word, and feeling, choosing Light over the negative conditions that conspire to create disharmony, and assures them that God's Love and protection are always available for the asking.

## Questions this message answers

- How do I stay in the Light when the world pulls me toward negativity?
- How do I choose Light when I feel like a victim of circumstances?
- How do dark forces affect spiritual communities?
- Why does prayer lift me out of negative conditions?
- How do I stay conscious of my thoughts, words, and feelings?
- How do I stop judging myself when I fall short spiritually?
- How does love dissolve the human condition?
- How do I maintain harmony within a spiritual community?
- What does it mean to be unassailable through faith?
- How do I become an example of God's Love in everyday life?
- Why does God entrust ordinary people with such a large mission?

Your teacher Augustine is here. I am so very pleased that you have taken the time to come together in this way. And please do not feel that this time is wasted or problematic in any way, for it is important for all of you to come together in harmony, and that your thoughts harmonize with one another, and you become aware of that which is not in harmony within you, and about you, and amongst you.

Are you aware of how many watch you, my beloveds? How many look to you and are eager for your leadership and your contribution to all who are a part of this flow of Divine Love in the world? For you are entrusted to accomplish much in this world, to bring the many disparate, yet eager, souls together in harmony, to knit and unite these many beautiful souls who are scattered throughout your world and often feel isolated and do not have the loving support which you share amongst you - this beautiful gift that I know that you cherish and honor. God has given you this gift. He has guided each one of you to be amongst your fellowship. For each one of you has a valuable part and role within this circle. And indeed in many ways you are not aware of this, only a glimpse of the potentials and possibilities that are inherent in this beautiful group.

And we in spirit are with you, my beloveds, to help you to grow into this role that is uniquely yours as you long for the love and the love opens the doors to your unfolding awareness and soul growth. And as you progress in the Love, so will come to you a greater understanding and knowledge of what it is that you do together, how you are going to become and are to some extent already a force in the world for Light and change and Love. To operate in this way, to bring this channel of Light to this dark place requires strength and discipline, faith and courage in your thoughts, your deeds, your prayers, your actions.

You must all be aware of each step that you take, each word that you speak, each feeling that you feel. For the conditions of this world and the dark forces of this world would conspire to cause strife, and anxiety, and anger, and judgements within each one of you, towards each of you. And it is for you, my beloveds, to choose Light, to release all those conditions which would cause disharmony amongst you and amongst all you meet. It is a choice, my beloveds, though at times when you feel in the midst of this condition you feel you are a victim, that so much conspires against you.

Yet in prayer, beseeching your Heavenly Father for assistance and Light, healing and Love, you may be lifted out of these conditions into a place of harmony and Love. Indeed each day brings its challenges and its opportunities to grow, to express yourselves in beautiful ways. And we know that you do often fall into the trap of negativity, but I tell you that you have great Light within your souls, great resources given to you by your Heavenly Father to climb into the Light, to walk in the light, to be the light and the Love. It is available to you for the asking and it is an exercise of your faith that this is so. Walk in the Light, my beloveds. Make those efforts to be with one another in loving harmony, and do not allow the negative conditions of this world to impinge upon this work that you have committed together to do.

You are all strong enough, you all have the Love within your souls and are very capable of walking in the Light always, and you can be unassailable given that choice and that faith that God is with you always, which He is, and will ever be with you, my beloveds, in His loving protection, and Light, and healing, and care. So much is given, my beloveds. And indeed you are asked to give of yourselves. But when you look at what has been given to you in Love, do you not feel that this is a small price? A small effort on your part to give to the world in this measure, to walk in Light, to be an example of God's Love and Truth, to be at peace within yourselves and to bring peace to those around you.

My beloved and beautiful souls, my dear students, be aware of the choices that you make and the thoughts that you have. And do not feel guilty or angry at yourselves for falling short of this goal of Light, but love yourselves. For Love is the key to all of this. Love is the answer that you seek, the truth that is God. And as you express Love more fully within yourselves and about you, the human condition, the darkness, the fear and the strife will fade away and be a distant memory, and you will know great freedom and joy, and your life will be an expression of God's Love and Will, and you will begin this great journey of your purpose who you are meant to be in the world, who you truly are that God created you to be as your souls shine forth and express this Light and Love in the world.

Walk with us, beloved souls, and seek your Heavenly Father with every breath. And you will be a part of a wondrous plan for the salvation of mankind. This is our mutual desire to work to these ends, to bring Light and peace to this world. And God shines His Light, His Love, His care upon you and into this world. Be with God, my beloveds. Be with God and we all will ride this beautiful wave of Love to distant shores, to many souls who eagerly await, who hunger for what you have, who desire for something better and who are eager to lay down their pain for freedom, and light, and peace, and Love. God bless you, my beloveds. We walk with you, and we pray for you, and pray with you, and are ever by your side. Beloveds, beloveds, seek the Love and all else will follow. God bless you and I love you. Your teacher Augustine, ever with you. God bless you.
