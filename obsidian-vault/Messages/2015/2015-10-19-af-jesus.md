# You Create Circles of Light in the World

**Spirit:** [[Spirits/jesus|Jesus]] · **Medium:** Al Fike · **Date:** 2015-10-19

> [!quote] The Door
> Together you are a Circle of Light. Individually you are waves of healing in a world that cries out.

Jesus blesses the gathered group, affirming that their collective prayers and service create a Circle of Light while each individual soul radiates outward into the world. He speaks of a world crying out in pain and confusion, and of those who walk the Divine Love path as an oasis of healing and peace, together generating waves of light that will ignite the consciousness of mankind.

## Questions this message answers

- How does gathering together in prayer create a circle of light that heals the world?
- What does it mean to be an oasis of love and peace for lost souls?
- How does individual soul expression combine with group effort in God's service?
- What does Jesus teach about the role of small groups in transforming the world?
- How do waves of love gradually change the consciousness of humanity?
- What does it mean to walk in humility and service as a channel of God's Love?

---

Beloveds, I come to touch each one of you, my beloveds, and to bring my blessing of Love to you who work earnestly to do the Father's Will, to bring Light into this world, and to do my work, which is to bring the message of Love to all mankind. You have come closer in that flow of God's intention and desire and plan to bring mankind in Light and harmony in this world.

Allow your souls to speak your truth, allow your selves to walk in humility and service. No greater joy will there be than to serve God in this way, to bring Truth in the flow of Love, to speak words of comfort and Love to the lost souls, to join together in this mighty effort to heal the world which cries out in its pain and confusion. So many lost, so many swept away by the conditions of this world and you provide an oasis, an oasis of Light and comfort, healing and peace as God's channels. Together, you create this beautiful Circle of Light and individually, you radiate out into this world expressing your true and beautiful selves, touching many souls, touching many souls as you are true to God's Love and all that is a part of His blessing within you, His presence about you, His mighty Touch that comes through you and touches deeply the souls who are open and cry out for that Touch.

Continue, my beloveds, continue and you shall bring mighty change to this world, waves and waves of Light and change as the consciousness of mankind is ignited with Love. Continue, my beloveds. God bless you, my brothers, my sisters, God bless you. I am your brother and your friend, Jesus. God bless you.
