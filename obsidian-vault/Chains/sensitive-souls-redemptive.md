# Chain: Sensitive Souls Redemptive

> The sensitive soul is the saving grace of this world

**The argument it traces:** The sensitive soul feels the cruelty of the world acutely and struggles to be in it -> sensitivity is a sign of soul growth, not vulnerability -> the Love within the growing soul is its protection (thoughts attract like energies by law) -> such souls refuse the darkness of judgment and fear, progress swiftly, and stand as the redemptive example whose presence sows seeds of Love
