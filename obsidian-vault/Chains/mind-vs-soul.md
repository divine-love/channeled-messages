# Chain: Mind Vs Soul

> The soul, not the mind, is the seat of truth and the receiver of Divine Love

**The argument it traces:** The mind and soul are distinct faculties -> the mind constructs and can obstruct -> the soul knows and receives directly -> truth and Divine Love come through the soul, not intellectual effort -> the two minds are ultimately integrated into one consciousness
