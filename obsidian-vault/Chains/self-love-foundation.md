# Chain: Self Love Foundation

> Loving oneself is the prerequisite for loving others

**The argument it traces:** One cannot extend compassion outward while suppressing or condemning the self -> the seeker is not to blame for their condition and should meet their own cleansing with compassion -> self-love opens the capacity to love others fully -> and the soul that loves itself becomes a clear channel of Love to the world
