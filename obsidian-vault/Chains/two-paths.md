# Chain: Two Paths

> The natural love path and the Divine Love path are distinct, and the Divine path is swifter and higher

**The argument it traces:** Every soul carries wounds requiring purification -> two paths exist: natural love (long, by full conscious recompense) and Divine Love (swift, by God's transforming fire) -> Divine Love reaches the Celestial Heavens where natural love cannot -> the choice is open to all
