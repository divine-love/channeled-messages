# Chain: Divine Love Mediumship

> How spirit communication works and what makes it clear and trustworthy

**The argument it traces:** The basic laws of communication are the same for all -> but clarity, quality, and safety of reception depend on the soul's development in Divine Love, not the ambitions or gifts of the mind -> a true instrument is prepared over years into a finely tuned, clear channel -> messages centred on ego, or not on God's Love and the flowering of the soul, are not from Celestial angels (discernment) -> and this capacity is not confined to designated mediums: every soul accompanied by angels has some avenue of communication that grows as it stays open and awakens in love
