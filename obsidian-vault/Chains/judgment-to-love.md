# Chain: Judgment To Love

> Judgment is incompatible with Love; the soul learns to choose Love instead

**The argument it traces:** Judgment of others reveals our own soul state -> beneath judgment lies hidden hurt -> the antidote is conscious choice, not suppression -> as the soul grows in Love, compassion and forgiveness come reflexively
