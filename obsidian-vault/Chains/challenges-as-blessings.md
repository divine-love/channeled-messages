# Chain: Challenges As Blessings

> Every difficulty is a blessing that strengthens the soul

**The argument it traces:** Hardship, whatever its source, is redeemed into soul-growth by God's design -> every struggle, test, and challenge brings insights and lessons that strengthen the soul -> the strengthened soul becomes a more powerful channel of Love -> so the challenge is met with trust rather than resentment
