# Chain: Incremental Journey

> Growth in Divine Love is inherently incremental

**The argument it traces:** The soul's growth advances in leaps, then trickles, impeded by world and self yet lifted by God -> the oscillation (two steps forward, one back) comes from the mind's fear-driven retreat to the familiar -> yet each forward effort reinforces truth -> and each step of the gradual awakening is swift in the measure of eternity
