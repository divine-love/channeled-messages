# Chain: Prayer Soul Longing

> True prayer is the longing of the soul, not the recitation of the mind

**The argument it traces:** Prayer is the foundation and anchor of the Divine Love path -> but words alone are not prayer; the sincere longing of the soul is what prayer essentially is -> repetition without feeling elicits no real response -> it is soul-longing that activates God's answer (the Law of Activation) -> as Divine Love accumulates, prayer becomes easier and the conscious knowing of God deepens
