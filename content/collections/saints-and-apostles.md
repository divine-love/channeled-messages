# The Saints & Apostles Speak

Messages from the biblical apostles and saints speaking directly from their perspective in spirit — Andrew, James, John the Beloved, Peter, Matthew, Mark, Luke, Francis of Assisi, and others. These spirits offer personal testimony, teaching, and encouragement grounded in their own experience of Divine Love.

---

| Message | Date | Description |
|---|---|---|
| [The Winds of Change Blow Upon This World](../messages/2015/07/2015-07-25-af-john-the-beloved.md) | 2015‑07‑25 | John the Beloved delivers a prophetic message declaring that God's Breath is blowing upon the world, bringing great change including climate shifts, rising oceans, and the crumbling of mountains. He calls the gathering to align with God's Will, open their souls to His Love, and be prepared to teach others the way through the coming tumult to peace and stability. |
| [Gathering the Souls Under the Wings of Love](../messages/2015/08/2015-08-10-af-john-the-beloved.md) | 2015‑08‑10 | John the Beloved prepares the gathering for an upcoming retreat, assuring them that God has purpose for every soul who will come together. He promises that Divine Love will pour into their souls in great abundance, dissolving doubt and fear, and that the gathering will send waves of love, healing, and change into both the physical and spirit worlds. |
