# The Saints & Apostles Speak

Messages from the biblical apostles and saints speaking directly from their perspective in spirit — Andrew, James, John the Beloved, Peter, Matthew, Mark, Luke, Francis of Assisi, and others. These spirits offer personal testimony, teaching, and encouragement grounded in their own experience of Divine Love.

|---|---|---|

He calls the gathering to align with God's Will, open their souls to His Love, and be prepared to teach others the way through the coming tumult to peace and stability. |
| [Gathering the Souls Under the Wings of Love](../messages/2015/08/2015-08-10-af-john-the-beloved.md) | 2015‑08‑10 | John the Beloved prepares the gathering for an upcoming retreat, assuring them that God has purpose for every soul who will come together. | [The Angels Come to Bless a Retreat Gathering in Gibsons](../messages/2015/08/2015-08-17-af-confucius-2.md) | 2015‑08‑17 | Six Celestial spirits speak in sequence to bless the August 2015 Gibsons Retreat gathering — Confucius, Mary, John the Beloved, Peter, Goldie, and Jesus — each offering their love, support, and encouragement to carry the truth of Divine Love boldly into the world. |
| [The Power of the Love Bonds Shared by Those Who Pray for God's Love Together](../messages/2015/08/2015-08-17-af-isaiah.md) | 2015‑08‑17 | Isaiah and Jesus close the August 2015 Gibsons Retreat with an affirmation that those who have gathered are now bonded for eternity, their souls connected in a shared mission. They encourage the group to hold fast to their commitments, commune with God through their souls, and walk as emissaries of Divine Love in the world. |
| [Drink Deep These Living Waters](../messages/2015/08/2015-08-17-af-john-the-beloved.md) | 2015‑08‑17 | John the Beloved blesses the gathering as angels crowd around to help build the light within their midst, inviting the souls present to drink deeply of God's Living Waters and raise themselves from the human condition into the Divine Presence. |
