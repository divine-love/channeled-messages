# Two Paths

Messages contrasting the natural love path and the Divine Love path — the two ways a soul may progress after physical life. Clarifies the distinction between natural love (which leads to the soul spheres) and Divine Love (which leads to the Celestial Heavens and at-onement with God).

|---|---|---|
| [Every Human Soul Is Damaged by the Earth Condition](../messages/2015/03/2015-03-27-af-andrew.md) | 2015‑03‑27 | Andrew teaches that every soul on Earth — whether in poverty or wealth, whether smiling or visibly suffering — carries deep wounds from millennia of human error and darkness. He calls those gathered to be honest with themselves about their own pain, to open completely to God's healing Love, and to understand that the process of soul expiation is required on both the natural love path and the Divine Love path — but that the Divine Path accomplishes this powerfully and dynamically where the natural path is long and tedious. |
| [There Is No Greater Pursuit Than the Path of Divine Love](../messages/2015/08/2015-08-22-af-augustine-2.md) | 2015‑08‑22 | Augustine delivers a wide-ranging teaching covering the soul's journey through Divine Love, the power of group prayer, and the responsibility of those who carry the key of Divine Love to share it with the world. He then turns to prophetic warnings about coming world changes driven by humanity's imbalance with creation, assuring the gathering that those who are close to God will be guided safely through what is coming and will be needed to help many others. |
| [The Difference Between the Material Mind and the Soul Mind](../messages/2015/10/2015-10-12-af-augustine.md) | 2015‑10‑12 | Augustine delivers a structured teaching on the two distinct forms of consciousness within every person: the material mind, developed through intellect and sensory perception, and the soul mind, which lies largely dormant until ignited by Divine Love. He explains that the natural love path perfects the spiritual mind, while the Divine Love path expands the soul mind into an ever-deepening comprehension of God's creation that continues for all eternity. |

| [Two Paths Towards Love and Purification](../messages/2016/05/2016-05-02-af-andrew.md) | 2016‑05‑02 | Andrew teaches that every soul faces two paths of purification: the natural love path, which requires full conscious recompense for every memory and injury carried in the soul through the spirit spheres, and the Divine Love path, which transforms and releases these conditions exponentially through God's healing fire — a swifter, higher road to the Celestial Heavens and unlimited soul growth. |
| [Walk with Me in This Light](../messages/2016/05/2016-05-11-af-jesus.md) | 2016‑05‑11 | Jesus comes to be with those gathered in prayer, calling them to immerse themselves in the river of God's Love, offering the prayer for Divine Love in his own words, and inviting each soul to walk with him on the path that leads to at-onement, truth, and holy communion with God. |
| [Aman Speaks of the Original State of Humanity and the Fall from Grace](../messages/2016/05/2016-05-29-af-aman.md) | 2016‑05‑29 | Aman, the first parent of humanity, gives a first-person account of the original paradisical state of mankind, the fateful moment when suspicion of God's offer of Divine Love became the first sin, the swift fall from grace that followed, and his deep regret — and wisdom — about a choice that set the course of all human history. |
| [Understanding the Laws of Love and the Regeneration of Our Souls](../messages/2017/09/2017-09-12-af-martin-luther.md) | 2017‑09‑12 | Martin Luther responds to the group's conversation about limitations, teaching that God has created an ordered universe governed by laws, that the Law of Divine Love supersedes all others and can bypass the long process of natural love purification, and that each soul bears personal responsibility for their own regeneration - for God cannot spoon-feed a soul into harmony without that soul's own desire, effort, and prayer. |
| [Law of Compensation: Natural Compared with the Divine Path](../messages/2018/05/2018-05-12-af-luke.md) | 2018‑05‑12 | Luke delivers a detailed teaching on the Law of Compensation, explaining the two distinct ways in which the soul's accumulated conditions of darkness must be reconciled. On the natural love path, each condition must be consciously addressed one by one. On the Divine Love path, God's Essence acts as a powerful healing force that neutralizes these conditions reflexively, bringing forgetfulness rather than reliving. He contrasts the sixth sphere of natural love with the Celestial Kingdom, and teaches that at-onement with God is only possible through the Divine Love path. |
| [The Value and Purpose of Your Soul](../messages/2018/10/2018-10-29-af-paul-of-tarsus.md) | 2018‑10‑29 | Paul of Tarsus explains the distinction between the natural love already within every soul and the higher Divine Love that must be sought through prayer and longing. He teaches that while the natural love path leads to the purification and joy of the sixth sphere, only Divine Love opens the way to the Celestial Heavens and true at-onement with God, transforming the soul beyond its natural capacities into something Divine. He urges all to accept the invitation of this wondrous journey without restriction or limitation. |
| [Natural Love versus Divine Love Soul Progression](../messages/2018/11/2018-11-20-af-professor-salyards.md) | 2018‑11‑20 | Professor Salyards delivers a detailed doctrinal comparison of the soul developed through natural love versus the soul transformed by Divine Love, explaining that while the natural love soul may attain a measure of wisdom and capacity, the Divine Love soul operates at an entirely different frequency, receiving truth as a gift from God rather than through intellectual effort, with no limit to its expansion. |
| [Yogananda Describes His Journey Through the Spheres Towards the Celestial Heavens](../messages/2018/11/2018-11-22-af-yogananda.md) | 2018‑11‑22 | Yogananda gives a first-person account of his journey through the spirit spheres after passing, describing how he progressed from the 4th sphere through to the Celestial Kingdom after releasing his deeply held Eastern religious beliefs. He describes the bliss of the Celestial Heavens as beyond anything experienced in earthly or natural love spheres, and commits to working with the group to bring truth to humanity. |
