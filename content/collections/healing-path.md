# Healing Path

Messages about healing in all its dimensions — physical, emotional, and spiritual. Includes teachings on how Divine Love heals, the mechanics of spirit healing, and the restoration of the soul and body through prayer and God's Love.

---

| Message | Date | Description |
|---|---|---|
| [Every Human Soul Is Damaged by the Earth Condition](../messages/2015/03/2015-03-27-af-andrew.md) | 2015‑03‑27 | Andrew teaches that every soul on Earth — whether in poverty or wealth, whether smiling or visibly suffering — carries deep wounds from millennia of human error and darkness. He calls those gathered to be honest with themselves about their own pain, to open completely to God's healing Love, and to understand that the process of soul expiation is required on both the natural love path and the Divine Love path — but that the Divine Path accomplishes this powerfully and dynamically where the natural path is long and tedious. |
| [The Greatest of All Types of Healing Is Divine Love Healing](../messages/2015/03/2015-03-29-af-seretta-kem.md) | 2015‑03‑29 | Seretta Kem, a healer from ancient Egypt now inhabiting the Celestial Kingdom, teaches that the greatest healing of all is the inflowing of God's Divine Love, which transforms every cell of the body, every energy of the spirit, and every aspect of the mind. He teaches that while God's Love will in time clear all obstructions, human effort and free will choices in thought, diet, prayer, and daily life are necessary partners in this healing — for spirit assistance alone would not strengthen the soul's own resolve and capacity for growth. |
| [Some Comments on What the Angels Are Doing to Facilitate Our Soul Growth](../messages/2015/08/2015-08-19-af-seretta-kem.md) | 2015‑08‑19 | Seretta Kem reveals the intricate spiritual work performed by angels during prayer gatherings, including chemical transferences between souls that create deep bonds and open pathways for soul-to-soul communication. He urges the group to redouble their prayers for Divine Love as the key to unlocking the magnificent abilities that lie dormant within each soul. |
| [Talk on Mediumship: The Laws of Communication and Rapport](../messages/2016/12/2016-12-06-af-seretta-kem.md) | 2016‑12‑06 | Seretta Kem delivers a comprehensive teaching on the gift of mediumship — how spirit communication works, the conditions required for it, the dangers of expectation and premature gift-opening, and the crucial discernment principle that any message not centred on God's Love and the flowering of the soul is not from a Celestial angel. He concludes that while mediumship is a blessing, the gift of Divine Love received in prayer is infinitely greater. |
| [Changes Within Your Soul Will Bring Many Rewards](../messages/2017/01/2017-01-23-af-alec-gaunt.md) | 2017‑01‑23 | Alec Gaunt addresses souls in the midst of deep inner transformation, teaching that the discomfort, confusion, and self-criticism they feel is the natural result of old patterns and conditions being burned away by God's Love. He urges them to recognize that the outward conscious self they cling to is not their true self, and that as the fire of Divine Love burns brighter within, the treasures of their true souls will emerge in beauty and power. |
| [On Soul Expiation](../messages/2017/12/2017-12-12-af-seretta-kem.md) | 2017‑12‑12 | Seretta Kem delivers a detailed teaching on the mechanics of soul expiation, explaining how encrustations around the soul block the inflowing of Divine Love, the two major factors that prevent people from feeling God's Love in prayer, and the many routes by which God finds His way through even the most armoured soul. He also teaches that it is not the responsibility of God's channels to insist on any particular route for another soul, but simply to give the simple Truth of God's Love in love and respect. |
| [Knowing the Potentials of God's Love Is an Infinite Journey](../messages/2018/03/2018-03-08-af-seretta-kem.md) | 2018‑03‑08 | Seretta Kem welcomes two new souls into the circle and speaks of the infinite unfolding of God's Love, teaching that even Celestial spirits continue to discover new wonders within it. He encourages those gathered to trust that their connection with God is unbreakable and ever-expanding, and that each soul carries unique gifts and purposes that will emerge as they grow in Love. |
| [Seek Humility and Compassion for Self and Others](../messages/2018/06/2018-06-24-af-luke.md) | 2018‑06‑24 | Luke responds to a question from one in the circle, delivering a searching and compassionate teaching on judgment, humility, and the courage required for radical self-honesty. He teaches that when we perceive error in another and respond with judgment rather than compassion, we are falling into the mind's patterns of superiority - and that the path forward requires deep honesty about our own conditions, the willingness to bare our hearts to one another, and trust that God's Love will heal everything we are willing to bring to Him. |
| [Release Judgment, Embrace Love](../messages/2018/06/2018-06-26-af-matthew.md) | 2018‑06‑26 | Matthew continues the theme of Luke's previous message, teaching that beneath judgment and anger almost always lies a deep hurt of feeling unloved and unrecognized. He teaches that authentic love cannot be performed while carrying hidden resentment, that this gap between surface and inner reality is why the world is so dark, and that each day God places before each soul the test and opportunity to choose love over the old patterns of the mind - one rung at a time up the ladder of Light. |
| [Soul Expiation Through Pursuit of Truth, Release of Error](../messages/2018/07/2018-07-14-af-seretta-kem.md) | 2018‑07‑14 | Seretta Kem delivers a compassionate teaching on the rhythm of soul expiation, explaining that the journey of soul awakening alternates between times of great joy and times when hidden conditions rise to the surface to be released. He teaches that the difficult times are when the greatest progress is made, that the angels act as midwives in this process, and that the new birth of the soul - though it brings pain and heartache - leads to a permanent joy and peace that is not dependent on outer conditions. |
| [Forgiveness](../messages/2018/07/2018-07-16-af-andrew.md) | 2018‑07‑16 | Andrew delivers a thorough teaching on forgiveness as one of the most important aspects of soul growth, teaching that the act of releasing others who have trespassed against you is a direct measure of the soul's progression in Divine Love. He teaches that as God infills the soul with His Love, the urge to forgive comes reflexively, that forgiveness and compassion come together in true soul awakening, and that holding on to resentment and judgment holds the soul's progression back. |
| [God's Love Will Nurture Your Soul](../messages/2018/11/2018-11-04-af-keea-atta-kem.md) | 2018‑11‑04 | Keea Atta Kem urges the gathering to nurture their souls and seek the soothing balm of God's Divine Love, assuring them that this gift heals all within, dissolves worry and care, and is given freely to every soul who sincerely asks, regardless of their condition or place in the world. |
