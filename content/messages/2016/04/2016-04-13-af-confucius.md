God bless you, precious souls. I am Confucius. Yes, I do reside within the kingdom of God’s Love, and I wish to address you fledgling birds to encourage you to spread your wings to fly, to soar to the Heavenly Father and find peace and joy in this place of Love. Yes, beloved beautiful birds, you have wings. Your souls have wings! You must spread these wings, open yourselves to this Gift of Love and allow God to Touch you deeply so that you may know of His Love for you, and that you may know that your souls may soar within this Love. Precious souls, open your wings and fly to God.

The breath of God will bring you close to Him, God bless you.
