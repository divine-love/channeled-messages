God bless you my beautiful flowers. To be with the birds and the trees and the green grass especially when you are feeling upset and vexed, go to the ground, let God’s beautiful Earth draw from you those feelings that disturb you, for the healing is there in the ground, in the trees and the flowers, the green. Allow God to draw from you that which holds you.

I am Goldie and I will be with you when you pray, when you sit in the green grass, when you contemplate the bees and the flowers, the birds and all the beauty around you, beloveds. Be with God in the garden and I will be there bringing joy.

God bless you, beloveds. God be with you wherever you are. Goldie loves you.
