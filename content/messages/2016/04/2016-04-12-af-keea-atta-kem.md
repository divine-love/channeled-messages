God bless you, dear souls. I have come to speak to you on receiving guidance.

Yes, you have received much in the way of messages from the angels as you come together in your prayers and this has been intended and we in spirit are very pleased that you have received these messages willingly and with great excitement. And this is but one way that we communicate with you who desire to be close to God through His Love.

We are always there to help to guide you, my beloveds. And though you cannot perceive us with your five senses, we do indeed have an influence and bearing upon your thoughts. We come in a different way, making a rapport with your minds and your souls. And we help you as you think and clarify these verbal messages and you consider how they are relevant to your particular life. We are with you as you read them and we are with all who read them and help to impress upon you the importance of any particular message, whether they be to inspire prayer and connection with God or give some details about the laws of God’s Creation and laws of God’s Love. All of it is a training, an education, a direction to your lives. All of it is a gift from God. And many messages will be given in the future, not only through this instrument but also others. The world will be flooded with the Truth of Love. The world will have much opportunity to read, to know the great privilege of God’s Love. This will be given in many ways. You will all be inspired to speak and to write, to pray and to collect many souls together into Circles of Light and we will be with you in powerful ways. For as you grow in the Love so our rapport, our presence will be felt in a greater way, in a clearer way in which the Love that we carry that is the Love of God will feel more tangible and those who are ignorant of this Love, yet yearning for it, will feel the power of it within your circles. And you may share with them these messages of Love, to help educate them. And there are many sources of these Truths that you may use, and we would encourage you to do so, to help educate those who are open to these Truths in their minds, in their souls.

Yes, much guidance will be given and as you embark upon your missions, your travels, your efforts to reach souls, you will be guided and you will not be confused or wonder of your next steps. They will be clear as you continue to forge the rapport between us and yourselves, between your souls and God. All will become clear, like a lens that is out of focus but will become more focused as your souls develop in the Love. And you will hear and feel and know the rightness of your steps, the truthfulness of your words and you will feel the flow of God’s Love in powerful ways and see the manifestations of His blessings for others, as you pray for them, as you embrace, as you enfold the children in Love.

You walk the Path Divine, my beloveds. Your feet are planted firmly upon this Path. What you carry is a great treasure of Truth. Who you are is a great and wondrous beacon of Light. How you act in this world must be in alignment with God’s Will and with this combination of attributes of Light, of a willingness to be guided, and you will change the world, my beloveds. You will bring the beginnings of change. You will set a fire to the world, a beautiful cleansing fire that will bring all that is in error to ashes and dust, which will encourage the new growth, the new world which will bring more harmony and peace and enlightenment to all who inherit this world, all the souls who will be born into this world. The children, their children and many generations to come will be influenced by those who are willing to carry the flame of Truth and express this through God’s guiding hand to be a part of God’s great plan for the salvation of mankind and the healing of this world.

Yes, we put in a great effort to communicate with you in many ways, for the time grows short and there is a great effort to be made and wondrous work ahead for those who are willing, for those who walk serving God, allowing Him to guide you and direct you and carry you in the flow of His Love. It is time to put aside your fears, your judgments, your pain and all that which is in error. We are giving you these moments to do so. And you must pray fervently to God to take your burdens from you, to replace them with joy and Love, wisdom and Truth, to give you the strength that you require to carry the flame of Truth into this world. The time grows short, my beloveds, it is time to set aside those childish things and stand tall in Love and Truth, harmony and service. We will guide you. God will guide you. Your souls will inform you and you will know a deep harmony, a wondrous joy, and the powerful flow of Love in your lives and in your beings.

Beloved souls, you are so loved and God’s tender care envelops you always. Allow yourselves to be embraced in every way and to release all those things within you that hold you back from this wondrous embrace from God. Open your souls wide to His Love, no hesitations, just acceptance and longing and faith and trust.

I love you, beloveds. I am Keea and I am with you in Love and will continue to serve our Heavenly Father in ways that I am directed to serve you and to work with you, to influence and guide you, as will many in our side of life who are with you. You are attended to by many. A great Light is being built. A great effort is being made and you will continue to see God’s unfolding miracle working within your lives. God bless you, beloved souls. God bless you in Love. God bless you, for you are loved and God’s Love shall always be yours. God bless you, beloveds. I am Keea.
