Yes, my father-in-law is very pleased that you continue to persevere under difficult conditions which try to thwart your efforts to work in this homeland, in this place, so in need of your presence to bring God’s presence, for God can come more powerfully through a soul who is attuned to God. And this you must demonstrate when you all walk through this threshold and pray together. Prepare yourselves for what is to come, for the efforts you make in those far off lands is very important at this time and will lead to other events unfolding, events that will usher greater opportunities for all of you to do God’s work. So I urge you not to take your efforts lightly. I urge you to redouble your prayers and to focus on doing God’s Will, to put aside your concerns, to be strong and humble in faith, to walk with assurity that God guides your steps to be that bright and beautiful channel of Love.

Do not be argumentative with those who would challenge you but smile and love that person who feels the power of your presence and is afraid and challenged. And so it will be as you continue to venture out into the world. So it will be. Many will look upon you and feel a sense of fear and confusion, even anger. This is the power of love, my beloveds, for it challenges darkness, it challenges the minds of so many. And you in your presence offer an invitation to all you meet to go to your soul, to go to God in this way, to be open to His blessings of Love.

Yes, it is not just what you say, my beloveds, it is what you are and how you are in the world and you must put aside any doubts or fears but to be as good and strong of faith that all will unfold in God’s Will and timing. And it is but for you to listen and to act when you are invited to act and to do in the world.

Love manifests in many ways. Do not judge what is or what are acts of love, beloveds. Every breath when you are in God’s Grace is an act of love. Every thought you have, prayer you make, decision you carry through that is done in love is beautiful and that act may be ostentatious or very simple. It is what is behind those actions, what motivates you. Are you following God’s Will? Can you with trust and faith enact God’s Will in everything you do? For God is waiting for you to be in alignment with His Will, to walk in this flow. And you will know this when you are in it for you have experienced this many times in the grace of God, in the flow of Love, in that beautiful place where you feel harmonious, and you feel that all negativity cannot touch you, but there is joy, and peace and a sense of knowing, of wisdom of what comes in the following steps of your efforts, soon to be in this flow and grace and you will not falter. And those who would thwart you will fall away.

The world is a difficult place, but you are to be in the world but not of the world. You are to be something else, a harbinger of Love. And in this only you will know what that is, how it will manifest, how you must be in the world. For each soul is unique and each soul will manifest God’s Will and this great Grace in unique and beautiful ways. Do not look to another to tell you what this is. Look within yourself and ask God, “show me how to manifest who I truly am, what You have created in me and help me to love and reinforce this beautiful manifestation of myself in the world.” And God will show you the way, will teach you the ways of Love, will help you to release all that which may hold you back and weigh you down. No, my beloveds, you are meant to work in this world, walk in this world in joy and freedom, in that great wondrous river of God’s Love. You feel it. You are barely in it and you wade ever deeper with each prayer, each day. Soon it will carry you and you will feel the power and the wonderment and you will trust in its flow to carry you where you are meant to be. Beloved souls, do not resist but let go. Let go.

God bless you. I am Faith and I am with you often and I encourage you to continue in your efforts to serve God in loving ways and in this all dilemmas will be solved, all questions will be answered, all impediments will fall away. God bless you, your friend, your dear friend, Faith is with you in all that you do for the glory of God. God bless you.
