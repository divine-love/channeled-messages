Dear, beloved souls I come, I am Jesus. I come to bless you in your efforts and I come to bring you a gift that will assist you in your efforts to break down those barriers to your work in this world, and I come because I love you all, my brothers and sisters, I love you all.

And my sister, you and I come from the same faith and I love you and we bring love into this world through the eyes of our understanding of the world. Yes, it is important to see with God’s eyes, to know with God’s knowing, to love with God’s Heart of Love and this understanding will come to you all, my beloveds, sooner than you may think. It is so close. It is so very close. And though you struggle with your minds so heavy with so many memories and thoughts and yet your souls are growing and healing your very being in so many ways, allow God to take you to that place of knowing and joyful understanding where revelations come and understanding blossoms without the struggle of the mind but the knowing of the soul.

Yes, my beloveds, it is for you to step into this dawning awareness, this growing pulsating Light within you and to be in that place, to be with God that you will walk your destined path as God’s instruments in this world. And you will walk in this world in a quiet way but the power of this will be unmistakeable and will cause ripples of change and many souls will respond to your touch, your smile and your simple words of love as you embrace all you meet and be God’s emissary in the world.

I am with you in your efforts, I am with you in my heart for my heart and your hearts beat as one in this great flow of Love. God bless you, beloveds. God bless you. I love you. I love you.
