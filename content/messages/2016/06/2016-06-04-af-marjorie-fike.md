…I have come to relay a message from your mother. She wants you to know that she is happy and the happiness that you so wish for her she now has in a life filled with beauty and creativity and joy. She continues to progress in the life of spirit and enjoys every moment of her life and she wants for you to have this life of joy too, to be free from your pain and yes she has regrets for what has happened in your childhood and she lacked the strength to curtail these difficulties but she wants you to know that her love for you has always been and will always be strong, and she prays for your healing and for your life to be a good life, as do many.

And for you, my beloved, beloved niece, I have a great love bond with you as you know and many pray for you. You are loved. You are loved and you are beautiful in so many ways. Never forget this, my beloved, dear, never forget how beautiful you are. And it is that beauty and all your gifts and talents that will bring Light to your life and resolution to your dilemmas and difficulties. Know that you have what you require to make a good life for yourself and if you open your soul to God’s Love it will only magnify the beauty that is within you and bring you the wisdom to make those choices for Light and bring you the healing that will help you to release that which holds you back.

My beloved, continue on your quest for healing. When you nurtured your brother and your mother you created a great Light within you but you also delayed the day of reckoning to deal with these pains so deep within your soul and now is your time for healing, to release that which is within you that is a heavy burden. And you must see this, my beloved, how this is such an important time in your life for your healing and this will have profound effects upon your future and create something beautiful.

Your beloved brother continues to grow. He is very hungry for knowledge and to understand the true values in life. And he does realise that the life in this world is a small yet significant stepping stone to many, much more life. There is much more life in spirit and many more experiences to be had as you journey towards God. For every soul journeys towards God in their own way at their own pace. And you must see this for your loved ones for though they are not with you they are still alive, experiencing life in a different way. And so all of us, all of your family continues on this journey of life whether it is here in spirit or in the flesh, it does not matter really, truly. What matters is living life in a way that you are not asleep but awake and you experience all the goodness that life has to offer which you know the source of life and the source of all Love and goodness for this shall inform so much of what you do and what you think, who you are. We all want the best for you, beloved [name-removed], and the best, the best is coming. The greatest part of your life on Earth is coming and it will be beautiful. Just a bit more to overcome and you will know, you will look back and see how this pain was worth the reward that is coming. Greater life awaits you. Greater life here in this world and in spirit awaits you as you shed the weight of your grief, the weight of your lack of self love. It is all part of the healing process. You must have faith in this and see the power and the gift that it is.

I love you, my dear and beloved, I love you as I love all of you here, my family. Thank you for allowing me to come to bring my message to you from your mother and your brother who love you so dearly. They are always close, beloved, we are always close and you must release your grief and be joyful for those who continue to live in a place of Light and freedom and joy. My journey started out poorly but I am now gloriously happy and I know the wonders of my Heavenly Father’s Love and I must tell you that it is wonderful and I am supremely happy and continue to progress in this Love and I want you to do the same, my beloveds. Seek ye God. Seek this Love, so much can come from this. So much will come from this. Beloveds, I love you and I am your mother and your aunt and I will always be close. I love you so. God bless you.
