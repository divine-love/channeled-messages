It's Goldie. Do you not see all the new life springing up from the ground, and do you not relate this to your soul where there is new life springing from your souls? The joy of this, to acknowledge that God has brought new life into you, bringing beautiful things springing from you. And you will continue to discover all the beautiful flowers that grow from your soul and others will see this too, beloveds. Continue to allow God to bring His beautiful Love within you and that Love is like the warmth of the sun bringing new and beautiful things to life.

You are blessed, beautiful souls, and you are beautiful in many, many ways. But you still do not see a way, the highest road, and your souls have grown [?] and many wondrous things will spring from you, gifts that God has put within you waiting for that new Light and His Love.

Beautiful souls, be joyful, celebrate the new life, celebrate the new you and all the beauty that is in life that surrounds you and nourishes you.

God bless you. Your friend loves you dearly and is happy to see the flowers blooming and all the flowers in this circle in bloom. God bless you, beloveds, I am Goldie.
