…and you must bring an open heart and your glorious Light, your presence with God in your prayers and your essence that flows to those who are longing and seeking. It is in this wordless way, this loving Light embracing of another soul, your prayers for God's presence and for us to assist in all that will unfold that your day will be successful and those who come are innocent, are humble souls who seek will be given the Truth and Love, will be given peace and healing and in so doing you do God's Will, you will complete your mission to touch the souls who hunger as God touches them through you, and you allow this in all its fullness and power, you allow this to be together in loving harmony, beloveds. For precious in the eyes of God are those who desire to do His Will in the flow of His Love and will be touched as they touch others, will walk in Light as they spread Light, will be gifted many blessings as they desire to be a channel of Love in the world.

Blessed are you, my beloveds, for you eagerly seek to be that channel of Love and you eagerly seek the Truth, you eagerly beseech God for His Love to flow into your souls. And so it shall be, the blessing, the benediction upon each one of you, will flow upon you in great power and beauty and will change you irrevocably, will set you upon the eternal Path of at-onement with God. Blessed are you, my beloveds, for you seek this, you hunger for this, you are eager for this, and your desires, your prayers, and your actions will be answered by God and He will implant within you such wisdom and Love and joy and peace, that you cannot but be that Light in the world.

Beautiful souls, walk in Light and be in this simple way in service to Love and enact God's Will, making changes reverberating throughout this world. It will change this world.

Beloved souls, God bless you in your efforts to love. I am Jesus and I will be with you, beloveds, and a host of angels are surrounding you. There will be great blessings for you. Be at peace, you have all been guided together and there is great purpose in this. And this day is but one manifestation of the many days and many blessings. God bless you. I am with you. God bless you.
