May God's peace permeate each of you, my beloveds, and rest as a Light within your souls bringing you great comfort and strength. Beloveds …. and I will be with you in your prayers and you each have a mission to fulfill in your efforts when you visit the beloved soul and we pray with … bring comfort, bring Light, embrace all and be a channel of God's Love. I will be with you in your prayers, I will be, as will many angels be with you as you pray in Love, embrace, and are together. Beloved souls, … this is God's Will… I am Mary…
