…Love. And my beloved son lights your way, as do many who reside within the Celestial Heavens.

Precious souls, you have been given a great Gift when you gathered together in prayer with the many souls who eagerly came to seek fellowship and Love and greater understanding of their souls. A special Gift was given to each one of you, my beloveds, and I urge you to seek, to know this and what this Gift entails, for it requires you, my beloveds, to go within your souls and know what resides there and know what God has given you and placed within you, for this Gift will bring greater understanding and strengthen you upon your journey and open you for what God intends for you to do in this world, my beloveds. There is such a great work ahead, awaiting your understanding and acceptance and true knowing.

Ask God to reveal this Gift to you in a conscious way. Let God work through you, my beloveds. Allow God to love you as deeply as you may let this in, this great Love of God, for as it pours within you it flushes away all that keeps you from this understanding and knowledge and brings you to that holy place of communion and truly knowing God and His Gifts that have been given to you, my beloveds.

Each soul here has a Light. Each soul here has a purpose, and each soul here has within them many Gifts which they are not yet aware of, which await the transformation of your consciousness to that of soul awareness and acting from that place of deep knowing and living from that place of deeply loving yourselves and all you meet.

So much awaits, you know this for we have told you this countless times. The journey never ends, my beloveds, it goes for all eternity and yes, so much awaits and we will continue to be with you, and pray for you, and love you, and guide you, and teach you. Our love is always with you. And God extends His deep and gracious care for each and every one of you, my beloveds. Accept all that is given, and in your daily lives continue to be that channel of Love in the world, for it is Love that will heal this world, Love that will bring the answers to all, Love that will bring harmony. God bless you, I am Mary and my love for you is deep, deep and strong. God bless you.
