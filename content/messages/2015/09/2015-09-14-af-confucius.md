God bless you. Yes, now is the time when God will draw many of you together from many different places in the world, many different cultures, and ideas, and perceptions of God, and He will draw you all together, my beloveds, for you are ready to open your hearts to many, to be accepting and loving and embracing all. This is important, my beloveds, to embrace all you meet, no matter their heritage, their attitudes, the color of their skin, or what social status from which they come. The very poor and the very rich and all in between are welcome in God's house in this Light, and you will have many opportunities, my beloveds, to invite them into the fold of this Light, this circle of Light.

And some of you will be traveling around this globe, encountering many different souls who are seeking. The differences are a reflection of the conditions of the mind, the material mind, but every soul longs for Love, every soul must be given this opportunity to be in prayer together, to receive this Love and you must do so as God's channels of Love, not judging but embracing, not in fear but in Love, acknowledging that in God's universe there is such diversity, and this is beautiful. This is beautiful, my beloveds, to embrace all the differences, all that is in your world, for every child is precious in God's eyes and you must see them in this way and in this way you will be God's channels of Love, you will do His work to bring all the children of the world together in Love and harmony, in acceptance and peace.

It is time to walk in this way, creating your Circles of Light as you progress on your life's path, my beloveds. Open your eyes, the eyes of your souls and see in this way the glorious creation of God in all its wonderment and beauty, in all its variety, and this is beautiful and each soul is precious, beloveds. I am Confucius and I love you, beloveds.
