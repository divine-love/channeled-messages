My students, I am Augustine, your teacher. You have asked for guidance regarding creating Centres of Light in the world. It is important to note that the doors will open to each of these places in harmony and when you find resistance, when it is difficult and convoluted and complex you must wait, continue to pray and to put your good intentions towards these efforts and do what is required to continue your connection and your Light within that place. And yes, there will be resistance to many of these efforts, for the negative forces of this world are continually trying to put out the Light and where there is fear and resistance there is an opening for this negativity, and you must persevere my students. Continue in your prayers, express your truth but above all, be loving and respectful and sensitive. For who can fight and oppose those who are loving and those who are strong in their Love and those who are willing to lay down their fears and their anger and take up the torch of Truth and Love?

In time all of those who resist and are angry and fearful will let go of these conditions and acknowledge God's Will. But it does require your persistence in prayer, in Love, and in effort. Do not display aggression but instead wisdom and patience and much prayer. For the power of prayer along with the power of Love can accomplish much in this world, it will overcome much and will bring harmony to many situations.

There are those of you here who are entrusted with forging the path to this effort of creating Centres of Light in the world, who are open to the guidance, who are open to God's Will in these regards. And when you feel you are not making headway, be assured that God's Will will be done and what appears difficult and possibly hopeless today will find an opening and hope and fulfillment in some day soon. Patience, my students, patience. Be at peace and know that as long as you are willing and you have the desire to be a part of this venture in the world, God will use you and find ways to bring about these efforts in constructive ways and in ways that will bring about the establishment of Centres of Light.

Be at peace and know that God's Will is with you and the awareness of this is within you and the power of this is all about you, will clear away the obstacles and bring about what is meant to be. God bless you, my students, your teacher Augustine is ever by your side, there to support, to bring love, to teach, to inspire, to work with you in many different ways. God bless you, my beloveds, Augustine loves you.
