I am Professor Salyards and some of you know me through the writings of James Padgett for I did many essays through him regarding the Truth of God's Love as I took my journey of discovery and prayer towards God and His Great Soul, and I wish to confirm what Solomon has shared with you.

True wisdom and an ability to understand the Truth can only be had through the soul and in order for this to be accomplished you must have the faculties of the soul ignited by God's Love. Your eyes must be opened in this way. The mind of the soul must become truly a part of you and your consciousness, and to accomplish this while on Earth, you become a great asset in teaching the Truths of God's Love in this world. Very few have truly been able to live their lives in this way and be channels of Truth in this way. It will be a remarkable accomplishment which many of you are on the verge of attaining in your lives. You must indeed redouble your efforts to pray for God's Love to open these faculties which allow you to see with deeper, greater, wider, more profound vision God's great creation, the laws of His creation, the laws of Love, the beauty that is all around you, the Light that is poured upon you and the Love that flows within you. To have this vision, my beloved, beautiful brothers and sisters, is a great key that will allow you to teach to the world these Truths in ways that will touch many souls, inspire many to seek and to understand, to follow you, and this gives you credibility and those around you will acknowledge your gifts for what you will say and share with others will be truly magnificent, surprising and inspiring.

Seek to open yourselves to the Truth in this way, ever seeking God's great blessing and knowing that you are upon the right Path to Truth. You will discover many treasures upon this Path and we walk with you. Yes, many walk with you, many you are unaware of and as this instrument opens and develops you will hear from us, many of us and we will encourage and teach you and show you the many secrets and mysteries of the universe as you are open to this. May the Light of Truth dawn within your consciousness, the power of Love inform all you do and the great blessing of peace and joy be your touchstone in your lives, my beloveds.

Your friend Professor Salyards is eager to assist you in all of this and I too love you and love all those who seek God's Love. We are truly brothers and sisters in this journey and effort. God bless you, God bless you all and good night.
