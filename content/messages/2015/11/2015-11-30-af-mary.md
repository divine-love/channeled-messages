God bless you, it is Mary and I love you, beautiful souls. And I love all my charges, all of these beautiful women who hold their Light up high in the world, who are beautiful in their nature, their loving ways, their beautiful souls, all that they give in the world. I love you all, my beloveds, and I am with you when you care for others, when you hold the children, when you love all that you encompass and who pass by. You are beautiful and this beauty is filled with Love and Grace.

Continue to be in this Light, my beautiful, beloved sisters. Continue to walk this Path of Grace. Continue to be close to God and to acknowledge the beauty within yourselves that is a Light that draws so many and I will continue to walk with you, my beloved souls, as your sister, as your guardian angel, as your loving helper. For the Light that shines within you draws me to you as God places His Hand upon us all in this beautiful work, in this beautiful journey of Truth and Love, of service, of teaching, of loving, of healing, of bringing peace, of comforting, of embracing all souls who are close to you.

Beloveds, continue to do this work of Love and to seek the great Comforter, the great flow of Love that comes from God. You will be guided and you will see for yourselves what God has planned for you and your own inner strength and beauty will inform your lives and bring in greater beauty, greater Love, greater Light. Beloved souls, be with God and I will walk with you and be with you in prayer. I love you, I love you, beautiful souls. Your sister Mary is ever by your side and loves you dearly. God bless you.
