It is Jesus. Welcome to this oasis of Light, this Circle of Light where you open to receive the Father's benediction within your souls and you desire to walk this Path Divine into greater Light, fulfilling your souls' desires to be with God in a way that is complete at-onement, pure Grace and peace beyond understanding.

God's Grace is here, my beloveds. Open your souls, feel its inflowing bringing those waves of peace with his Love, those waves of Living Waters washing up against your soul, through you, into you, embracing you so that you may know, that you may truly know that God is with you, that God loves you, that God has His Hand upon you, my beloveds, for this is so and shall always be so. For God has gathered His little children together in this circle of Light so that you may truly feel His Touch together and know this great Gift as it opens and flourishes within your being carrying you above the conditions of this world to a place of purity and joy, peace and fulfillment.

Blessed souls, be at peace. God is with you. God loves you. God bless you. I am Jesus and I love you.
