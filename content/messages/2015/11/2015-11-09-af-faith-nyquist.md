Dear God, may you bless these earnest souls who come together from far and wide that have Your Love burning within their souls and desire to be Your channels of Love in this world. Bless them, keep them under Your cloak of protection with Your Loving Hand upon each beautiful soul in this circle and may Your Peace that passes all understanding be with them as they traverse this world, as they deal with the conditions in this world and help them to shed all that which is not in harmony with Your Love.

My beloved souls, I am Faith and I love you and I have come to pray with you and ease your concerns regarding the future endeavours which you have all played a part and all are a part of these efforts to create Centers of Light in the world. And those whom you must deal with and negotiate with to bring about God's Will in a material way in this world, bring about dilemmas and consternation and great questions. It is not easy to bring Light into this world, a world filled with the concepts and constructs of the material mind lacking in soul perceptions and wisdom.

Yes, my beloveds, you bring a different perspective, a more solid and loving perspective, and it is difficult for you to communicate this in a way that others will understand and trust your perspective, will feel the power of your faith and Love and obedience to God's Will. And when I walked the Earth, this was indeed my great challenge, to be heard, to be seen, to be acknowledged as I went about my Father's Will and work. This has always been the great challenge for those who take on this mantle and this work. It is difficult to bring clarity when there is so much that brings confusion and misunderstanding and the lack of faith. And I tell you, my beloveds, that we in spirit are working diligently to clear away the obstacles and will be with you in your efforts to do the same, to bring God's Will manifest in this world.

It is often a difficult task and may take circuitous routes but God's Will will prevail, it will prevail, my beloveds as long as you are willing to walk this road, to listen to your guidance, to be steadfast in the Truth, to trust in your Heavenly Father to allow what is meant to be manifest to be manifest in this world for we rely upon you, my beloveds. We rely upon you in many ways to be God's channels in the world.

And so, it is that we are with you always and we work diligently to clear the path for you, to show you the way, to inspire and influence you in your daily efforts to be a Light in this world. Yes, you all struggle, and yet, you see how God's Love prevails and will prevail. The power of this Love, the healing, the harmony and the peace. It envelops you, it cleanses you, it brings harmony and peace within you. And so, you will bring this to others in many parts of the world. It seems a difficult and complex task but, my beloveds, you must have faith that God will wrought what is meant to be, will bring this and as long as you are willing to walk with God you will be a part of it in relation to your faith and proportion to your faith, it will be powerful and dynamic and beautiful. For when God brings about His Will manifesting in this world it is always a beautiful thing where all involved are blessed, where there is harmony and healing and peace.

Continue in your efforts to bring about God's Will and we shall continue in our efforts to support you and to influence and to clear the way. Yes, your journey in this regard to place yourselves within that center of Light far off has not been easy. There have been setbacks and pitfalls and disappointments and anguishes, but as you see, you continue, you continue upon this road and you persist and this reflects your strength and your faith and your trust and I applaud your efforts, my beloveds. God's Will will be done, it will be done and you will be surprised at the insights and blessings and inspirations that will be given for you to accomplish this work and bring about a resolution for those who are in pain. You will bring about clarity for those who are confused, bring about insight for those who are narrow in their thinking and to reignite the enthusiasm and the faith and the power of God's Love for those who are drifting off this path. Yes, there is much work to do and to accomplish and you will continue, my beloveds, in your efforts to bring resolution and fulfillment of God's Will.

This is my encouragement to you; to persist, to pray, to long for the inspiration of what you require to bring this to fruition, this wonderful effort to maintain and make greater this Center of Light in the world. Beloveds, God is with you and may His Peace rest upon you and His Love find a deep inflowing into your souls. Beloved, beautiful souls God bless you. I am Faith and I love you.
