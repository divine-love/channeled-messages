God bless you, your teacher Augustine is with you in your morning prayers in your small gathering of Love.

Beloved souls, each of you has a great purpose and it is for you at this time as you walk on this Earth, as you contemplate this walk that is your life, to come to know your purpose, beloveds, and to ask God to reveal to you what it is that He placed you upon this world to do. For each has unique gifts and abilities and a deep purpose and you play a part in the plan for the salvation of mankind and the healing of this world, a great part and important role to play and when you discover this and when this grows and blossoms within you, becomes a conscious part of yourself, you will find a deep fulfillment and joy in this acknowledgement and the manifestation of this purposeful blessing in the world. For to know your purpose, my beloveds, and to express this, brings the deepest joy as it is magnified by the Heavenly Father's Love within you. And this is a great blessing.

Although each soul in the world is blessed with a special purpose, very few come to know this and express it. And even fewer are able to express this in the flow of God's Love amplified and made pure and more powerful by this Love. So you see, my students, you have been nurtured and protected and taught and blessed and readied for this. God has put His Hand upon you in this way in the hopes that you will choose to reveal to yourselves who you truly are, what lies deep within you, that beautiful treasure, that great purpose, and that you will not turn away, you will not deny yourselves this true self, true ability, and true Gift that lies within you.

And for some this brings a tinge of fear, a wonderment: "How will this change my life? How will this manifest in my life?" And I tell you, my beloveds, that each soul who is willing to work with God to be in the flow of God's Will - their lives will be harmonious, every aspect of their life will fall into place in beautiful ways. There will not be hurt and pain and devastation within your life, but joy and recognition and peace. Do not fear but embrace what it is God means for you to do, for in this comes great joy and in this comes great rewards both here and when you are ready to live in the world of spirit.

So, I ask you my beloveds, what legacy will you leave behind in your life? What will you accomplish? Not from the power of your own being but in a willingness to discover what God wishes for you to do and to listen and enact God's desire and His Will through you, amplified by your beautiful being, expressed uniquely, for God created you as a unique soul with unique Gifts and these Gifts await your acceptance, your acknowledgement and your expression.

And you will know not fear, you will not be shy to be who you truly are, you will be strong and immune from the negativity of this world. And the joy you will feel will compensate for all negativity that may be expressed towards you and those barbs, spears that are sent in your direction, will fall, fall away harmless and impotent. For within you will be that true knowledge, that great strength, that loving and wise perspective of the world and all those you encounter in it. And you will be able to love with a depth and a breadth, a power and a beauty that you cannot imagine at this time, but this will grow as you continue to nourish your souls, to know yourselves from that deep place, that deep place where you and God are with one another in Holy Communion, in peace and joy. For there is only Love, there is only Love.

Precious souls, continue in your prayers, in your efforts to reach God to open your souls to His blessing, His Divine Essence. There is no other greater pursuit. In this way all of what I speak will be revealed and expressed and come into your reality and you will be guided thus through your life. You already walk this Path firmly upon this life divine, this understanding and revealing of Truth, this coming to know what Love truly is, who God truly is, who you truly are. You continue to progress and we will continue to assist you in your progression and we will never leave you, my beloveds, and we have never left you, even those of you who felt abandoned have not been abandoned for God's Love continues to burn within each soul in this circle and shall continue for all eternity, my beloveds, all eternity. For the power of this Love within you will bring you to your at-onement with God as you walk your Path towards Light.

God bless you, my beloveds. Your teacher Augustine has a great, deep and abiding love for each one of you. Each beautiful soul in this circle. I love you, I watch over you and I guide you and you are God's Gift to me, to have this opportunity to be one of your guiding Lights. It is my blessing and I rejoice in this, beloved souls. I rejoice in this and I love you. God bless you.
