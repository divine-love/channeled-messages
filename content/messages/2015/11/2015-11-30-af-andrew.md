God bless you my beloveds, I am Andrew.

May the peace that passes all understanding may be yours, my beloved, dear souls, as you continue to gather together in prayer, as you seek to be ever closer to God, to be in His Light, in His Peace and as you open your souls to the inflowing of His Divine Essence. Beloved souls, you become bonded in this experience. You become a part of one another through sitting in this circle of Love and Light. And you begin to walk together, in unison, as you open yourselves in this way.

The longings of the soul, my beloveds, the longings of the soul must be listened to, and must be allowed to reach your Heavenly Father through your prayers and your intentions, your desires to be close to God. Do not place any obstructions in front of this relationship which will form a lasting foundation in your lives in this world and in the world to come. Beloved souls, bless you in your efforts, in your prayers, in your efforts to love one another.

And yes, many of you will work together to further the Truth of God's Love in the world. There is much that awaits you, my beloveds, a great deal of learning and growing within your souls, expressing and knowing your Gifts, developing your Gifts with the Love that flows within. Yes, you are changing, you are growing and you are shedding the old ways, being open to the new, being refreshed by the Touch of God as your minds begin to acquiesce to the desires of your souls. You will see new avenues laid out before you and new ways of thinking and being, of loving and growing, and this will allow you to make those connections and rapport with many souls in this world who seek as well, although they may not have the understandings that you have. They may have a different life experience, a different culture and language, but it will be the soul, one soul to another soul that will build the bridge, that will build the connection of Light between you. And this Light shall spread all around the world, my beloveds. These many connections that you will make will help strengthen your work, will help further God's plan for the salvation of mankind, one soul to another, another to another until the whole world is lit by Love.

Beloveds, is this not what you desire? To have peace in this world, to have respect and admiration and love, to acknowledge each soul in the world and for each soul to acknowledge each other soul and this is what will come of your work. It begins, God's Touch flows through you to others and will reach many, many souls in this world and many souls in the world of spirit.

Yes, there is a great work, a great unfolding of God's Will and Plan in the world and you begin to open your eyes to this, my beloveds, to see this wondrous Plan, to feel the power of it amongst yourselves. Though some of you may not have the understandings within your mind of this, you feel the recognition of your souls, a lightness, an opening, a feeling of being seen. Indeed you are seen, my beloveds, seen by your Heavenly Father, seen by the angels, seen by one another. For you are all Lights, all beautiful and all have a place in this beautiful growing network of Light which will encompass the world in time and change this place where you live, this world which has suffered and struggles so.

A great healing is happening, a great flow of Light is encompassing your world, touching many souls like a bell toll awakening, awakening many. That longing will turn their eyes to Truth, to Love and to Light, and you, my beloveds, so hold that rope that pulls the bell to ring, for God holds you in His Hand. This great chain, this great message is flowing out into the world through you and through others and you will all join together in this great effort to awaken the souls of mankind.

You begin this journey and you feel the pull of it, the power of it and the strength of it and we are joyed at your acknowledgment and recognition of this great wondrous work to bring healing and Love to mankind. And we will succeed, my beloveds, we will succeed. For the power of this, the beauty and the Light of this will push back the darkness and the negativity, the ignorance, fear, the resistance and the error. The power of this will push back all that stands in the way of every soul having the opportunity to lay down that which keeps them in the darkness and to enter into the Light.

Yes, the work begins and you are ready, many of you are ready and eager, and you will march forward under God's guidance and the flow of His Love. Beloved souls, continue to pray for the Father's Love to enter your souls, to be in preparation and readiness, alert to God's guidance within, and He will guide you upon your Path unique and beautiful. And He carries you ever forth, beloved souls. Be at peace and know that Love is the key to all and Love shall be your blessing ever more. God bless you, I am Andrew and I love you.
