It is Confucius, God bless you. There is a great network of Light being formed in the world, my beloveds. Many souls are being connected in this Light, this lattice of Light and you, my beloveds, as you pray are being used to formulate and to maintain this Light in the world. You assist in bringing greater connections that Love and blessings and Light may flow freely to those who are connected in this vast network blessed by God. And, it is important when you travel in the world you help to strengthen these bonds, this Light, and when you pray for others you also bring a stronger connection in your efforts.

Remember, beloveds, you do many things for God, on many different levels you serve God, and much of this you are not aware of. But it is important that you come together in Love and harmony, in prayerful beseeching of your Heavenly Father for His Love. For this very act brings many blessings to this world as you are a channel of His Love and Light for this world. Indeed, as time progresses and you progress within your soul you will see more of what I speak and understand it more clearly.

Continue in your efforts, continue in your prayers and include all those whom you know are a part of this vast network and you will glean more knowledge; conscious understanding of the many souls God is connecting to you, through you and all around you. This knowledge will come and many souls you will meet in precious time together as God sews each beautiful thread in this wonderful tapestry, this wonderful tapestry which you are forming together and creating something truly beautiful and important in this world.

Precious souls, be in God's Grace always and I will be with you in your efforts as will many, many angels of God. God bless you, beloved souls. God bless you, precious, precious, beautiful souls.
