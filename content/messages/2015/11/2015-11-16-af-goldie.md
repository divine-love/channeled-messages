.... and I am with you, especially when you feel the burdens I am with you to help lighten your spirit, I am with you and I love you. Do not dwell too long in your pain and your thoughts that go around and around. Ask for me to help you, my beloved, beautiful friends and I will. I will bring you laughter and joy. I will lighten your spirits and help you to see the funny side of life. For so much is folly and you worry so of such trivial things. And know that God will lift you above, bring you close, and love you up, and embrace you. Beloved souls, your friend Goldie is with you and I want you to laugh and be joyful. Life is too short. Bring laughter into your lives and love God with all your hearts and no burden will feel too heavy when you bring this into your heart. God bless you, beloveds, Goldie loves you. God bless you.
