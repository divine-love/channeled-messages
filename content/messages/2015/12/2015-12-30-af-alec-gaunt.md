Yes, you have accomplished much in this past year and you've taken new steps forward, made efforts to bring in many souls within your grasp to teach this Truth of God's Love where you might and wherever you go, and the coming year will bring many more opportunities, my friends, and challenges and blessings and it is for you at this time to redouble your efforts to pray for God's Love, to prepare yourselves for what is to come, for God will need you more than ever, my beloveds. There is so much work awaiting you and many doors will open for you, doors that you had not anticipated will be surprising and some you will be delighted with and others you will be very challenged by, but all will be opened by God for a reason, for each one of you continues to grow within your souls, each one of you requires these experiences, these opportunities to grow in your souls.

And when you are challenged do you not pray more, ask for guidance, seek within yourself the truth, express as best you can love and truth? And this is not to say that God presents these challenges to you, but indeed through the conditions of this world you inevitably stumble into situations which are challenging and you must see this as a blessing, as a Gift, for as I have said there is much to learn through adversity and I am not suggesting that the entire year will be in this Light for it will not. There will be many blessings, joyful interludes, experiences and communion with your Heavenly Father in a wonderful and deep way where you will feel more intensely the inflow of the Father's Love into your souls, you will see more clearly the Father's Will for you, you will come to understand in a deeper way who you truly are, to see your Gifts, what God has created, this wonderful being that is you will be more revealed to yourself and to those around you, for the Love will shine, the Love will bring that Light so that others may see, that you may see your true selves.

Continue in your prayers, beloved friends, and know that God's Hand is upon you firmly, you are firmly within His grasp and you will walk further upon the Path of service and growth within your souls and Love will shine within you, beloved, beautiful friends. God bless you, my friends, and keep you within this wondrous Love and Light and guide you further, deeper, more alive within yourselves, your souls expanded with Love, your expression more infused with Love, your actions informed by Love. God bless you. God bless you. I am Alec and I love you.
