I am John.

There is a powerful presence of God in this room tonight, a blessing given, a blessing that has drawn all of you here to this circle. And many of you are familiar with this condition of Love and some of you have an inkling of Its power and beauty. I urge you all to open yourselves to this Love, this powerful touch from God that is given as a great blessing and there are few places in this world where this blessing and this condition can be sustained in this intensity. Beloved souls, take full advantage of what is given. Allow this to touch every cell within your being and fill your soul to overflowing. Drink deep these Living waters and open yourselves wide, truly feel this Love my beloveds, it is yours, a Gift, a blessing, a touch. It is yours from God who created you, who loves you, who carries you in Light.

And you are deeply blessed. God bless you and I love you.
