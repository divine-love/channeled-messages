And may the Father's Love find lodgment within your souls and His Peace be a mantle upon your shoulders. I am Jesus and I am with you in your efforts and struggles to find Light within yourselves and within your lives. And all of us, whether we be in spirit in the Celestial Heavens and on Earth must go to our Heavenly Father for the source of Light and His blessings to propel us upon our Path of discovery and at-onement with God. You my brethren have a mighty struggle, as do all the souls upon this world, a great struggle for Light. And because you make this effort and you are committed to the Truth and you long for the Father's Love, you are blessed with the presence of Celestial angels to support you and guide you upon this Path and struggle. You are never alone and you are always loved. It is for you to discover this to truly know it and to take this within yourselves and accept who you truly are and how truly loved you are.

Walk with me upon this Path. Be in the Light of the Father's Love. Seek ye the Kingdom of Heaven and all things shall come unto you, my beloveds. All things shall come unto you. And you will find the joy and the freedom, the true strength within, the true knowing that comes with the Father's Touch of Love within you. Be at peace. Be at peace, beautiful souls, and know that you are my brothers and sisters who walk in peace and Love, who seek for the highest, who wish to be redeemed and reborn in Love. And so it shall be as you walk with me. You will walk that Path of rebirth of the soul, a great blessing from God that will make you fit to reside in the Kingdom of God, His Heaven of Love. Continue to pray, continue to long for this Gift. And as you pray and long, so shall the angels accompany you and pray with you and comfort you and protect you in the conditions of this world creating a place of Light, of refuge where you and God walk hand in hand in holy communion. Blessings to you, my beloveds. I love you dearly and you are my disciples of Truth. God bless you. God bless you.
