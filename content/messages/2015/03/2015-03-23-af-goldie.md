Bring the daffodils into the room and there will be joy and laughter amongst you. I will ensure this. My dear brother, why are you like a man who walks from one piece of ice to another in a river that flows so swiftly? Why do you not seek solid ground, and make life easier for yourself? God will open the doors and bring you to that place where you are not in jeopardy of falling into the cold waters. You must trust in your guidance, where you will be led and given reprieve and joyful, joyful life. But you must allow God to guide you. Ask for help, and I know that you do, help will be given.

Be clear in your thinking, my beloved brother, for you help many now, but in the future this will be magnified many times. But you must find stability so that you may grow in the Love in a more full way. Bless you, brother. How many smiles you put on faces, my brother, and bring comfort to so many. Bless you brother, for God loves you so and wants everything to make a life good and strong for you.

Goldie loves you and will be with you in your prayers with the souls who come together, and many, many will be with you. Continue to prepare the place and we will walk with you and sing with you as you work. Blessings to you, Goldie loves you.
