God bless you my beloved souls. I am Confucius.

I come to bless you in your prayers and though you come from many different types of diversities and perspectives, you are all members as a family of humanity and it is for you to discover your commonalities of understanding and to allow your souls to shine forth in this little gathering. Every gathering has great potential to bring spiritual truth, to bring great blessings to the soul and to the mind. Utilize your time together my beloveds in ways that will enlighten and lighten your path. Share with one another your longings and your thoughts, your prayers and your love and in this you will all receive blessings of healing and comfort and love.

There is much to discover my beloved, beautiful souls. Allow yourselves the opportunity to be open to this, to put aside your judgments and fears, to be as little children, bathing in these beautiful waters of God's Love enjoying the warmth of the Son, smiling and expressing the purity of life. You are all brothers and sisters together. Be radiant and joyful and I will be with you in your time together and come to speak to you once more.

God bless you beloved, beautiful souls. You are all precious in God's sight. God bless you.
