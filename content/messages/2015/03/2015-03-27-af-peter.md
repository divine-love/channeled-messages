I am Peter and I come to pray with you as you gather together and to be together in fellowship. Your Heavenly Father eagerly awaits your prayers and the abundance of His Love awaits its bestowal within your souls, an infinite Love and this my beloved children is what your souls long for, desire, crave, and this is what your souls were created for, to receive this Essence of God, to be ignited by this blessing, to feel the passion of Love burning bright within you, my beloveds. Open yourselves. Send your longings to God and God will respond in powerful ways, beyond your imaginings my beloveds, beyond your present understandings and beyond the limitations of your mind's awareness. God will carry you to places unimaginable and you will feel the peace that passes all understanding. You will know Him with such intimacy and awareness that these things cannot be described, but experienced from that deep place from within you. The angels surround you, my beloveds. They are with you in prayer and at this moment, you are receiving the blessing of His Love. Open wide, breathe deeply these waters of life. Allow them to flow into you and they will bring you upon that path to at-onement with your Creator.

The door opens. Are you ready? Do you desire this? Can you open yourselves up to this wondrous Light and blessing? You embark upon a short journey together and we will be with you as you pray, as you converse, as you open yourselves to one another and in this way you will forge the bonds of love which will last my brothers and sisters, for all eternity, a path of Love and discovery, service and joy. God has lit the path, ready for you to follow and we shall walk together, hand in hand, to that place of supreme peace, wondrous joy, and Love Divine.

I am Peter, and I love you, beloved souls. Walk with me. God bless you.
