Bless you beautiful souls, I am Augustine, once the bishop of Hippo while on Earth. I played a great part in developing the church of Christ, and in so doing I encouraged many erroneous beliefs and understandings of Jesus' true message. And so today I work with souls on the Earth and souls in spirit to bring this Truth and clarity, the Truth of God's Love, which is the message that Jesus brought to the world. This simple Truth of Love, the power of it, the redemption of it, the Truth that shall set you free my beloved souls.

For once I entered spirit and learned of this Truth, I prayed with all my earnestness to receive this Love within and indeed I found the power of this Love and I ascended through the spheres of spirit and entered into the Celestial Kingdom. The blessing of Divine Love is an incremental thing. Each step brings you closer to God, every dollop of this Love brings greater light to your soul and enlightenment to your being. Yes it takes time, it takes prayers, it takes longing. Prayers that go to your Heavenly Father at any time and in any place - these prayers can be sent forth on wings of desire to be with your God, your Creator. And when the awareness of this Love enters your being, when you truly know that God has placed something beautiful within you, then you begin the journey, a journey of transformation, of letting go of all of those things which are not in harmony with this Love, with God's creation, with what you are truly intended to be.

For each soul is unique and each soul will come to understand and express its unique qualities which are truly a gift from God. These will flourish, and expand within your being and you will have great joy and a sureness and security of knowing that you are truly loved by God and in this you will truly love yourselves. And in this you will love your brothers and sisters. For each is a beautiful and unique creation. Each is worthy of God's Love, each is beautiful. So some of you begin your journeys to find this Truth to truly know it, and others have been journeying for a great portion of their lives and it is indeed a blessing from God to have this fellowship and encouragement for one another to continue the journey and not fall back into the human conditions which in many ways deny the existence of God and do not foster the openness and awareness of the soul. Fellowship is important, it is a strength that can be shared. It is a way supporting one another for each has a unique perception and understanding and journey to take and in sharing this with one another, it opens new awarenesses, opportunities for growth, different perspectives which open insights into the mind's struggle to make sense of their lives in this world and the meaning that comes with life.

Beloved, beloved souls - love one another, share with one another your hearts, your dreams and your desires. Open yourselves and open yourselves to God's loving influence that you may enter into the stream of His Love which will carry you, embrace you, enfold you and bring you great peace, wondrous experiences and opening to the Truths of God's glorious creation. Do you not feel God's presence now, beloveds? Drink deep of His Love, drink deep. For each soul here thirsts for this, longs for this and it is at this time here in great abundance waiting to inflow into you and work its miraculous transformative power. And it will bring you joy and peace, comfort and wisdom.

Blessings to you beloveds, blessings from your teacher Augustine who loves you, God bless you.
