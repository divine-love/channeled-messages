I come, I am Jesus.

I come to bless this place and bless all who dwell within it, I come because you have called me by beseeching the Heavenly Father for His Love. For where His Love is, I am there also, I am His channel of Love, I am His messenger of Love. And I beseech you to follow me. To walk in the Light of this Love which is beyond all other Love, this transformative power that will bring all that you require and to desire to be in the Grace of God, to be with God, to know God, and to truly know yourselves.

Each soul here has received God's Love within, and each soul here has an invitation to follow this path of Love to bring this truth deep within and to live this Truth in their lives as fully as they can, as beautifully as can be expressed, as uniquely as is each soul.

Blessed are those who seek love, blessed are the peacemakers, blessed are those who desire to change this world to something better and purer and more infused with love and harmony. You are needed my beloveds, the world needs you to be examples of Love, harbingers of Love, channels of Love. Walk with me and I will light the way beyond the darkened confusions of the human condition to a place of clarity and true faith and an understanding of the Truth. I am with you, I am with you and I shall never leave you, beloveds. I am your brother, Jesus. God bless you. God bless you.
