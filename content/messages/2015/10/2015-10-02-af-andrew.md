I have been with you in your conversations, your discussions and your concerns and anxieties. Beloved daughter, the responsibility does not rest upon your shoulders, my beloved. It is shared amongst you and shared by the Angels and you have powerful allies in us and in God's Will. I would advise you to be at peace and to allow the inspiration to flow, for yes, this step is crucial but you do not know what is to come and the choices that will be made by those involved in the interplay and unfolding of this great blessing and gift from God. This is indeed meant to make deep change in this world, not only through this instrument but through others that will be drawn into this flow of Love and power. It is meant to be a part of this place so far away and as a testimony to the power of this great effort, there is also a response from the negative forces that would try to put out the light and this effort, to divert and cause disruption and disharmony.

Do not allow your anxieties, beloveds, to cloud your pure intentions which are to serve God Have faith in what God will provide along your path and inspire in your efforts and bless you as you move forward. Yes, it is a step by step process, carefully done, and with Love and all of you have what is required to bring about the desired outcome and those who are presently obstructing will fall away from the picture and the door will open my beloveds, in time, with patience and perseverance and faith. You have nothing to concern yourselves my beloveds other than to be in harmony with God's Will for as you are harmony, that which is meant to be in harmony will flow with ease and the insights will come, the inspiration, the correct words. Remember my beloveds, do not make things too complicated. Be direct and simple. You already know what is meant to be for this place. It is for you to communicate God's Will in clear and certain terms, in loving rebuke, to those who would thwart God's Will. In prayer and in service, you will bring about the correction of this path and those who would subvert God's Will, use subversive means to do so, will be caught out in their efforts and exposed. And remember, my beloveds, all those precious souls who look to this place for comfort, for prayers and healing. This is who you are to serve, my beloveds, all these precious and lost souls are God's children and they are waiting, they are waiting for your efforts, your prayers, your touch and God will bring you to that place where this may be so, where you may enact your channel of Love in beautiful, beautiful ways that even now you cannot comprehend in all its fullness and beauty.

There is so much work that awaits, there are so many in need. Think of this when you write, when you elicit the response from the lost soul who believes he is serving God. Think of this my beloveds for God wishes a true and clear channel to do His Work. And those who have gone before him, the beautiful soul who initiated this work years ago, desires this as well for there to be a clear and beautiful channel once again under that roof, who will travel and bring light and speak words of love and healing and comfort to many. This is what is meant to be, my beloveds, this is God's answering the prayers of many. So I urge you to trust in God's Blessings and Will upon your life to bring about what He desires, what is meant in the unfolding of His Plan for the salvation of mankind. You are each gifted with this responsibility, as are many and it is not a burden, my beloveds, it is a gift bringing great blessings to you as well as great blessings to many others. And it is your mind that builds the worry and the stress and the barriers and anxiety, but your soul, my beloveds, your soul knows its great purpose in service, in love and dedication. Be with your souls, be at peace, beloveds and know that all is unfolding as it should and you will play your parts and see the road ahead clearly and joyfully.

I am Andrew. I love you dearly, my beloveds and I will continue to work with this instrument to bring about God's Will in his life and with you, my beloveds, in your life my beloveds, for this is what we, God's Angels, are entrusted to do and we shall be forever by your side helping and assisting the unfolding of your destinies. God bless you, your brother Andrew loves you.
