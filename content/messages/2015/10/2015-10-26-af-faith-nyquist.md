Yes, every soul must take its unique journey, an enfoldment and an unfolding of his path and purpose upon their journey which is guided and touched by God as the soul becomes infused with the Father's Love and your Gifts emerge accordingly. As you begin to release your will to the Father's Will, so your worries and cares are absorbed in your faith and trust in your Heavenly Father. And so you see, my beloveds, how God guides you along your chosen path, and you begin to see what opportunities are placed before you and what precious souls you meet upon your path and what opportunities to be a channel of Love that come with these connections with others.

Do not judge another soul for what they believe, for belief is of the mind and this, in time, you will see is irrelevant. It is that soul connection, your loving soul to their soul that is what is meant by being a channel of Love in the world. You must allow this love to flow uninhibited and remove the restrictions that come with your mental prejudices and fears; to be just love, acceptance, and to be a channel of wisdom and truth.

You shall encounter those upon your path and bring your wisdom and truth to fullness, manifesting in your lives in every aspect. And, there are times when you see error and disharmony in another. It is not for you to correct this, it is for you to show Truth within yourselves and who you are in the world, to be an example. It is for God to lead them into the Light and for their souls to draw them into the Light. So, do not judge, my beloveds, be Love, be at peace and walk in the grace and serenity that comes with this. Do not concern yourselves with that which you see are obstructions upon another's path, but pray for them, my beloveds, love them and guide them through your example.

And, your prayers for your sons, and your prayers for the dear soul who struggles in that small center of Light across your continent are answered and are always being answered as the angels draw close to these dear and precious men who struggle in their lives seeking to find themselves and to find an expression of their true selves in this world. Be at peace, my beloveds. God's Hand firmly rests upon them all and though they suffer and struggle and are at times confused and are in pain, God will lead them through. For all those you pray for, all the souls in your lives benefit from your prayers, and your love and support, and your example and strength. Be true to this, my beloveds, be true to yourselves and true to your Heavenly Father who leads you ever forward in Light and guides you in subtle, yet powerful ways to help you along your path and the work that awaits you. Be at peace and know that you are truly loved, protected and guided. Do not allow any openings for negative forces to influence your thinking and your interactions with the many souls you encounter, but be in God's Grace always. Discipline in your thoughts, discipline in your prayers, it requires strength, it requires trust and faith, it requires that you put your love of God first and foremost in your lives and all else will fall into place, my beloveds, in God's good time and grace all else will fall into place. God bless you, I am Faith and I love you, God bless you.
