Augustine here. You have done good work today, my dear students. You have looked within yourselves and you have assessed your desires, and you have reflected upon who you are in the world and what you are willing to do in the world on behalf of God and His Will, and you have seen the needs that are ever-present in this world and you have thought about where you may meet those needs and you have written your statements and drawn your pictures and this is all important work and I task you with another: I wish for you each one to list five questions you wish to ask of God and to bring them to your circle tomorrow.

Think about these questions, think about what is important to you, what you may ask that will help you in the future that may clear away some doubt, some lingering fears, something that causes you concern or consternation and of course you may necessarily not list five, maybe one. But then it is good to ask questions, it helps you to focus your thinking and we will attempt to answer your questions, my beloveds, and indeed if your question is so large as what is the secret of the universe, this may be difficult to answer although you do know that the Father's Love will bring all knowledge to you.

Try and contain your curiosity, my beloveds, and utilise this opportunity that we may be of help and service to you and we will prepare this instrument to be able to give information, but he is not the only channel in this room. Each of you is able to give with wisdom and knowledge and indeed you may be inspired to answer these questions yourselves, to answer for another and so I would encourage discussion before you come to prayer so that you all may be clear on what it is you wish to ask and those questions that already have been answered, need not to be asked.

Beloved students, indeed you are given the opportunity to go deeper within yourselves, to reflect and to move forward with greater knowledge and with greater inspiration and it is our desire that you will leave these meetings with a clear and focused desire to do one thing for God, and to follow through with that one commitment, to do so in Love, to do so as a dedication to your Heavenly Father, to do so for your brothers and sisters in the world. If each of you did one thing, committed to one thing, you will bring greater Light into this world for you all know that this one thing must be in service to benefit mankind and with the knowledge of your gifts, your capabilities and the Love within your souls you are sure to make a difference in this world, to shift something, to attract souls to Truth, to alleviate a condition of darkness, to teach the Truth, to sacrifice something for the good of others, to walk a Path of Light leading to greater service and greater work.

Yes, God awaits your decision, your actions, your commitment, your tribute of Love. Think of this, my beloved students, think of what you wish to do and if you require others to assist you in this, so much the better for everyone, every soul who assists another soul in Light and Love shall be blessed tenfold, a hundredfold, shall be blessed for all eternity. For as you truly give in Love, as you truly step forward and stretch yourselves beyond that place that is comfortable and known, you grow within your souls, you open a greater channel of Love within you, you are changed and life is about change and growth and progress in Light. Pray for God to inspire you to put that seed of a wonderful service, a wonderful tribute within you and be joyed that you are truly able to give to God in this way and to give to your brothers and sisters. For all gifts of Love to God, are gifts of Love to one another, to mankind.

God bless you, my beloved souls, carry on upon your journey of discovery, your sojourn that leads to opening your eyes further to discovering more fully what it is that you truly desire within your souls and to come to know God and His Will in greater measure. Always step forward, my beloveds, always desire to step forward in the flow of God's Love. So I bid you good night, until we meet in the morning and you have contemplated this day and contemplated what is meant for the morrow, and said your prayers, and had your thoughts, and conversed with your loved ones who are here with you. And we will be with you through these hours and days. God bless you, beloved souls. God bless you.
