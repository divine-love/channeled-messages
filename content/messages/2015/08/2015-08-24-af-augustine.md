It is your teacher Augustine here. I have heard this instrument say that the special dispensation given has now been removed. This is not correct. He is feeling the effects of his rather undisciplined diet and this is a lesson of discernment, my beloveds. For each of you needs to be more sensitive and wiser in your perceptions as to how God influences you and brings blessings to your lives. You will be challenged, my beloveds, as you go back to your lives in your respective cities and places of residence. The human condition is a powerful force in this world. It draws upon you, it influences you, it takes from you and rarely gives something valuable back. And, of course, you must all live in this world and cope with this condition that is not in harmony with God's Love and Laws. And you struggle with this, and you have been given an opportunity to be separated from these conditions, and you have been nourished and fed physically and spiritually, and you have known a fellowship that is beautiful and bright. And some of you feel a tinge of fear upon your return home, and I say to you that God will insulate you from the human condition if you are willing to be attuned to Him in ways that you have been attuned to God in these past days.

It requires your efforts, my beloveds, your prayers, the focusing of your thoughts, and indeed at times the disciplining of your diets. It requires effort, for the laws of cause and effect are real, the laws of God's Love are real and they need to be obeyed, and you need to be wise, my beloveds, in your actions, your thoughts and earnest in your prayers. For if you are not, these feelings of joy and relief and Love and Peace will dissipate, will dissipate until you feel like you are back within the struggles that you so wish to be away from and not a part of. This does not mean that your challenges will disappear in the world, in your world, but it will mean that the way you approach the emotional reactions to them, the anger, and the fear, and the frustration will all but disappear and you will feel a sense of empowerment to be able to cope and find ways and solutions to these dilemmas.

God offers all of these blessings to you, my beloveds, but of course it is your choice. It is your choice what next step you make in your lives, in your thoughts, in your actions. Will they be informed by Love or will they be informed by the human condition which resides in all the souls in this world? For this condition has been built for many, many thousands of years of choices made by humanity, of thoughts nurtured by those who are not informed by their souls, but their minds. My beloveds, it is important for you to recognize the difference and we have guided you through in your discussions to help you to understand the difference, the importance of having the soul discernment, the soul perceptions, the ability to see clearly the difference. This is your next step, my beloveds, to recognize and empower the soul consciousness within your being, to allow this to be that part of you which informs your choices and your actions, for in this is God guiding you and filling your souls with His Love. You will know His Will. You will understand what you must do. You will be at ease in any situation no matter how challenging it may be. You will know peace and a centeredness in God – peace and a centeredness in God, do you not all seek this, my beloveds? It is available to you, it is promised if you accept this Gift that comes from God's Love, if you trust and adopt the wisdom that flows into your mind from your soul, if you walk in great faith and be strong in your faith and your faith be strong within you, then God can use you as His bright and beautiful channels of Love and change in this world. For the wisdom and the manifestations of Love which will come through you and into your being and all about you will draw many to you, will draw the attention, the openness that you speak of that you wish for your brothers and sisters in this world to have, that their eyes might be opened to another way of being and living in this world that will be in harmony with creation, in harmony with all the creatures of this world, in harmony with the beautiful Earth itself.

And yes, when you say you must do this one step at a time, this is correct. But first and foremost you must have your souls and your minds aligned with God's Will and we have spent these days together to teach you, to inspire you, to show you how this is done. Please listen to our words. Please adopt these ways. And I know that the human condition is very compelling. It is difficult. It is difficult to rise above it and not be drawn into it, but you must do this my children, you must in order for you to be a part of God's Great Plan for you and for all those who are willing to bring about the salvation of mankind. For mankind is in desperate need of change and greater harmony and peace. Desperate. The negative forces in this world, the great power to destroy, the hatred, the strife, the poverty, the ignorance, the deprivation that is on all levels in this world that humanity lives within is burdened by and distorted in their thinking, their doing. This must change, it must change and we rely upon willing souls such as yourselves to enact this change, to make these efforts which will be small to begin with, but each step informs the next step, each effort informs the next effort.

You must do something. You must feel within your souls this need, this desire, this sense of purpose that comes with a soul imbued with God's Love. Love must prevail, my beloveds, it must prevail within you. It does live within your souls. It must prevail within your minds and your actions and all about your life. And as Love takes a permanent place within all of these parts of you and your life it will spread, it will expand, it will grow, and it will create great Circles of Light in the world. For God will feed you, give you what you require, fill your souls with His Love. The more you allow that Love to spread and grow within the world, in your world, God will provide what is necessary to feed this Light, to inform this Light. Beloveds, seek your guidance, be firm upon this Path, allow your doubts and questions and fears to fade away. These cloaks that you wear, allow them to fall upon the Path and continue to walk forward, free of this condition which inhibits your growth, inhibits your Love, inhibits your thoughts.

God bless you, my beloveds, you are loved, and you will move forward supporting one another, and each of you will be inspired, deeply inspired, and have been deeply touched by the Father's Love. Continue in your efforts. Continue in your fellowship. Continue to Love one another and we will work with you to create these Circles of Light in the world. God bless you, beloveds.
