I am Mary, God bless you.

Love the children, my beloveds, love all the children. If you feel within your heart this deep compassion and you are drawn to the children, go where your heart leads you, touch the children. Teach them the Truth of God's Love, show them your love. Be a channel of Love for these beautiful and precious souls that will inherit the Earth, inherit the Earth. That they may make when they are grown a better world. A world that is based on love and respect and the strength of the soul perception of God's creation and the deep peace within that they may not feel fear and anxiety, nor be troubled. The precious children, nurture them, bring your Light and bring the Light of God's Truth to them, for they understand, they know that this is the Truth, the simple Truth that even a child may know.

I am with you all in your efforts, in your struggles. I am with you all and I shall continue to be with you as you walk through your life and be in the world as a Light. I am with you. God bless you.
