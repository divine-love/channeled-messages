It is Seretta Kem. You have each been given a blessing of healing and have been touched by God in a way which will remove certain barriers to your soul's progression. This blessing and healing will have a permanent effect upon your being. It is a gift given for your efforts to come together. A special gift. And for each it is unique, and suited for your particular progression upon the Divine Path and will have its unique effects upon your being.

Bless you, beloved souls. Bless you for your efforts, for your love, for the beauty that is within your souls. God bless you. You are loved by many, many angels, who seek to help to secure you within the flow of the Father's Love and to make you that pure and beautiful channel that God wishes for you to be in this world. Yes, indeed, you can only move forward, you can only move forward in greater Light. And you will release all that which holds you in the human condition. This shall be released as you garner more of the Father's Love within you. We will help you in your birthing into a place of purity and Light. We surround you with our love, and we walk with you every moment, every moment we are with you and it is our blessing to do so. God bless you. I am your helper; I am your friend, and I am with you to support you in many ways. I am Seretta Kem, and I love you. God bless you.
