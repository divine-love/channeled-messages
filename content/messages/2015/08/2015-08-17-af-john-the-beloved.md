God bless you, gracious and beloved souls, I am John the Beloved.

A great Light builds within your midst and the angels crowd around you, my beloveds. Helping you to build this Light that is a gift from God, helping you to reach that place of stillness that you may receive a great inflowing of the Father's Love. Beloved souls, you are being blessed at this moment, your souls receiving the great benediction of Love that is promised you by your Heavenly Father. Drink deep these living waters, beloved souls, drink deep and long. Put your longings to your Heavenly Father. Raise yourselves up from the human condition to the Divine Presence of God.
