I wish to tell you and your inner circle you have been given a special dispensation, and to those within your inner circle. For you to do this work in this wider circle you've been given a cloak of protection, you have been given a special angel who guards and sustains you, and a special Light has been given to you to help you to not engage so readily in the material and human conditions. Sustain this, my beloveds. Sustain this so that you may do the work that is planned for you to do. For many things will come of these meetings, changes and shifts within many people and a coming together in a more powerful and effective way of all of the earnest souls who wish to do God's work. Do not underestimate the power of this or the importance of this? The world comes to a very crucial crossroads and so do you, my beloveds, so do you. Choose wisely, listen carefully and know that God wants the highest, the greatest Good for each of you and for every soul in this world. Allow His Touch to affect every part of you and all around you. Be at peace, beloved souls. Be at peace. I am James and I love you. God bless you, beloved souls. God bless you.
