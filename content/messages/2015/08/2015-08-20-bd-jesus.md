Beloveds, you have each received the Love Divine. You must know this, my precious ones, for you are deeply needed in your world to bring the peace and the Love that will bring the power of Love Divine to all life, all the lives. Yes, my beloveds, I am Jesus. And you must carry the power of the Love. Let it flow, for this will bring the change that is needed in your world. So many souls are lost and you have the power to let the Divine Love flow into your world. You will bring the peace and Love Divine that is deeply longed for and needed. There are many lost souls that suffer in your world and you must let the power of the Love Divine flow from your souls, my beloveds, and you will deepen the power and healing and comfort and Love Divine.

Your world is ready for this to come, beloveds. Know this and let it flow from your hearts, the Love you carry. It will grow and deepen and there will be peace able to come, beloveds. You are deeply needed and deeply loved. Go forth and let it flow from your souls, precious beloveds, you have been touched and blessed.
