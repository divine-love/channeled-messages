May God's Love find a deep and abiding lodgement within you, my beloveds. May this Love flood every chamber, every nook and cranny of your souls washing away all that is not in harmony with its presence and its Gift within you, lighting your way in this world, bringing its wisdom, its Truth, its power, within you, my beloveds. Let this rise up into your consciousness, this wisdom, this Truth that the veils of error and deceit and delusion which clog your minds may be cast aside, that you may see clearly with the eyes of your souls, helping you, guiding you and bringing you to your Heavenly Father in deep, deep communion with Him, that source of all Love and Truth and life.

Yes, the bonds that you have forged in these days have brought into your hearts a true and loving connection and awareness of one another, and this, my beloveds, will help you along your Path. For now you know in this world you have brothers and sisters who love you and support you, and you may all share your wisdom, your love for God together and carry one another ever higher upon your road to God, sharing the perspectives, the view from where you stand with one another as a loving gift, as an act of Love and commitment towards one another, my beloveds. Walk together, continue your relationship, revealing yourselves to one another.

I am Mark and I am with you, my son. I help you upon your journey in this world. And through your many trials and tribulations. My son, you have grown within your soul. That Love has shone through all these experiences. You have chosen Light, you have been strong. Do not underestimate the strength that you carry within you and the compassion that you have for others and yourself is a true demonstration of the Love that you carry within your soul and this deep compassion will help many souls who struggle in this world. Touch the lost souls, my son, pray for those whom you know require the hand of God upon them. Be humble and kind and your gifts shall flourish. When you play your music you bring Light into the room and it touches all who are there to listen. It is a gift, one of many gifts, my son.

And, my daughter, you too have many gifts of healing and a great channel of Love within you, beloved daughter, and together you carry a great Light, you walk a beautiful Path and many are drawn to you, my beloveds, they are drawn to your Light, they are drawn because they feel Love, they feel your acceptance and they are comforted by your presence.

Continue to walk your Paths together, to be together in Love, to allow God to work through you to embrace all those whom you encounter. You will feel Love flowing through you out to all the many souls. This world does indeed need your presence and Light and Love and soon you will teach many of God's Great Love. And as you walk in this world you are an example of God's Great Love living within two beautiful souls. And you will carry this Light that you have experienced here in this far-off land to your home and it will grow, my beloveds, it will grow and you will step ever higher upon the Path, closer to your Heavenly Father in deep communion with God. You will know His guidance. You will know as you step along the Path where you must go.

Beloved, beautiful souls, all of you, my beloveds, are embraced by the Heavenly Father and your lives will reflect this in every way. And though you may struggle within your minds and your emotions to come to a place of clarity and surety within you, this already exists within your souls. You must set aside any feelings of doubt and anxiety and accept that God has touched you deeply and in this deep Touch, it has changed you irrevocably. Accept the transformation of your souls, my beloveds. Allow this beautiful Gift to be truly a part of you. Do not separate yourselves from your souls but go deeper within to that part of you and remain within this consciousness and you will find that your lives will reflect this great Light within you, and this great Light within you will reflect into your lives. Beloveds, beautiful souls, continue to be in God's presence always. Keep your connection with your Creator alive and burning within your conscious selves. Allow your souls to be in their rightful place within you and all about you, for God then will work His beautiful miracles of Love in your lives for others in service bringing you to pinnacles of joy and peace and fulfillment. God bless you, my beloveds. I am Mark and I love you. I love you dearly.
