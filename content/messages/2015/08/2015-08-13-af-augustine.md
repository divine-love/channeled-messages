It is Augustine here.

God bless you my beloved students. You must continue to pray for this instrument (Al) in these coming days and support him for we will be using him in many ways and we have much to convey through him. And this will be taxing. You cannot allow him to be distracted by the material and he is tempted to put his fingers in many pies and you must remind him of his one and only duty in this time.

Yes indeed you all will receive your guidance but there are some who are coming who will not be so attuned to that and so we will guide through this instrument in many ways to facilitate and trigger their own understanding.

Yes there is much on the agenda we have to bring forth. Be aware of this and allow this to happen. God is orchestrating these events and I know you are all willing to do God's work and to allow His Will to manifest in this way. God bless you, my beloved students, beautiful souls. We will continue to communicate and you will be surprised in what will be given. This instrument has reached a new level in his gifts and much preparation has been made even down to the cellular structure within him and within his brain. We have done much to change him and prepare him and of course none is possible without receiving the great Love of God in him. It is truly a transformative force. How wonderful it is that we can use this Love in so many intricate ways and wondrous ways for each of you. For each is cared for and prepared in ways that are very much beyond your present capability of understanding. And this not necessary, my children. But I tell you, the great work has been made upon each one of you in preparation for the work to come. And within you, you are all very much aware of this.

Continue to pray together, to love together, to converse in loving ways, to share yourselves, to share your hearts, to share your aspirations of what you truly desire. It is important to be open with one another. To bring this understanding, for these times are precious, these times are very precious and will lay further foundations for the work ahead. Utilize your time, utilize it well.

God bless you. God bless you, my precious students. You are truly loved and beautifully blessed, God bless you.
