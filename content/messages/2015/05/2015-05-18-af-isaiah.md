… new realms of understanding and thinking, of soul awareness, of knowing your Heavenly Father. It sweeps you to places unfamiliar, and lands unknown to you. It sweeps the Earth changing all within its path. This is the breath of God, the Will of God, and all is done with Love. Do not resist these changes but embrace them, and acknowledge the gift that is the breath of God, and you will come to know what it is to walk in this grace and Love. God bless you, my beloveds. I am Isaiah. Bless you and may God continue to embrace you and breathe this new Light within. God bless you.
