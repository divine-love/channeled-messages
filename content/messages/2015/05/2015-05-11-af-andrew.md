At this very moment the Father's Love flows into your souls quenching that thirst from deep within you to receive the Divine Essence, to be close to your Heavenly Father. Seek this Love, this gift with all your hearts and all your minds and all your desires within your souls; it will bring you to that place of true knowing, of true understanding of God's creation, of the aspects of your beings, of the aspects of God's great being, and all the creation about you. Great insight is given through the soul perceptions which are ignited by the Love within your souls.

So many in your world, my beloveds, think that they know the truth, that they understand the reality in which they exist, but I tell you the vast majority of humanity does not see clearly or understand with any great depth the true existence that is God's creation. Indeed, the mind is so limited in its ability to understand truth. It is like looking at something with only two dimensions where there are many dimensions, many aspects of creation which you do not see or perceive or understand but nonetheless they exist. There is so much to discover, my beloveds, so much to know and your mind hungers for knowledge, but truly it is your souls that can impart knowledge and truth to your mind and this is accomplished through your relationship with your Heavenly Father in the growing and expanding of your souls in love.

So I urge you to earnestly seek in this way and the whole universe of God's creation will unfold before you as you grow in Love, as you become in alignment with your Heavenly Father so much awaits, an eternity of discovery, an unfolding of truth, a truly magnificent tapestry of beauty and Love and reality that is all flowing from your Heavenly Father. Seek for the highest, my beloveds, seek for truth and when you find it it will be confirmed over and over again within you that it is truly the reality of God's creation. His Love will lead you there and light the way and His angels will help you to find it and all will be in harmony as you grasp these aspects of truth, this knowledge that can only come from the soul and perceived by the soul's perceptions.

Grasp each day, my beloveds, as an opportunity to uncover the beauty of God's creation, grasp each day as an invitation to be with God, to walk in the Light of God's Love. Each footfall brings you closer, each prayer and aspiration from your soul will bring you higher into this great understanding. Yes, there is much to learn and to experience and as you accumulate within you the truth, the wonderment, the Love, you will then be a beacon of Light and you will in your way impart your knowledge, the strength of this, the beauty of this, the surety of this truth. It is the universe of God's Love and creation. Walk with God, my beloveds. Walk with us, the angels, and the discoveries will be breathtaking, wondrous, magnificent, and beautiful. God bless you, my beloveds, I am Andrew and I love you. God bless you.
