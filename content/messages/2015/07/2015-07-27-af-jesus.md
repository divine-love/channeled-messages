You cannot walk until you crawl, my beloveds, and you cannot run until you can walk. And as you raise yourself up from the floor and as you learn to walk at this time, soon you will run. Be steady on your feet, my beloveds, walk the road and when you are about to fall we will catch you. And as you continue to walk you will gain the confidence, the balance which you require to truly move in the flow of God's Love. And when you are that strong and beloved child walking with joy, feeling the Lightness upon your feet, how the Love will flow and the joy will well up within you, the joy of walking with God, running with God, singing and loving with God. It will be, it is your destiny to be in this Love with all the fullness and the joy and the Lightness that is meant for you and each soul upon this world.

Be free, my beloveds, of your worries and your burdens and know how truly loved you are. You are beloved and beautiful souls, children of our Heavenly Father who wish to stand on their own two feet with their eyes raised to their Heavenly Father and their hearts full of Love. Walk, my beloveds, walk and walk with me. I am Jesus and I shall walk with you, my beloved brothers and sisters, all children upon the Path of Love walk with me. God bless you. God bless you.
