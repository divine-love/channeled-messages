He is so serious. He is so earnest that he wants you to grow in light and awaken in love. But remember, my friends, to bring joy in every thing you do, to think of this journey not as a test but as a gift, that everything that God brings to you is a gift and that in it there is an element of joy. The joy in your souls continues to grow as you grow. The love in your souls continues to be purified and awakened as you continue to receive this gift of all the Love of God. We are with you. Be joyful. Allow God to uplift you always and I will do my part to bring to you joy because I love you. I love you dearly. God bless you. Goldie loves you.
