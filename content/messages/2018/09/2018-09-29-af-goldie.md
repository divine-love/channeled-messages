Your dear friend wants me to come to your service to lighten the crowd. I will, for this is a joyous occasion, celebrating the birth of Vicky into the world of spirit. What a beautiful occasion, to begin her life anew in this beautiful world that God created. If only more knew how beautiful and wonderful is God's creation, all that worry and concern would melt into joy and laughter. So much the world has to learn. So much the world thinks it knows, and knows nothing at all of God's creation and God's universe.

My friends, bring joy into your hearts, a spring into your step, celebrating life in all its forms and ways and manifestations of God's wonderful creation. We are all so blessed, are we not, in all the wonderment and goodness of our lives. The Touch of God in our souls, the understanding of true life in our mind. So blessed we are, so very blessed.

I love you. God bless you. I am Goldie and I love you.
