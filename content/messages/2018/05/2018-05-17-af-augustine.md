God bless you, it is your teacher Augustine. Yes I have come to clarify your questions regarding the guidance that I gave through this instrument a few days ago. For the most part, I wish to say that I'm not indicating that 60% of your day must be within your soul and 40% within your mind. This is not the goal. The goal is to have the mind dominated by the soul at all times. That when you are perceiving the world, interacting with the world, that the root expression of your being may come from the soul. And if you think of the soul as a light, then that light must shine bright and influence the mind. So that this combination; this marriage of soul and mind may involve the dominance of the soul guiding the mind, guiding your consciousness. That when you are in this circumstance of the dominance of the soul, you become aware of its influence, the mind of the soul, the light of the soul, the love of the soul, the faculties of the soul and that you carry this consciousness with you wherever you go and whatever you do. To put forth an expectation that you would express the consciousness of the soul in its completeness and all its power and beauty to such a degree that the mind is completely subjugated by the soul is an unrealistic expectation at this time of any of you who are progressing with the Divine Love. I was merely attempting to encourage you to bring the soul consciousness to a more acute realization, that it may dominate the consciousness of your being, yet still operating in conjunction with the consciousness of your mind. The soul consciousness would be the dominant factor as the mind contributes and adds to this level of awareness, this shift of thinking, being and perceiving. This is possible my beloveds. This is possible.

Think of this as if you were mixing colors. On one hand you have yellow and on the other hand you have red. Now if the mind were considered the red color and the soul the yellow and you desire to make a beautiful orange, a sunset orange, what percentage would you mix together? Predominately yellow mixed with red. And so we wish for the predominance of your consciousness to be of the soul, but we understand that you must also include the infusion of the mind with the soul in order to create the harmonious intermingling of thoughts and consciousness. It is not one or the other my beloveds. Your experience in life is not one or the other, it is not soul or mind. It is a combination of the mixing of these two aspects of yourselves once you have discovered and come to the realization of your own soul and this awareness comes into your conscious self, influencing your thinking and your perceptions.

And so we encourage you to heighten your awareness, your conscious acknowledgement of your soul, bringing the predominant factors of the soul forward so that your thinking, who you are, how you respond and experience life is colored by the soul and the soul faculties. This in turn is colored by God and His Love for you. Thus the chain becomes more palpable in your experience of life; this connection with God, this connection with your own soul. Allowing the mind to make room for the other consciousness, this other way of being in the world. Though this cannot happen by a mere desire and whim to be in this place, it takes practice and focus, prayer and patience to come to that place of harmony between the mind and the soul. I encourage you to pray for this gift and when this gift is upon you and at times it is a conscious factor in your awareness, recognize it, recognize that place. Understand and revere that place of soul consciousness. And where does this recognition come, this awareness come, it comes from your material mind. For as you are in this material world, these factors and aspects of your being must come into play.

And so you must adjust and work towards an alignment between mind and soul creating a new consciousness, a new way of being in the world. A way that is predominately influenced by Love, the Divine Love, the Light within your soul and this touch of God within your life. When you come to rely upon these aspects of your life experience, this commitment to God, the awakening of your soul, greater harmony will ensue within your life. You will be able to walk the world with wisdom, with perception, with knowing. Knowing beyond those things that your material mind may recognize and acknowledge with the soul perceptions, your understanding is deepened. You see beyond the veil of the mind to the condition of the soul, the condition of the true self-revealing itself in the world expressed by all those around you. And thus your perception of all your brothers and sisters, of the energies and conditions of this world, the many layers and aspects of this life are revealed and your perceptions of reality are changed by the awakening of your soul.

You will readily acknowledge these awakenings, these differences of perception. They will become more real to you and it becomes the reality that most of you are experiencing at this time as you pray to be a channel of Love in the world for God to use you to bring change, to teach the truth, to bring Love. These faculties within you, faculties of your soul must come into play. For these are gifts, these are abilities that God will use not only to inform you upon your daily path, but to guide you and show you the Truth. They will equip you to be in the world as an example of Love and to have perceptions that are true but often unseen by many. These are gifts that are coming, but for them to come you must allow the soul its dominance in your consciousness and this takes, as I've said, practice and application, prayer and patience, it comes with the power of God's Love within your souls. It comes with the transformation of your souls, the awakening of your souls so that you will see differently, perceive differently, think differently. Come to know God's reality. In the rudimentary form, it is the feelings, the feelings that come up from the soul. And as you progress in the soul's awakening and attuning yourself to the faculties of the soul, other things come. You will be able to see others for who they are. You will see life from different perspectives, dimensions if you will. You will see yourself in your relationship with God, with your relationship within your own life. You will be able to share your perceptions, your perceptions of Truth, your perceptions of the reality of this world that are not just that which is seen by the five senses, known by the five senses, but from other sensibilities that are inherent within the soul.

The soul has many abilities and capacities to understand the world, the world of God's Creation. The inner world and the outer world. The physical world and the spiritual world. These things may be readily perceived by the soul, but first you must attune yourself to these things, these perceptions and abilities that are awakening within you. Continue to pray my beloveds, continue to walk in God's world, to pray to God, to reach up and up to God, to seek His Blessings, to seek the inflow of His Love that continues to awaken your soul so that you will have the capacity and ability, the strength, the desire, the awareness that will open these doors of perception and understanding. It will open doors so that you may act as God's instruments in the world and channels of His Love. That God may guide you to serve others in Love so that you may see more clearly your own purpose and abilities in this regard. As you mature in this awakening of the soul, it is time to acknowledge this, to seek to live in this reality, the reality dominated by the soul, yet is inclusive of the mind, the material mind.

And we will assist you in this journey of discovery. It is like the birthing of a child seeing it grow and expand in its awareness and abilities, so you come upon a new birth within yourself, a new being whose abilities continue to grow, who is emerging from the slumber that all mankind seems to be within to a place of awakening and knowing. This is your destiny my beloved souls, this is where you long to go within your true self, to be fully alive and awake and in harmony with God. May you do so my beloved friends with each breath you take within each day in your life may you come closer to this place and we will do so together. You are not alone in this. You are with God and God sends His angels to accompany you, to assure you, to guide you, to help you upon this journey. God's beautiful plan, this gift of life, this gift of the emerging of the soul in His Love is such a wondrous journey. And we in spirit are privileged to witness this with each soul that comes to the awareness of the Love of God and the power of His Love to transform each and every soul who so desires this gift.
