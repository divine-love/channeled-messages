# Divine Love Archive - Series

This file defines the thematic series used to group messages into curated collections for the website. Series are assigned to messages via the `series` field in the message front matter.

---

### "Mind & Soul"

Messages that explore the tension between the material mind and the soul, the awakening of the soul mind, and the transition from mind-dominated to soul-centred living.

**Anchor message:** [Nurturing the Mind or the Soul: It's a Choice](messages/2016/07/2016-07-10-af-augustine.md)
*The yearning itself is the prayer, and the crack in the armor of the mind is where God enters*

He calls the circle to release mind-driven willfulness, seek the deeper consciousness of the soul, and become beacons of God's reality as the world stands on the verge of great change. |
