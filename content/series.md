# Divine Love Archive — Series

This file defines the thematic series used to group messages into curated collections for the website. Series are assigned to messages via the `series` field in the message front matter.

---

### "Mind & Soul"

Messages that explore the tension between the material mind and the soul, the awakening of the soul mind, and the transition from mind-dominated to soul-centred living.

**Anchor message:** [Nurturing the Mind or the Soul: It's a Choice](messages/2016/07/2016-07-10-af-augustine.md)
*The yearning itself is the prayer, and the crack in the armor of the mind is where God enters*

**Messages:**
| Message | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| [Free Will versus God's Will](messages/2015/12/2015-12-14-af-augustine.md) | Augustine teaches that the human mind alone cannot know God's Will, and that only a soul enlivened by Divine Love can perceive and align with it. He calls the circle to release mind-driven willfulness, seek the deeper consciousness of the soul, and become beacons of God's reality as the world stands on the verge of great change. |
| [Awakening of the Soul Mind](messages/2015/12/2015-12-27-af-augustine.md) | Augustine describes a gift given to the circle to strengthen their ability to distinguish between soul perception and mind perception, teaching that as Divine Love deepens, the soul mind comes into focus like clearing vision, bringing a truer and more powerful awareness of God and reality. |
| [Accepting the New Awarenesses of an Opening Soul](messages/2016/01/2016-01-04-af-confucius.md) | Confucius teaches that the great challenge of the spiritual life is allowing the soul to come into direct communication with the mind, dissolving the filters and prejudices that obstruct true perception. He assures the circle that as this merging of awarenesses deepens through God's Love, they will see both the beauty of God's Creation and the darkness of man's, yet remain anchored and unswayed by the power of Divine Love within them. |
| [Expectations of the Mind: On Releasing Control](messages/2016/04/2016-04-12-af-augustine.md) | Augustine calls his students to release the mind's need to control outcomes and impose its will upon the world and those around them, teaching that true humility is this act of letting go, and that in doing so God's Will can infuse every part of their lives, marking the beginning of their ministry in the world. |
| [Breaking Free of the Shell Around Your Soul](messages/2016/06/2016-06-13-af-james.md) | James reassures those anxious about their spiritual progress that despite their mental struggles and self-judgment, they are already channels of Love in the world — and that the restlessness and fragility they feel is the shell of encrustation cracking as their true souls emerge, like a chick breaking free from the egg, with God and His angels as midwives. |
| [The Awakened Soul](messages/2016/07/2016-07-09-af-confucius.md) | Confucius welcomes a circle of souls seeking Truth and gently directs them inward — to plumb the depths of their own souls — teaching that it is God's Love that ignites a soul to awaken, and that the awakened soul has a capacity to understand Truth that the mind alone simply cannot access. |
| [Nurturing the Mind or the Soul: It's a Choice](messages/2016/07/2016-07-10-af-augustine.md) *(anchor)* | Augustine delivers a comprehensive teaching on the soul's yearning as the true key to God, contrasting the mind's compulsion to rationalize and control with the soul's deeper capacity to receive Divine Truth. He traces the roots of human error to the mind's domination over the soul, charts both the natural love and Divine Love paths to their ultimate destinations, and urges each soul to plumb its own depths and follow the yearning that leads to at-onement with God. |
| [Express the Potentials of Your Awakening Souls](messages/2016/08/2016-08-16-af-james.md) | James acknowledges the noise and distraction of the material world and encourages the circle to remain in the flow of God's Love throughout each day, assuring them that as they do, the great treasure of gifts within their awakening souls will rise into expression — and they will come to know God's Will, walk in Light, and touch many souls as testimony to His Love. |
| [Time to Accept a New Way of Being](messages/2016/12/2016-12-29-af-matthew.md) | Matthew speaks prophetically of changes coming in the new year for the circle and the world, urging them to release the illusions and mental constructs built up over a lifetime and accept the new reality of soul-centred truth — for they are being prepared to lead others through the same transformative process in the times ahead. |
| [Changes Within Your Soul Will Bring Many Rewards](messages/2017/01/2017-01-23-af-alec-gaunt.md) | Alec Gaunt addresses souls in the midst of deep inner transformation, teaching that the discomfort, confusion, and self-criticism they feel is the natural result of old patterns and conditions being burned away by God's Love. He urges them to recognize that the outward conscious self they cling to is not their true self, and that as the fire of Divine Love burns brighter within, the treasures of their true souls will emerge in beauty and power. |
| [Release Self-Will and Embrace the Perfect Will of God](messages/2017/03/2017-03-27-af-james.md) | James speaks with compassion to souls caught between the world of man and the world of God, teaching that the struggle to release self-will and surrender to God's Will is the central challenge of the Divine Path - and that while the mind clings stubbornly to the old ways, the soul has already chosen, and progress is inevitable for those who continue to pray and turn their face to God. |
| [Faculties of the Soul Compared to a Camera Lens](messages/2017/04/2017-04-10-af-james.md) | James clarifies how God's Will flows through a soul by using the metaphor of a camera: the image of Truth is placed directly within the soul by God's Love, the iris represents the soul's will and desire which regulates how much flows through, and the lens is the mind which either focuses or distorts what comes forth. When all three are in alignment, the result is a clear and undoubted expression of God's Will in the world. |
| [Lesson on Judgement](messages/2017/06/2017-06-15-af-augustine.md) | Augustine delivers a comprehensive lesson on the urge to judge, teaching that our reactions to others' conditions reveal our own soul state, that judgment is incompatible with Love, and that the antidote is not suppression but conscious choice - catching yourself in the act of judgment and choosing Love instead. He includes an extended first-person inner monologue as a template for how to make that choice in the moment. |
| [Only the Soul Can Know God's Love](messages/2017/08/2017-08-21-af-matthew.md) | Matthew teaches that the soul's experience of God's Love cannot be described intellectually, only felt - and that each soul's experience of this Touch is unique. He warns against turning personal spiritual experience into a dogmatic formula, urging all to validate and accept the wide range of ways that God's Love manifests within different souls. |
| [Release of Limitations](messages/2017/09/2017-09-12-af-augustine.md) | Augustine acknowledges the real limitations of living in a material body and mind shaped by early conditioning, and teaches that God's Love, incrementally building within the soul, has the power to purify these conditions from the inside outward - bringing not a loss of self but a simplification and clarification of consciousness, where soul wisdom replaces mental complexity and self-judgment gives way to deep self-love. |
| [Understanding the Laws of Love and the Regeneration of Our Souls](messages/2017/09/2017-09-12-af-martin-luther.md) | Martin Luther responds to the group's conversation about limitations, teaching that God has created an ordered universe governed by laws, that the Law of Divine Love supersedes all others and can bypass the long process of natural love purification, and that each soul bears personal responsibility for their own regeneration - for God cannot spoon-feed a soul into harmony without that soul's own desire, effort, and prayer. |
| [The Responsibility of Acting in Love Lies with You](messages/2017/12/2017-12-18-af-martin-luther.md) |  |
