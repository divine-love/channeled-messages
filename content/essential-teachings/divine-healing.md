# Divine Healing

Messages that describe, demonstrate, or teach the mechanics of divine healing. Includes both accounts of healing events and instruction on how divine healing works — through prayer, soul purity, and the inflowing of God's Love.

---

| Message | Date | Description |
|---|---|---|
| [Healing Merkaba](../messages/2015/11/2015-11-28-af-seretta-kem.md) | 2015‑11‑28 | Seretta Kem congratulates the group for creating a powerful healing merkaba together and speaks of the great exponential flowering of interconnected Circles of Light in the world. He calls them to embrace all souls with tolerance and acceptance regardless of their beliefs or philosophies, assuring them that God will open door after door as they travel the world connecting with other circles of Light in God's great plan to bring healing and harmony to the Earth. |
| [Remember to Nourish Your Soul](../messages/2016/02/2016-02-10-af-confucius.md) | 2016‑02‑10 | Confucius counsels those doing healing work to pray for God to touch each soul through them, so that they act in resonance and harmony with the flow of Divine Love. He teaches that while many energies and emanations of body and spirit exist, the emanation of love from the soul is the most powerful, and that the healer must nourish their own soul foremost, keeping prayer and longing for God at the forefront so that all gifts, learnings, and healing fall into harmony. |
| [Questions Answered about Divine Love Healing](../messages/2019/01/2019-01-26-af-sri-yarisupta.md) | 2019‑01‑26 | Sri Yarisupta answers a question about healing energies, teaching that the universe contains a vast rainbow of healing gifts and that attempting to define, narrow, or ritualise them limits what God can bring through an open channel. He affirms that every soul has some degree of healing gift, and that receiving the Divine Love widens and deepens all healing capacities beyond what the mind can currently imagine. |
