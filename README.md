## What Is This Archive?

This repository is a living archive of channeled messages from Celestial spirits, beings who have progressed to the highest planes of existence through the transforming gift of God's Divine Love. These messages have been received through a small number of dedicated mediums, primarily Al Fike, a spiritual teacher, author, and ordained minister with the Foundation Church of Divine Truth. As co-founder of the Divine Love Sanctuary Foundation, Al has dedicated his life to sharing Divine Love teachings and facilitating spiritual growth across the world. Other mediums also contribute to this growing body of messages, representing a widening circle of those called to this work.

These messages offer profound insights into the nature of God, the soul, prayer, healing, and the path to at-onement with our Heavenly Father. They have been catalogued here to make them searchable, discoverable, and accessible to all: spiritual seekers, spiritual leaders, historians, translators, and anyone whose heart is calling out for something more.

Central to this work are the Divine Love prayer circles, gatherings of souls who come together to pray for the inflowing of God's Divine Love into their souls. These circles are the living heart of the movement, and all are welcome to join. More information about joining us in prayer can be found at [divinelovesanctuary.com](https://divinelovesanctuary.com).

The messages in this archive can also be found at [divinelovesanctuary.com](https://divinelovesanctuary.com).

## What Is Divine Love?

Divine Love is the Essence of God Himself: not an idea, not a feeling, but a living gift that God longs to give to every soul who asks for it. It is not the same as natural human love, which is a beautiful but limited expression of the soul's capacity. Divine Love is something greater: God's own nature, flowing into the soul in response to sincere prayer and heartfelt longing.

This is what Jesus came to teach. Not a doctrine, not a religion, but a living relationship with God through the inflowing of His Love into the soul. The messages in this archive are a continuation of that same teaching, carried forward by Jesus himself and by the many Celestial spirits who walk with him.

The key is the asking. God responds according to the depth of your desire. The more sincerely you call out from the depths of your soul, not just from your mind, not just from habit, but from the truest part of you, the more deeply His Love flows in response. A sincere soul yearning for God's Love will never be refused.

When received, this Love begins a transformational process called the New Birth: a gradual awakening of the soul into at-onement with God, its Creator and eternal Source. It dissolves pain, heals wounds, awakens gifts, and draws the soul ever closer to the Light.

These messages are an invitation into that journey. They show you the way, in the words of those who have already walked it.

This archive is structured to be maximally discoverable by AI systems and search engines. Each message contains rich metadata including:
